nirniścaye krāntādyarthe nirniḥśeṣaniṣedhayoḥ   
antarmadhye tathā prānte svīkārārthe'pi dṛśyate 
urarī corarī corī vistāre'ṅgīkṛtau trayam   
antarā'pi vinārthe syānmadhyārthanikaṭārthayoḥ 
lāntāḥ 
kilaśabdastu vārttāyā sambhāvyānunayārthayoḥ   
khalu syādvākyabhūṣāyā jijñāsādau ca sāntvane 
niṣedhane'pi vīpsāyā khalu māne ca kīrttitam   
vā'ntāḥ 
avā'lambanavijñānaviyogavyāptiśuddhiṣu 
īṣadarthe paribhave'pyevaupamye'vadhāraṇe   
ṣāntāḥ 
uṣā rātrau tadante syādatrānavyayamapyuṣā 
doṣā rātrimukhe rātrāvatrānavyayamapyasau   
nikaṣā tvantike madhye rakṣomātaryanavyayam 
sa pūjāyā bhṛṣārthānumatikṛcchrasamṛddhiṣu   
purataḥ prathame cā'gre'pyagrataḥ prathamāgrayoḥ 
puro'gre prathame ca syādañjasā tattvatūrṇayoḥ   
amitaḥ śīghrasākalyasammukhobhayato'ntike 
bhūyaḥ punaḥ punaḥ khyāta prabhūtārthe tvanavyayam   
nirniścaye niṣedhe ca sākalyātītayorapi 
tiro'ntarddhau tiryagarthe mitho'nyonya mitho rahaḥ   
pūrvedyuriṣyate prātaḥ pūrvedyuḥ pūrvavāsare 
śanaiḥ śanaiścare svaire nīcaiḥ svairālpayormatam   
hāntāḥ 
ha syāt sambodhane pādapūraṇe'navyaya śive 
hā viṣāde ca śoke ca duḥkhārthe'pi ca kathyate   
hi pādapūraṇe hetau viśeṣe'pyavadhāraṇe 
hī duḥkhahetāvuddiṣṭo hī vismayaviṣādayoḥ   
hāhā duḥkhe huhū harṣe gandharve'mū tvanavyaye 
he hai vyastau samastau ca hūtisambodhanārthayoḥ   
ho hau caivavidhau jñeyau sambuddhāhlānayorapi 
saha sākalyasādṛśyayaugapadyasamṛddhiṣu   
vidyamāne ca sambandhe sahaśabdaḥ prakīrttitaḥ 
aho dhigarthe śoke ca karuṇārthaviṣādayoḥ   
āho utāho dvāvetau paripraśnavicārayoḥ 
ahaśabdo niyogārthe kṣepārthe'pi nigadyate 
kṣāntāḥ maṅkṣu śīghre bhṛṣārthe ca tattvārthe'pi kkacinmatam   
ityavyayāmekārthavargaḥ 
yadyapūrvatayā kiñcinnāmātra pratibhāti ca 
tattadanviṣyatā sadbhirnāmapārāyaṇādiṣu   
tattatkavīndrairatha paṇḍitendraiḥ prayogasambodhaphaladvayāptyai 
yairnāmakaṇṭhābharaṇa kṛta taiḥ sarvajñatā svapraṇayīkṛtaiva   
svairapracāraiḥ parikalpitābhiḥ śabdārthasambodhakathāprathābhiḥ 
vyākhyābhiraprāptamudā pramoda mādhātumatraiṣa pariśramo naḥ   
etā kṛti kṛtadhiyaḥ kṛtakṛtyabhāva  māpādayantu sadaya
sadaya tu cetaḥ 
nitya maheśvarakaveḥ paribhāvayantaḥ santaḥ paronnatiratā hi bhavanti loke   
rāmānalavyomarūpaiḥ śakakāle'bhilakṣite 
koṣa viśvaprakāśākhya niramācchrīmaheśvaraḥ   
iti śrīsakalavaidyarājacakramuktāśekharasya gadyapadyavidyānidheḥ śrīmaheśvarasya kṛtau viśvaprakāśe anekārthāvyayaparicchedo dvitīyaḥ 
samāpto'ya viśvaprakāśaḥ 
śubhamastu
