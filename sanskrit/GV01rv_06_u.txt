RV anāviddhayā tanvā jaya tvaṃ sa tvā varmaṇo mahimā pipartu
RV dhanvanā gā dhanvanājiṃ jayema dhanvanā tīvrāḥ samado jayema
RV dhanuḥ śatrorapakāmaṃ kṛṇoti dhanvanā sarvāḥ pradiśo jayema
RV vakṣyantīvedā ganīganti karṇaṃ priyaṃ sakhāyaṃ pariṣasvajānā
RV yoṣeva śiṅkte vitatādhi dhanvañ jyā iyaṃ samane pārayantī
RV te ācarantī samaneva yoṣā māteva putraṃ bibhṛtāmupasthe
RV apa śatrūn vidhyatāṃ saṃvidāne ārtnī ime viṣphurantīamitrān
RV bahvīnāṃ pitā bahurasya putraściścā kṛṇoti samanāvagatya
RV iṣudhiḥ saṅkāḥ pṛtanāśca sarvāḥ pṛṣṭhe ninaddho jayati prasūtaḥ
RV rathe tiṣṭhan nayati vājinaḥ puro yatrayatra kāmayate suṣārathiḥ
RV abhīśūnāṃ mahimānaṃ panāyata manaḥ paścādanu yachanti raśmayaḥ
RV tīvrān ghoṣān kṛṇvate vṛṣapāṇayo 'śvā rathebhiḥ sahavājayantaḥ
RV avakrāmantaḥ prapadairamitrān kṣiṇanti śatrūnranapavyayantaḥ
RV rathavāhanaṃ havirasya nāma yatrāyudhaṃ nihitamasya varma
RV tatrā rathamupa śagmaṃ sadema viśvāhā vayaṃ sumanasyamānāḥ
RV svāduṣaṃsadaḥ pitaro vayodhāḥ kṛchreśritaḥ śaktīvanto gabhīrāḥ
RV citrasenā iṣubalā amṛdhrāḥ satovīrā uravo vrātasāhāḥ
RV brāhmaṇāsaḥ pitaraḥ somyāsaḥ śive no dyāvāpṛthivī anehasā
RV pūṣā naḥ pātu duritād ṛtāvṛdho rakṣā mākirno aghaśaṃsa īśata
RV suparṇaṃ vaste mṛgo asyā danto gobhiḥ saṃnaddhā patati prasūtā
RV yatrā naraḥ saṃ ca vi ca dravanti tatrāsmabhyamiṣavaḥ śarma yaṃsan
RV ṛjīte pari vṛṃdhi no 'śmā bhavatu nastanūḥ
RV somo adhi bravītu no 'ditiḥ śarma yachatu
RV ā jaṅghanti sānveṣāṃ jaghanānupa jighnate
RV aśvājani pracetaso 'śvān samatsu codaya
RV ahiriva bhogaiḥ paryeti bāhuṃ jyāyā hetiṃ paribādhamānaḥ
RV hastaghno viśvā vayunāni vidvān pumān pumāṃsaṃ pari pātu viśvataḥ
RV ālāktā yā ruruśīrṣṇyatho yasyā ayo mukham
RV idaṃ parjanyaretasa iṣvai devyai bṛhan namaḥ
RV avasṛṣṭā parā pata śaravye brahmasaṃśite
RV gachāmitrānpra padyasva mānūṣāṃ kaṃ canocchiṣaḥ
RV yatra bāṇāḥ sampatanti kumārā viśikhā iva
RV tatrā no brahmaṇas patiraditiḥ śarma yachatu viśvāhā śarma yachatu
RV marmāṇi te varmaṇā chādayāmi somastvā rājāmṛtenānu vastām
RV urorvarīyo varuṇaste kṛṇotu jayantaṃ tvānu devāmadantu
RV yo naḥ svo araṇo yaśca niṣṭyo jighāṃsati
RV devāstaṃsarve dhūrvantu brahma varma mamāntaram
