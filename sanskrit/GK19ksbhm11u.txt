sauptikaparva
nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamam 
devīṃ sarasvatīṃ vyāsaṃ tato jayamudīrayet  Bhmj 
pratāpadhāmni yāte 'staṃ ravau kamalinīpriye 
śokāvakīrṇakeśena tamobhirabhavanmahī  Bhmj 
kṣayaṃ kālena nīte 'hni śānte saṃdhyācitānale 
asthiśeṣairivāpūrṇaṃ tārakābhirabhūnnabhaḥ  Bhmj 
śyāmā pratyagraduḥkhena prasarattimirāñjanā 
babhāra tārataralāṃ nakṣatrāśrukaṇāvalīm  Bhmj 
ghore tasminniśāvaktre ghorasattvaghane vane 
śramānnidrāmupagate kṛpe sa kṛtavarmaṇi  Bhmj 
dhyāyanduryodhanavadhaṃ manyuhālāhalākulaḥ 
pratijñāṃ śatrunidhane drauṇireko vyacintayat  Bhmj 
atrāntare dadarśoccanyagrodhaśikharāśrayam 
vadhyamānamulūkena vāyasānāṃ ca maṇḍalam  Bhmj 
kākānkālabalenaitya dṛṣṭvā ghūkena pātitān 
tāmeva pāṇḍavavadhe nītiṃ drauṇiracintayat  Bhmj 
krudhā krūrāṃ dhiyaṃ kṛtvā kṛpabhojau vibodhya saḥ 
uvāca niḥśvasandīrghaṃ dahyamāna iva krudhā  Bhmj 
rājā rājasahasrāṇāmekākī viṣamasthitiḥ 
vyājādasmāsu jīvatsu bhīmasenena pātitaḥ  Bhmj 
śastraṃ vahadbhirmithyaiva dṛṣṭo 'smābhiḥ sa bhūtale 
aśrupātapratīkārākṣamaiḥ klībatarairiva  Bhmj 
deśakālau samāśritya vadhyaḥ sarvātmanā ripuḥ 
tasmātprasuptānadyaiva sānugānhanmi pāṇḍavān  Bhmj 
śrutvaitatkūṇitamanāḥ prasuptavadhapātakam 
kṛpaḥ kṛpākulo nindannabhāṣata sutaṃ svasuḥ  Bhmj 
dhigetatkutsitaṃ yuddhaṃ niśceṣṭo yatra hanyate 
bhagnadhairye 'pi no vīrāḥ praharanti yaśasvinaḥ  Bhmj 
bhavānahaṃ ca bhojaśca kartāraḥ prātareva tat 
punaryena na yāsyāmo duryodhanavadhavyathām  Bhmj 
surāṇāmapi paryāptāḥ saṃhatāḥ samare vayam 
pravekṣyāmo vairivanaṃ jvalitā iva pāvakāḥ  Bhmj 
gautamenetyabhihite saṃmate kṛtavarmaṇaḥ 
niḥśvasansāśrunyanaḥ punardraiṇirabhāṣata  Bhmj 
māyopamā vidagdheṣu nikṛtyāvicarastu ye 
spṛśantyadharmamaryādāṃ na te tatphalabhāginaḥ  Bhmj 
arjunena hataḥ karṇaḥ sa ca śāntanavo yathā 
tatkiṃ na viditaṃ loke śeṣaṃ vaktuṃ na pāryate  Bhmj 
adhunā svasti dharmāya racito 'smai mayāñjaliḥ 
parābhavamimaṃ tāvanna sahe marmadāraṇam  Bhmj 
drauṇirdāruṇasaṃkalpamityuktvā kṛtaniścayaḥ 
rathena tamasi prāyācchibiraṃ kauravadviṣam  Bhmj 
kṛṣṇasya śāsanātpūrvaṃ yāteṣu śibirāntaram 
saha sātyakinā yodhaiḥ pāṇḍaveṣu vinānugaiḥ  Bhmj 
kṛpabhojāvatikramya javāddrauṇirupāgataḥ 
śibiradvāramāsādya mahadbhūtaṃ vyalokayat  Bhmj 
hariśmaśrujaṭānetraṃ jvālānāmiva saṃcayam 
tamasāmiva saṃghātamañjanācalasaṃnibham  Bhmj 
sahasraśīrṣanayanaṃ sahasracaraṇādbhutam
sarpayajñopavītāṅgaṃ sarpakeyūrakaṅkaṇam 
bhayaṃkaraṃ bhayasyāpi kṛtāntasyeva daivatam  Bhmj 
taddṛṣṭvā ghorasaṃrambho drauṇirdurjayavikramaḥ 
sarvāyudhāvalī tasmai prāhiṇauddhairyabhūdharaḥ  Bhmj 
tānyāyudhāni sahasā jvalitānyeva tejasā 
bṛmbhamāṇaṃ mahadbhūtaṃ tadāsādya kṣayaṃ yayuḥ  Bhmj 
drauṇistatastadākāramarīcibhyo vyalokayat 
cakrāyudhasahasraṇi niḥsṛtāni sa vismayaḥ  Bhmj 
kṣaṇādgrastāyudhastena gāḍhānuśayatāpitaḥ 
prastutaṃ malinaṃ karma nininda svayamākulaḥ  Bhmj 
sa rathādavatīryātha prayatastripurāntakam 
śūlinaṃ śaraṇaṃ prāyāttatstotramukharānanaḥ  Bhmj 
ātmopahāraṃ rudrāya tasmindātuṃ samudyate 
jvālākulā hemavedī purastātpratyadṛśyata  Bhmj 
tadudbhūtairgaṇairghorairnānāprāṇimukhodaraiḥ 
nānāpraharaṇairdīptairvyoma kṣipramapūrayat  Bhmj 
tato vedyāṃ mahāvahnau jvalite droṇanandanaḥ 
bhaktyātmānaṃ trinetrāya nivedyābhyapatatsvayam  Bhmj 
tuṣṭo 'tha bhagavānetya svayamandhakasūdanaḥ 
tamuvāca svamevāṃśamuttiṣṭhāṅgirasāṃ vara  Bhmj 
saṃsthānāya mayaitāvatkṛtaṃ kaiṭabhavidviṣaḥ 
ātgānaṃ viddhi me kṛṣṇaṃ sa me bahumataḥ sadā  Bhmj 
kālapakvāstu pāñcālā naiṣāṃ rakṣāsti kutracit 
gṛhāṇa khaḍgametena dārayaitānaśaṅkitaḥ  Bhmj 
ityuktvā bhairave dīptaṃ khaḍgaṃ datvā tirohite 
tadā vikārādduṣprekṣyo drauṇiḥ śibiramāviśat  Bhmj 
dvāri bhojakṛpau dhṛtvā vidrutānāṃ vadhāya saḥ 
praviśya śayane śubhre dhṛṣṭadyumnaṃ vyalokayat  Bhmj 
tārahārāṃśunakharaṃ dīptakesarisaṃnibham 
ghūrṇamānaśikhairdīpairvismayādiva vīkṣitam  Bhmj 
anekaraṇasaṃmardaśrāntairnidrāvimohitaiḥ 
adhiṣṭhitaṃ bahubhaṭairmuktaparyāṇavāhanaiḥ  Bhmj 
taṃ dṛṣṭvā kopaśikhinā dahyamānaḥ smaranpituḥ 
uttiṣṭha re gurughneti drauṇiruktvā padāspṛśat  Bhmj 
bodhitaḥ sahasā tena saṃbhramasvīkṛtāyudhaḥ 
droṇaputraṃ parijñāya cakampe drupadātmajaḥ  Bhmj 
drauṇistamutthitaṃ vegānmālyavadbhiḥ śiroruhaiḥ 
ākṛṣyāpātayatkaṇṭhe gāḍhaṃ niṣpīḍya vihvalam  Bhmj 
niṣpiṣyamāṇaḥ saṃrambhādraktodgārākulānanaḥ 
kupitvā pādayordraiṇiṃ nakhairdantaiśca visphuran  Bhmj 
śastreṇa chindhi māṃ tūrṇaṃ mā padeneti gadgadam 
kaṇṭharuddhasvaraḥ śvāsādasphuṭākṣaramabhyadhāt  Bhmj 
tamabravīddroṇasutaḥ pāpa padbhyāṃ nihanyase 
lokānāṃ śastrapūtānāṃ na pāpaṃ gurughātinām  Bhmj 
ityuktvā caraṇāghātairjarjaraṃ tamapothayat 
tasminhate tadvimardaśabdena bubudhe janaḥ  Bhmj 
 dhṛṣṭadyumnavadhaḥ    
tato drauṇiṃ samālekya ripuraktacchaṭāṅkitam 
pāñcālā draupadeyāśca kālo 'yamiti menire  Bhmj 
nidrāśeṣāruṇadṛśāṃ śastrāṇyānayatāṃ mithaḥ 
krośatāṃ varma varmeti teṣāmāsīnmahāsvanaḥ  Bhmj 
kva gajaḥ kva rathaḥ kvāśvaḥ kva cāpaṃ kva śilīmukhāḥ 
iti bruvāṇānkhaṅgena drauṇiściccheda tānkrudhā  Bhmj 
hatvottamaujasaṃ padbhyāṃ kaṇṭhe kaṭhinavikramaḥ 
gadāhastaṃ yudhāmanyuṃ niṣpipeṣa virāviṇam  Bhmj 
tataḥ khaṅgena śūrāṇāṃ śirāṃsi bhuvi pātayan 
kālarātryāḥ sa vidadhe vinodaṃ kandukairiva  Bhmj 
kṛṣṇāsutānāpatato draupadeyānasaṃbhramāt 
cakāra karavālena sa kālakavalocitān  Bhmj 
prativindhye hate vīre sutasome nipātite 
śrutakīrtai śatānīke chinne ca śrutavarmaṇi  Bhmj 
śikhaṇḍī kṛṣṭakodaṇḍaḥ śarairdraiṇimapūrayat 
droṇaputro 'pi khaḍgena kruddhastaṃ vidadhe dvidhā  Bhmj 
 draupadeyaśikhaṇḍivadhaḥ    
kopādācāryaputreṇa vadhyamānāḥ prabhadrakāḥ 
matsyasṛñjayapāñcālāḥ kālaṃ dadṛśurantike  Bhmj 
asitaṃ raktavasanaṃ raktamālyānulepanam 
hasantaṃ vīkṣya taṃ vīrāḥ saṃhāraṃ menire sphṛṭam  Bhmj 
ghanāndhakāre vīrāṇāṃ rathakuñjaravājinām 
yodhānāṃ ca kṣayaṃ dvāri cakraturbhojagautamau  Bhmj 
evamakṣauhiṇīṃ hatvā vasāraktānulepanaḥ 
niryayau raudracarito drauṇirbhairavavigrahaḥ  Bhmj 
sa gatvā sahitastūrṇaṃ hārdikyena kṛpeṇa ca 
dadarśa bhuvi rājānaṃ bhagnaśaktiṃ suyodhanam  Bhmj 
vārayantaṃ gadāgreṇa muhuḥ kravyādamaṇḍalam 
vyathayā marmadinyā bhakṣayantaṃ vasuṃdharām  Bhmj 
śoṇitaṃ vadanodvāntaṃ parimṛjyāśu tasya te 
sarvapāñcalasaṃhārakathayā vavṛṣuḥ sudhām  Bhmj 
sa kiṃciccheṣajīvo 'pi tacchrutvā vṛttakandharaḥ 
paryantagrasitāvyaktabhāṣitaiḥ praśaśaṃsa tān  Bhmj 
sa pariṣvajya yatnena drauṇiṃ sāśruvilocanam 
punarnaḥ saṃgamaḥ svarge bhūyādityavadacchanaiḥ  Bhmj 
aṅge vidhāya dayitāṃ sa gadāṃ sadaiva bhuktāṃ ciraṃ vasumatīmavagūhya dorbhyām 
karṇāvalambitabalodgatajīvacāro vīraḥ śanaiḥ suravadhūpraṇayī babhūva  Bhmj 
 duryodhanasvargagamanam    
atrāntare prayātāyāṃ kṣapāyāṃ kṣapitārayaḥ 
jajñire pāṇḍavāḥ karma dāruṇaṃ drauṇinā kṛtam  Bhmj 
muktānmahābhayāttasmātkṛṣṇā śrutvā hatānsutān 
anujāṃśca sapāñcālānpapāta bhuvi mūrcchitā  Bhmj 
śanakairlabdhasaṃjñā sā vilapantī sulocanā 
pārthānāṃ śokavidhurā hṛdayāni vyadārayat  Bhmj 
yāvanna viśikhaiḥ kṛttamuttamāṅgaṃ vikarmaṇaḥ 
drauṇerdṛṣṭaṃ mayā tāvajjīvitasyāyamañjaliḥ  Bhmj 
ityuktvā draupadī duḥkhādvīkṣamāṇā vṛkodaram 
novāca kiṃcitsaṃtāpānmaraṇe kṛtaniścayā  Bhmj 
tato bhīmaḥ priyāśokaśūlenābhyāhato hṛdi 
prayayau rathamāruhya droṇaputrajighāṃsayā  Bhmj 
vrajatastasya kālagneriva lokāndidhakṣataḥ 
dhūmamālābhramaṃ cakre lalāṭe bhrukuṭiḥ kṣaṇam  Bhmj 
raudro rudrāśrayo drauṇiḥ kruddhaśca pavanātmajaḥ 
gacchāmaḥ pṛṣṭhatastasmādityūce pāṇḍavānhariḥ  Bhmj 
tataḥ kirīṭipramukhāḥ saha sātyakinā yayuḥ 
rathinaḥ seṣavaḥ sarve pāṇḍuputrā yuyutsavaḥ  Bhmj 
tataste drauṇimāsādya prasuptavadhapātakāt 
dhvastacchāyaṃ kriyāhīnaṃ kopenākulatāṃ yayuḥ  Bhmj 
abhidrute bhīmasene drauṇiṃ nakulasārathau
pādacārī ghṛtābhyaktaḥ sarasvatyāstaṭe kṣaṇam 
apāṇḍavaṃ jagatkartuṃ brahmaśīrṣāstramādade  Bhmj 
tadvināśāya pārtho 'pi brahmāstramasṛjatkṛtī 
yenābhavandiśo vyāptāḥ kampātaṅkataraṅgitāḥ  Bhmj 
atrāntare samabhyāyānnāradena saha svayam 
muniḥ satyavatīsūnurbhagavānbhūtabhāvanaḥ  Bhmj 
so 'bravīddroṇatanayaṃ jiṣṇuṃ ca kṣayaśaṅkitaḥ 
ayaṃ divyāstrasaṃmarde hatavīraṃ jagaddahet  Bhmj 
pārtha saṃhara divyāstraṃ tvaṃ ca drauṇe girā mama 
akāṇḍe mā bhavantvete lokāḥ pralayabhāginaḥ  Bhmj 
ityukte muninā kṣipraṃ saṃjahārāstramarjunaḥ 
drauṇiḥ prayogakuśalo neśo 'bhūdastrasaṃhṛtau  Bhmj 
tataḥ prajvalite loke vyathite suramaṇḍale 
abhimanyuvadhūgarbhe drauṇirastramapātayat  Bhmj 
bhūyastatpratiṣedhāya putrasaṃtānavāñchayā 
nindandroṇasutaṃ pārthastadastraṃ kupito 'sṛjat  Bhmj 
tataḥ kṛṣṇo 'vadajjiṣṇuṃ viṣaṇṇaṃ saṃtatikṣayāt 
mā śuco dhairyajaladhe na te vaṃśo vinaṅkṣyati  Bhmj 
jātvenaṃ jīvayiṣyāmi dagdhamapyastratejasā 
uttarāyāḥ sthitaṃ garbhe saubhadratanayaṃ śiśum  Bhmj 
iti bruvāṇe govinde pārthāstre jvalite bhuvi 
uvāca droṇatanayaṃ vyāso devarṣiṇā saha  Bhmj 
tyaja cūḍāmaṇiṃ rakṣa jīvaratnaṃ mahāstrataḥ 
aśaktaśca pramādī ca jānīṣe na yathocitam  Bhmj 
vatsarāṇāṃ sahasra tvaṃ visrarogī samāhitaḥ 
durbhikṣavyañjako duḥkhī vijane vicariṣyasi  Bhmj 
iti śapto gurusutaḥ kopādvyāsamabhāṣata 
vyasane patitaḥ kasmātkṣipto 'haṃ saṃkaṭe tvayā  Bhmj 
yatra yatra bhaviṣyāmi tatra tatra mamānugaḥ 
tvamapi pratiśāpena mune nityaṃ bhaviṣyasi  Bhmj 
ityuktvāstropahārāya cūḍāratnaṃ vitīrya saḥ 
prayayau droṇatanayo muniśca muninā saha  Bhmj 
tataḥ praśānte brahmāstre pāṇḍavāḥ saha śauriṇā 
cūḍāratnaṃ samādāya yājñasenyai nyavedayan  Bhmj 
 aiṣīkamastram    
evaṃ prasādya pāñcālīṃ śokārtāḥ pāṇḍunandanāḥ 
pāñcālakadanaṃ ghoraṃ dhyāyanto na yayurvṛtim  Bhmj 
saṃjayeneti kathite śrutvā rājāmbikāsutaḥ 
papāta mūrcchito mohāllīnaśokānalaḥ kṣaṇam  Bhmj 
taṃ śītasalilāpūrairāśvāsyovāca saṃjayaḥ 
mantriṇaśca priyānputrāñśocantaṃ vyathitendriyam  Bhmj 
svapnamāyāvilasitaiḥ paryante śokamohadaiḥ 
saṃsāravibhramairdhīrāḥ spṛśyante na manīṣiṇaḥ  Bhmj 
devaindrajālikakṛtaiśchāyākrīḍanakairiva 
putrairdhanaiśca dāraiśca na muhyanti madāśayāḥ  Bhmj 
upekṣitastvayaivāyaṃ durnayādbāndhavakṣayaḥ 
vṛtte tasminnamithyaiva śokaṃ kartumihārhasi  Bhmj 
prabhāvaprabhavairbhāvairmāyāvibhavabhāvitaiḥ 
abhāvaniṣṭhāparyantaiḥ satāṃ na kriyate bhramaḥ  Bhmj 
svakarmamudrite loke niyatau pralayodadhau 
kalayāmi na kālasya kaśca tāvati vartate  Bhmj 
ityādi saṃjayamukhā nṛpamugramohabhagnaṃ svayaṃ vihitadurnayadṛṣṭapāram 
helāvalatkalabhacñcalakarṇatālalolāṃ bhavasthitikathāmasakṛttamūcuḥ  Bhmj 
iti kṣemendraviracitāyāṃ bhāratamañjaryāṃ sauptikaparva
