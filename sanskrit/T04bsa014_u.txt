Śikṣāsamuccayakārikā Śsk
yadā mama pareṣāṃ ca bhayaṃ duḥkhaṃ ca na priyam 
tadātmanaḥ ko viśeṣo yattaṃ rakṣāmi netaram  Śsk 
duḥkhāntaṃ kartukāmena sukhāntaṃ gantumicchatā 
śraddhāmūlaṃ dṛḍhīkṛtya bodhau kāryā matirdṛḍhā  Śsk 
śikṣādaro mahāyānādbodhisattvasya saṃvaraḥ 
marmasthānānyato vidyādyenānāpattiko bhavet  Śsk 
ātmabhāvasya bhogānāṃ tryadhvavṛtteḥ śubhasya ca 
utsargaḥ sarvasattvebhyastadrakṣā śuddhivardhanam  Śsk 
paribhogāya sattvānāmātmabhāvādi dīyate 
arakṣite kuto bhogaḥ ki dattaṃ yanna bhujyate  Śsk 
tasmātsattvopabhogārthamātmabhāvādi pālayet 
kalyāṇamitrānutsargāt sūtrāṇāṃ ca sadekṣaṇāt  Śsk 
tatrātmabhāve kā rakṣā yadanarthavivarjanam 
kenaitallabhyate sarvaṃ niṣphalaspandavarjanāt  Śsk 
etatsidhyetsadā smṛtyā smṛtistīvrādarādbhavet 
ādaraḥ śamamāhātmyaṃ jñātvātāpena jāyate  Śsk 
samāhito yathābhūtaṃ prajānātītyavadanmuniḥ 
śamācca na caleccitaṃ bāhyaceṣṭā nivartanāt  Śsk 
sarvatrāpacalo mandamatisnigdhābhibhāṣaṇāt 
āvavarjayejjanaṃ bhavyamādeyaścāpi jāyate  Śsk 
anādeyaṃ tu taṃ lokaḥ paribhūya jināṅkuram 
bhasmacchanno yathā vahniḥ pacyeta narakādiṣu  Śsk 
ratnameghe jinenoktastena saṅkṣepasaṃvaraḥ 
yenāprasādaḥ sattvānāṃ tadyatnena vivarjayet  Śsk 
eṣā rakṣātmabhāvasya bhaiṣajyavasānādibhiḥ 
ātmatṛṣṇopabhogāttu kliṣṭāpattiḥ prajāyate  Śsk 
sukṛṭārambhiṇā bhāvyaṃ mātrajñena ca sarvataḥ 
iti śikṣāpadādasya bhogarakṣā na duṣkarā  Śsk 
svārthavipāka vaitṛṣṇyācchubhaṃ saṃrakṣitaṃ bhavet 
paścātāpaṃ na kurvīta na ca kṛtvā prakāśayet  Śsk 
lābhasatkārabhītaḥ syādunnatiṃ varjayetsadā 
bodhisattvaḥ prasannaḥ syāddharme vimatimutsṛjet  Śsk 
śodhitasyātmabhāvasyabhogaḥ pathyo bhaviṣyati 
samyaksiddhasya bhaktasya niṣkaṇasyeva dehinām  Śsk 
tṛṇacchanna yathā śasyaṃ rogaiḥ sīdati naidhate 
buddhāṅkurastathā vṛddhiṃ kleśacchanno na gacchati  Śsk 
ātmabhāvasya kā śuddhiḥ pāpakleśaviśodhanam 
sambuddhottayarthasāreṇa yatnabhāve tvapāyagaḥ  Śsk 
kṣameta śrutameṣeta saṃśrayeta vanaṃ tataḥ 
sāmādhānāya yujyeta bhāvayedaśubhādikam  Śsk 
bhogaśuddhiṃ ca jānīyātsamyagājīvaśodhanāt 
śūnyatākaruṇāgarbheceṣṭitātpuṇyaśodhanam  Śsk 
grahītāraḥ subahavaḥ svalpaṃ ceda manena kim 
na cātitṛptijanakaṃ vardhanīyamidaṃ tataḥ  Śsk 
ātmabhāvasya kā vṛddhirbalānālasyavardhanam 
śūnyatākaruṇāgarbhāddānādbhogasya vardhanam  Śsk 
kṛtvādāveva yatnena vyavasāyāśayau dṛḍhau 
karuṇāṃ ca puraskṛtya yateta śubhavṛddhaye  Śsk 
bhadracaryāvidhiḥ kāryo vandanādiḥ sahādarāt 
śraddhādīnāṃ sadābhyāso maitrī buddhādyanusmṛtiḥ  Śsk 
sarvāvasthāsu sattvārtho dharmadānaṃ nirāmiṣam 
bodhicittaṃ ca puṇyasya vṛddhihetuḥ samāsataḥ  Śsk 
siddhiḥ samyakprahāṇānāmapramādāviyojanāt 
smṛtyātha samprajanyena yoniśaścintanena ca  Śsk
