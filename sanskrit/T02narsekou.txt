lohaṃ tīvrāgnisaṃtaptaṃ vedhayedāsamantataḥ  Sekoddeśa 
tethaivaikapradeśe 'pi saṃsthitaṃ sukhamakṣaram 
cittaṃ kāmāgnisaṃtaptaṃ vedhayedāsamantataḥ  Sekoddeśa 
yathā ca viddhalohānāṃ naiva malo bhavet kvacit 
tathā ca viddhacittānāṃ na vāsanā bhavet kvacit  Sekoddeśa 
hematvaṃ hi gataṃ lohamagninā nirmalaṃ bhavet 
bhūyo bhūyastathā cittaṃ nirmalaṃ rāgavahninā  Sekoddeśa 
yathā mahāmaṇisparśādyāti śīlā pradīptatām 
tathākṣarasukhasparśāt sukhatāṃ cittameti vai  Sekoddeśa 
kimatra bahunoktena lokasaṃvṛtisatyataḥ 
avitarkyo rasasadbhāvo lohasyāvedhanaṃ prati  Sekoddeśa 
vitarkyo jñānasadbhāvaḥ kiṃ punaḥ paramārthataḥ 
malāgantukaliptasya cittasyāvedhanaṃ prati  Sekoddeśa 
nāgantuko malaścitte na cittāccirakālikaḥ 
na cittena vinā jāto na citte saṃsthito 'vyayaḥ  Sekoddeśa 
yadyāgantuka eva syāccittaṃ prāgamalaṃ tadā 
cirakālī yadā cittāttasyaiva sambhavaḥ kutaḥ  Sekoddeśa 
yadi cittaṃ vinā jātastadā khakusumaṃ yathā 
yadi sthitaḥ sadā citte na naśyet sarvatastadā  Sekoddeśa 
tāmrasya kālimā yadvadrasayogena naśyati 
na tasya sattvatā naśyennirmalatvena yā sthitā  Sekoddeśa 
tadvaccittamalaḥ śūnyatāyogena praṇaśyati 
na tasya jñānatā naśyennirmalatvena yā sthitā  Sekoddeśa 
rasaviddho yathā loho na punarlohatāṃ vrajet 
sukhaviddhaṃ tathā cittaṃ na punarduḥkhatāṃ vrajet  Sekoddeśa 
na virāgāt paraṃ pāpaṃ na puṇyaṃ sukhataḥ param 
ato 'kṣarasukhe cittaṃ veśanīyaṃ sadā nṛpa  Sekoddeśa 
vaktuṃ na śakyate saukhyaṃ kumāryā surataṃ vinā 
yauvane surataṃ prāpya svato vetti mahāsukham  Sekoddeśa 
evaṃ na śakyate vaktuṃ samādhirahitaiḥ sukham 
samādhāvakṣaraṃ prāpya svato vindanti yoginaḥ  Sekoddeśa 
akṣarajasukhajñāne sarvajñairapi śaṃkyate 
tathā kvacinna kartavyo virāgo manaso 'sukhāt  Sekoddeśa 
cyutervirāgasaṃbhūtirvirāgādduḥkhasaṃbhavaḥ 
duḥkhāddhātukṣayaḥ puṃsāṃ kṣayān mṛtyuriti smṛtaḥ  Sekoddeśa 
mṛtyoḥ punarbhavasteṣāṃ bhavān mṛtyuścyutiḥ punaḥ 
evaṃ virāgasaṃbhūteḥ sattvānāṃ nānyathā bhavaḥ  Sekoddeśa 
tasmāt sarvaprayatnena cyutirāgaṃ vivarjayet 
yenākṣarasukhaṃ yāti yogī saṃsārabandhanāt  Sekoddeśa 
kāmuko 'pi virāgānna kāmaśāstraṃ samīhate 
mayokte kiṃ punastantre yogī duḥkhaṃ samīhate  Sekoddeśa 
śukrākṣarasvabhāvena sādhayet paramākṣaram 
ādhāre cyutimāpanne ādheyasya virāgatā  Sekoddeśa 
ādhārādheyasaṃbandho yāvannākṣaratāṃ vrajet 
cittamakṣaratāprāptaṃ nādhārādheyalakṣaṇam  Sekoddeśa 
akṣarodbhavakāyasya ūrṇṇācakragatasya tu 
ālikālisamāyogo hūṃkāro neṣyate nṛpa  Sekoddeśa 
bimbaṃ śūnyodbhavaṃ hetuḥ phalamakṣarajaṃ sukham 
phalena mudrito heturhetunā mudritaṃ phalam  Sekoddeśa 
śūnyatā bimbadhṛgghetuḥ karuṇākṣaradhṛk phalam 
śūnyatākaruṇābhinnaṃ bodhicittaṃ tadacyutam  Sekoddeśa 
nirvāṇarahitaṃ bimbaṃ saṃsārātītamakṣaram 
śāścatocchedanirmuttastatoryogo 'dvayo 'paraḥ  Sekoddeśa 
abhāvo nāsti bimbasya abhāvodbhūtalakṣaṇāt 
bhāvo nāstyakṣarasyāpi bhāvasambhūtalakṣaṇāt  Sekoddeśa 
bhāvābhāvasamāyogo vajrayogo 'dvayo 'paraḥ 
rūpārūpavinirmuktaḥ pratiseneva darpaṇe  Sekoddeśa 
bimbaṃ na bhavamāyāti nāpi nirvāṇamakṣaram 
anyonyāliṅgitaṃ śāntaṃ napuṃsakapadaṃ param  Sekoddeśa 
prajñāhetorajātatvāt prajñāhetūdbhavaṃ phalam 
prajñāhetorajātatvāt prajñājātaṃ na hetujam  Sekoddeśa 
ato na hetujaṃ jñānaṃ prajñājñānamanuttaram 
phalena hetunānyonyaṃ na parasparamudraṇam  Sekoddeśa 
hetuḥ phalaṃ ca yat sarvaṃ tat pratītyasamudbhavam 
anyonyamudritaṃ bimbaṃ notpannaṃ na ca nirvṛtam  Sekoddeśa 
prajñā cātyantanirvṛtā utpannaśca paro 'kṣaraḥ 
hetuphalavinirmuktirna parasparamudraṇam  Sekoddeśa 
ajātasyāniruddhasya yajjñeyasyeha darśanam 
tat svacittasya nānyasya bāhyajñeyavibhāgataḥ  Sekoddeśa 
ato na cātmanātmānaṃ mudrituṃ śakyate kvacit 
kiṃ chinatti mahākhaḍga ātmānamātmadhārayā  Sekoddeśa 
bandhyāduhitṛsaṃgena svapne saukhyaṃ yathā bhavet 
gaganodbhavabimbena sevitena tathātmanaḥ  Sekoddeśa 
na prajñā nāpyupāyo 'sau sahajaḥ prajñayā saha 
āpūrṇaścaiva saukhyena sarvāvaraṇavarjitaḥ  Sekoddeśa 
ākāśavannirāvaraṇo viṣayendriyavarjitaḥ

svayambhūrvajrasattvo 'sau mahārthaḥ paramākṣaraḥ 
mahārāgo mahāsattvaḥ sarvasattvaratiṃkaraḥ  Sekoddeśa 
bodhisattvo mahādveṣaḥ kleśakṣayī mahāripuḥ 
samayasattvo mahāmoho mūḍhadhīmohasūdanaḥ  Sekoddeśa

samayaścandrāmṛtaṃ śukramacyutistasya bhakṣaṇam 
samayasattvo 'nayā vṛttyā mūḍhadhīmohasūdanaḥ  Sekoddeśa 
ekatvaṃ sarvavajrāṇāṃ prajñākāyākṣaraiḥ saha 
mahākrodho mahāśatrurmārāṇāṃ krodharūpiṇām  Sekoddeśa 
mahākṣarasukhopāyāt sattvārthaṃ na tyajet kvacit 
sattvān moktuṃ mahālobhaḥ kṣaralobhaniṣūdanaḥ  Sekoddeśa 
ete hyuktāḥ ṣaḍākārā akṣarajñānavedanā 
vijñānarūpasaṃskārasaṃjñāḥ skandhakulāni te  Sekoddeśa

manaścakṣuḥśrutikāyanāsajihvāstathaiva ca  Sekoddeśa 
śabdarasadharmadhātukagandhaspraṣṭavyarūpiṇaḥ

kāyaguhyendriyacittaṃ trividhaṃ maṇḍalaṃ sadā 
prajñopāyatrivajrāṇāṃ jāyate vajrasattvatā  Sekoddeśa
