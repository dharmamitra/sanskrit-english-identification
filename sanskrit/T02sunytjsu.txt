om namaḥ śrībhagavatyai vajravārāhyai 
ānandāmbudhimaṇḍanādupagatā sambuddhalakṣmīrasau
sarvākāraguṇānvitā jagadaghadhvāntaughavidhvaṃsinī 
jyotirjñānanidherdhṛtiḥ smṛtinidheḥ śāntirmanīṣānidheḥ
pāyādvajravirāsinī bhagavatī lokatrayaṃ durgateḥ  Tjs 
śrīvajradevīpadapadmareṇu

prajñākṛpādvaitapadaṃ praśastam  Tjs 
vajradevīpadadvaṃdvavandanānandavarttinā

subhāṣitaprade nityaṃ matiḥ kalpanasotsukā  Tjs

satsvastikānabhilikhetkrameṇa vāmahastena  Tjs 
ākṛṣya vajradevīmpraviśya mantrākṣareṣu baddhvā paritoṣayet 
suvidhānāt jaḥ huṃ vaṃ horiti paṭhitvā  Tjs

bhakṣyairbhojyairlehyaiḥ peyaiścoṣyaiḥ sakāmaguṇaiḥ  Tjs 
vividhairvalaiḥ samadanairupahāraiḥ pañcabhiratiparārdhyaiḥ

evaṃ lavdhanimitto mudrāṃ mahatīmavāpnoti  Tjs

iti tattvajñānasaṃsiddhau bhāvanāvidhiḥ
