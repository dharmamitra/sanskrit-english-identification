pratikāyaṃ anirvacanīyamukhāni pratimukhaṃ cānirvacanīyajihvā nirmāyasvaparapāpān aśeṣān diśet svaparapuṇyāni cānumodayet aciram abhisambodhiprāptān buddhān bhagavato dharmacakrapravartanāya adhyeṣayet ye dharmacakraṃ pravartya āyuḥ saṃskārān parijihīrṣante tānāsaṃsāram aparinirvāṇāya prārthayeta teṣāṃ samastakuśalānām anuttarasamyaksambodhau pariṇāmanāṃ kuryāt yathā sūtre āgatāni tathā pratyaṅgapadāni savistaraṃ paṭhet tataḥ saptapūjānantaraṃ sarvasattvān avalambya triśaraṇagamanapadāni triḥ paṭhet tataḥ bodhicittamutpādayetyathā vidhau prāpyate tathā ca kuryāt antaśaśca evambuddhaṃ
