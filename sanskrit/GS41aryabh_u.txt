daśagītikāpāda
AB a praṇipatya ekam anekam kam satyām devatām param brahma
ABc āryabhaṭas trīṇi gadati gaṇitam kālakriyām golam
ABa vargaakṣarāṇi varge avarge avargaakṣarāṇi kāt ṅmau yas
ABc khadvinavake svarās nava varge avarge nava antyavarge vā
ABa yugaravibhagaṇās khyughṛ śaśi cayagiyiṅuśuchlṛ ku ṅiśibuṇḷṣkhṛ prāk
ABc śani ḍhuṅvighva guru khricyubha kuja bhadlijhnukhṛ bhṛgubudhasaurās
ABa candraucca rjuṣkhidha budha suguśithṛna bhṛgu jaṣabikhuchṛ śeṣa arkās
ABc buphinaca pātavilomās budhaahni ajaarkaudayāt ca laṅkāyām
ABa kaahas manavas ḍha manuyugās śkha gatās te ca manuyugās chnā ca
ABc kalpaādes yugapādās ga ca gurudivasāt ca bhāratāt pūrvam
ABa śaśirāśayas ṭha cakram te aṃśakalāyojanāni yavañaguṇās
ABc prāṇena eti kalām bham khayugaaṃśe grahajavas bhavaaṃśe arkas
ABa nṛṣi yojanam ñilā bhūvyāsas arkaindvor ghriñā giṇa ka meros
ABc bhṛgugurubudhaśanibhaumās śaśiṅañaṇanamaaṃśakās samaarkasamās
ABa bhaapakramas grahaaṃśās śaśivikṣepas apamaṇḍalāt jhaardham
ABc śanigurukuja khakagaardham bhṛgubudha kha scaaṅgulas ghahastas nā
ABa budhabhṛgukujaguruśani navarāṣaha gatvā aṃśakān prathamapātās
ABc savitur amīṣām ca tathā dvāñakhisāhdāhlyakhicya mandauccam
ABa jhaardhāni mandavṛttam śaśinas cha gachaghaḍhachajha yathā uktebhyas
ABc jhāgḍaglārdhadḍa tathā śanigurukujabhṛgubudhauccaśīghrebhyas
ABa mandāt ṅakhadajaḍā vakriṇām dvitīye pade caturthe ca
ABc jāṇaklachlajhna uccāt śīghrāt giyiṅaśa kuvāyukakṣyāantyā
ABa makhi bhaki phakhi dhakhi ṇakhi ñakhi ṅakhi hasjha skaki kiṣga śghaki kighva
ABc ghlaki kigra hakya dhaki kica sga jhaśa ṅva kla pta pha cha kalāardhajyās
ABa daśagītikasūtram idam bhūgrahacaritam bhapañjare jñātvā
ABc grahabhagaṇaparibhramaṇam sas yāti bhittvā param brahma
gaṇitapāda
AB a brahmakuśaśibudhabhṛguravikujagurukoṇabhagaṇān namaskṛtya
ABc āryabhaṭas tu iha nigadati kusumapure abhyarcitam jñānam
ABa ekam daśa ca śatam ca sahasram ayutaniyute tathā prayutam
ABc koṭiarbudam ca vṛndam sthānāt sthānam daśaguṇam syāt
ABa vargas samacaturaśras phalam ca sadṛśadvayasya saṃvargas
ABc sadṛśatrayasaṃvargas ghanas tathā dvādaśaaśris syāt
ABa bhāgam haret avargān nityam dviguṇena vargamūlena
ABc vargāt varge śuddhe labdham sthānaantare mūlam
ABa aghanāt bhajet dvitīyāt triguṇena ghanasya mūlavargeṇa
ABc vargas tripūrvaguṇitas śodhyas prathamāt ghanas ca ghanāt
ABa tribhujasya phalaśarīram samadalakoṭībhujāardhasaṃvargas
ABc ūrdhvabhujātadsaṃvargaardham sas ghanas ṣaṣaśris iti
ABa samapariṇāhasyaardham viṣkambhaardhahatam eva vṛttaphalam
ABc tadnijamūlena hatam ghanagolaphalam niravaśeṣam
ABa āyāmaguṇe pārśve tadyogahṛte svapātalekhe
ABc vistarayogaardhaguṇe jñeyam kṣetraphalam āyāme
ABa sarveṣām kṣetrāṇām prasādhya pārśve phalam tadabhyāsas
ABc paridhes ṣaṣbhāgajyā viṣkambhaardhena sā tulyā
ABa caturadhikam śatam aṣṭaguṇam dvāṣaṣṭis tathā sahasrāṇām
ABc ayutadvayaviṣkambhasya āsannas vṛttapariṇāhas
ABa samavṛttaparidhipādam chindyāt tribhujāt caturbhujāt ca eva
ABc samacāpajyāardhāni tu viṣkambhaardhe yathā iṣṭāni
ABa prathamāt cāpajyāardhāt yair ūnam khaṇḍitam dvitīyaardham
ABc tadprathamajyāardhaaṃśais tais tais ūnāni śeṣāṇi
ABa vṛttam bhrameṇa sādhyam ca caturbhujam ca karṇābhyām
ABc sādhyā jalena samabhūs adhasūrdhvam lambakena eva
ABa śaṅkos pramāṇavargam chāyāvargeṇa saṃyutam kṛtvā
ABc yat tasya vargamūlam viṣkambhaardham svavṛttasya
ABa śaṅkuguṇam śaṅkubhujāvivaram śaṅkubhujayor viśeṣahṛtam
ABc yat labdham sā chāyā jñeyā śaṅkos svamūlāt hi
ABa chāyāguṇitam chāyāagravivaram ūnena bhājitam koṭī
ABc śaṅkuguṇā koṭī sā chāyābhaktā bhujā bhavati
ABa yas ca eva bhujāvargas koṭīvargas ca karṇavargas sas
ABc vṛtte śarasaṃvargas ardhajyāvargas sas khalu dhanuṣos
ABa grāsaūne dve vṛtte grāsaguṇe bhājayet pṛthaktvena
ABc grāsaūnayogalabdhau saṃpātaśarau parasparatas
ABa iṣṭam viekam dalitam sapūrvam uttaraguṇam samukham madhyam
ABc iṣṭaguṇitam iṣṭadhanam tu atha vā ādiantam padaardhahatam
ABa gacchas aṣṭauttaraguṇitāt dviguṇaādiuttaraviśeṣavargayutāt
ABc mūlam dviguṇaādiūnam svauttarabhajitam sarūpaardham
ABa ekauttaraādiupacites gaccaādiekauttaratrisaṃvargas
ABc ṣaṣbhaktas sas citighanas saekapadaghanas vimūlas vā
ABa saekasagacchapadānām kramāt trisaṃvargitasya ṣaṣṭhas aṃśas
ABc vargacitighanas sas bhavet citivargas ghanacitighanas ca
ABa samparkasya hi vargāt viśodhayet eva vargasamparkam
ABc yat tasya bhavati ardham vidyāt guṇakārasaṃvargam
ABa dvikṛtiguṇāt saṃvargāt dviantaravargeṇa saṃyutāt mūlam
ABc antarayuktam hīnam tadguṇakāradvayam dalitam
ABa mūlaphalam saphalam kālamūlaguṇam ardhamūlakṛtiyuktam
ABc tadmūlam mūlaardhaūnam kālahṛtam svamūlaphalam
ABa trairāśikaphalarāśim tam atha icchārāśinā hatam kṛtvā
ABc labdham pramāṇabhajitam tasmāt icchāphalam idam syāt
ABa chedās parasparahatās bhavanti guṇakārabhāgahārāṇām
ABc chedaguṇam sachedam parasparam tat savarṇatvam
ABa guṇakārās bhāgaharās bhāgaharās te bhavanti guṇakārās
ABc yas kṣepas sas apacayas apacayas kṣepas ca viparīte
ABa rāśiūnam rāśiūnam gacchadhanam piṇḍitam pṛthaktvena
ABc viekena padena hṛtam sarvadhanam tat bhavati evam
ABa gulikāantareṇa vibhajet dvayos puruṣayos tu rūpakaviśeṣam
ABc labdham gulikāmūlyam yadi arthakṛtam bhavati tulyam
ABa bhakte vilomavivare gatiyogena anulomavivare dvau
ABc gatiantareṇa labdhau dviyogakālau atītaaiṣyau
ABa adhikaagrabhāgahāram chindyāt ūnaagrabhāgahāreṇa
ABc śeṣaparasparabhaktam matiguṇam agraantare kṣiptam
ABa adhasupariguṇitam antyayujūnaagrachedabhājite śeṣam
ABc adhikaagrachedaguṇam dvichedaagram adhikaagrayutam
kālakriyāpāda
AB a varṣam dvādaśamāsās triṃśatdivasas bhavet sas māsas tu
ABc ṣaṣṭis nāḍyas divasas ṣaṣṭis ca vināḍikā nāḍī
ABa guruakṣarāṇi ṣaṣṭis vināḍikā ārkṣī ṣaṭ eva vā prāṇās
ABc evam kālavibhāgas kṣetravibhāgas tathā bhagaṇāt
ABa bhagaṇās dvayos dvayos ye viśeṣaśeṣās yuge dviyogās te
ABc raviśaśinakṣatragaṇās sammiśrās ca vyatīpātās
ABa svauccabhagaṇās svabhagaṇais viśeṣitās svauccanīcaparivartās
ABc gurubhagaṇās rāśiguṇās aśvayujaādyās guror abdās
ABa ravibhagaṇās raviabdās raviśaśiyogās bhavanti śaśimāsās
ABc ravibhūyogās divasās bhaāvartās ca api nākṣatrās
ABa adhimāsakās yuge te ravimāsebhyas adhikās tu ye cāndrās
ABc śaśidivasās vijñeyās bhūdivasaūnās tithipralayās
ABa ravivarṣam mānuṣyam tat api triṃśatguṇam bhavati pitryam
ABc pitryam dvādaśaguṇitam divyam varṣam vinirdiṣṭam
ABa divyam varṣasahasram grahasāmānyam yugam dviṣaṭkaguṇam
ABc aṣṭauttaram sahasram brāhmas divasas grahayugānām
ABa utsarpiṇī yugaardham paścāt apasarpiṇī yugaardham ca
ABc madhye yugasya suṣamā ādau ante duṣṣamā induuccāt
ABa ṣaṣṭiabdānām ṣaṣṭis yadā vyatītās trayas ca yugapādās
ABc triadhikā viṃśatis abdās tadā iha mama janmanas atītās
ABa yugavarṣamāsadivasās samam pravṛttās tu caitraśuklaādes
ABc kālas ayam anādiantas grahabhais anumīyate kṣetre
ABa ṣaṣṭyā sūryaabdānām prapūrayanti grahās bhapariṇāham
ABc divyena nabhasparidhim samam bhramantas svakakṣyāsu
ABa maṇḍalam alpam adhastāt kālena alpena pūrayati candras
ABc upariṣṭāt sarveṣām mahat ca mahatā śanaiścārī
ABa alpe hi maṇḍale alpā mahati mahāntas ca rāśayas jñeyās
ABc aṃśās kalās tathā evam vibhāgatulyās svakakṣyāsu
ABa bhānām adhas śanaiścarasuragurubhaumaarkaśukrabudhacandrās
ABc eṣām adhas ca bhūmis medhībhūtā khamadhyasthā
ABa sapta ete horāīśās śanaiścaraādyās yathākramam śīghrās
ABc śīghrakramāt caturthās bhavanti sūryaudayāt dinapās
ABa kakṣyāpratimaṇḍalagās bhramanti sarve grahās svacāreṇa
ABc mandauccāt anulomam pratilomam ca eva śīghrauccāt
ABa kakṣyāmaṇḍalatulyam svam svam pratimaṇḍalam bhavati eṣām
ABc pratimaṇḍalasya madhyam ghanabhūmadhyāt atikrāntam
ABa pratimaṇḍalabhūvivaram vyāsaardham svauccanīcavṛttasya
ABc vṛttaparidhau grahās te madhyamacārāt bhramanti evam
ABa yas śīghragatis svauccāt pratilomagatis svavṛttakakṣyāyām
ABc anulomagatis vṛtte mandagatis yas grahas bhavati
ABa anulomagāni mandāt śīghrāt pratilomagāni vṛttāni
ABc kakṣyāmaṇḍalalagnasvavṛttamadhye grahas madhyas
ABa kṣayadhanadhanakṣayās syur mandauccāt vyatyayena śīghrauccāt
ABc śanigurukujeṣu mandāt ardham ṛṇam dhanam bhavati pūrve
ABa mandauccāt śīghrauccāt ardham ṛṇam dhanam graheṣu mandeṣu
ABc mandauccāt sphuṭamadhyās śīghrauccāt ca sphuṭās jñeyās
ABa śīghrauccāt ardhaūnam kartavyam ṛṇam dhanam svamandaucce
ABc sphuṭamadhyau tu bhṛgubudhau siddhāt mandāt sphuṭau bhavatas
ABa bhūtārāgrahavivaram vyāsaardhahṛtas svakarṇasaṃvargas
ABc kakṣyāyām grahavegas yas bhavati sas mandanīcaucce
golapāda
AB a meṣaādes kanyāantam samam udacapamaṇḍalaardham apayātam
ABc taulyaādes mīnaantam śeṣaardham dakṣiṇena eva
ABa tārāgrahaindupātās bhramanti ajasram apamaṇḍale arkas ca
ABc arkāt ca maṇḍalaardhe bhramati hi tasmin kṣitichāyā
ABa apamaṇḍalasya candras pātāt yāti uttareṇa dakṣiṇatas
ABc kujagurukoṇās ca evam śīghrauccena api budhaśukrau
ABa candras aṃśais dvādaśabhis avikṣiptas arkaantarasthitas dṛśyas
ABc navabhis bhṛgus bhṛgos tais dviadhikais dviadhikais yathā ślakṣṇās
ABa bhūgrahabhānām golaardhāni svachāyayā vivarṇāni
ABc ardhāni yathāsāram sūryaabhimukhāni dīpyante
ABa vṛttabhapañjaramadhye kakṣyāpariveṣṭitas khamadhyagatas
ABc mṛdjalaśikhivāyumayas bhūgolas sarvatas vṛttas
ABa yadvat kadambapuṣpagranthis pracitas samantatas kusumais
ABc tadvat hi sarvasattvais jalajais sthalajais ca bhūgolas
ABa brahmadivasena bhūmes upariṣṭāt yojanam bhavati vṛddhis
ABc dinatulyayā ekarātryā mṛdupacitāyā bhavati hānis
ABa anulomagatis nausthas paśyati acalam vilomagam yadvat
ABc acalāni bhāni tadvat samapaścimagāni laṅkāyām
ABa udayaastamayanimittam nityam pravaheṇa vāyunā kṣiptas
ABc laṅkāsamapaścimagas bhapañjaras sagrahas bhramati
ABa merus yojanamātras prabhākaras himavatā parikṣiptas
ABc nandanavanasya madhye ratnamayas sarvatas vṛttas
ABa svarmerū sthalamadhye narakas baḍavāmukham ca jalamadhye
ABc amaramarās manyante parasparam adhassthitās niyatam
ABa udayas yas laṅkāyām sas astamayas savitur eva siddhapure
ABc madhyaahnas yamakoṭyām romakaviṣaye ardharātras syāt
ABa sthalajalamadhyāt laṅkā bhūkakṣyāyās bhavet caturbhāge
ABc ujjayinī laṅkāyās tadcaturaṃśe samauttaratas
ABa bhūvyāsaardhena ūnam dṛśyam deśāt samāt bhagolaardham
ABc ardham bhūmichannam bhūvyāsaardhaadhikam ca eva
ABa devās paśyanti bhagolaardham udacmerusaṃsthitās savyam
ABc ardham tu apasavyagatam dakṣiṇabaḍavāmukhe pretās
ABa ravivarṣaardham devās paśyanti uditam ravim tathā pretās
ABc śaśimāsaardham pitaras śaśigās kudinaardham iha manujās
ABa pūrvaaparam adhasūrdhvam maṇḍalam atha dakṣiṇauttaram ca eva
ABc kṣitijam samapārśvastham bhānām yatra udayaastamayau
ABa pūrvaaparadiślagnam kṣitijāt akṣaagrayos ca lagnam yat
ABc unmaṇḍalam bhavet tat kṣayavṛddhī yatra divasaniśos
ABa pūrvaaparadiśrekhā adhas ca ūrdhvā dakṣiṇauttarasthā ca
ABc etāsām sampātas draṣṭā yasmin bhavet deśe
ABa ūrdhvam adhastāt draṣṭur jñeyam dṛśmaṇḍalam grahaabhimukham
ABc dṛśkṣepamaṇḍalam api prāclagnam syāt trirāśiūnam
ABa kāṣṭhamayam samavṛttam samantatas samagurum laghum golam
ABc pāratatailajalais tam bhramayet svadhiyā ca kālasamam
ABa dṛśgolaardhakapāle jyāardhena vikalpayet bhagolaardham
ABc viṣuvatjīvāakṣabhujā tasyās tu avalambaks koṭis
ABa iṣṭaapakramavargam vyāsaardhakṛtes viśodhya yat mūlam
ABc viṣuvatudacdakṣiṇatas tat ahorātraardhaviṣkambhas
ABa iṣṭajyāguṇitam ahorātravyāsaardham eva kāṣṭhaantyam
ABc svaahorātraardhahṛtam phalam ajāt laṅkāudayaprācjyās
ABa iṣṭaapakramaguṇitām akṣajyām lambakena hṛtvā yā
ABc svaahorātre kṣitijā kṣayavṛddhijyā dinaniśos sā
ABa udayati hi cakrapādas caradalahīnena divasapādena
ABc prathamas antyas ca atha anyau tadsahitena kramautkramaśas
ABa svaahorātraiṣṭajyā kṣitijāt avalambakaāhatām kṛtvā
ABc viṣkambhaardhavibhakte dinasya gataśeṣsayos śaṅkus
ABa viṣuvatjīvāguṇitas svaiṣṭas śaṅkus svalambakena hṛtas
ABc astamayaudayasūtrāt dakṣiṇatas sūryaśaṅkuagram
ABa paramaapakramajīvām iṣṭajyāardhaāhatām tatas vibhajet
ABc jyā lambakena labdhā arkaagrā pūrvaapare kṣitije
ABa sā viṣuvatjyāūnā ced viṣuvatudaclambakena saṅguṇitā
ABc viṣuvatjyayā vibhaktā labdhas pūrvaapare śaṅkus
ABa kṣitijāt unnatabhāgānām yā jyā sā paras bhavet śaṅkus
ABc madhyāt natabhāgajyā chāyā śaṅkos tu tasya eva
ABa madhyajyāudayajīvāsaṃvarge vyāsadalahṛte yat syāt
ABc tadmadhyajyākṛtyos viśeṣamūlam svadṛśkṣepas
ABa dṛśdṛśkṣepakṛtiviśeṣitasya mūlam svadṛśgatis kuvaśāt
ABc kṣitije svā dṛśchāyā bhūvyāsaardham nabhasmadhyāt
ABa vikṣepaguṇaakṣajyā lambakabhaktā bhavet ṛṇam udacsthe
ABc udaye dhanam astamaye dakṣiṇage dhanam ṛṇam candre
ABa vikṣepaapakramaguṇam utkramaṇam vistaraardhakṛtibhaktam
ABc udacṛṇadhanam udacayane dakṣiṇage dhanam ṛṇam yāmye
ABa candras jalam arkas agnis mṛdbhūchāyā api yā tamas tat hi
ABc chādayati śaśī sūryam śaśinam mahatī ca bhūchāyā
ABa sphuṭaśaśimāsaante arkam pātaāsannas yadā praviśati indus
ABc bhūchāyām pakṣaante tadā adhikaūnam grahaṇamadhyam
ABa bhūravivivaram vibhajet bhūguṇitam tu ravibhūviśeṣeṇa
ABc bhūchāyādīrghatvam labdham bhūgolaviṣkambhāt
ABa chāyāagracandravivaram bhūviṣkambheṇa tat samabhyastam
ABc bhūchāyayā vibhaktam vidyāt tamasas svaviṣkambham
ABa tadśaśisamparkaardhakṛtes śaśivikṣepavargitam śodhyam
ABc sthitiardham asya mūlam jñeyam candraarkadinabhogāt
ABa candravyāsaardhaūnasya vargitam yat tamasmayaardhasya
ABc vikṣepakṛtivihīnam tasmāt mūlam vimardaardham
ABa tamasas viṣkambhaardham śaśiviṣkambhaardhavarjitam apohya
ABc vikṣepāt yat śeṣam na gṛhyate tat śaśāṅkasya
ABa vikṣepavargasahitāt sthitimadhyāt iṣṭavarjitāt mūlam
ABc samparkaardhāt śodhyam śeṣas tātkālikas grāsas
ABa madhyaahnautkramaguṇitas akṣas dakṣiṇatas ardhavistarahṛtas dik
ABc sthitiardhāt ca arkaindvos trirāśisahitaayanāt sparśe
ABa pragrahaṇaante dhūmras khaṇḍagrahaṇe śaśī bhavati kṛṣṇas
ABc sarvagrāse kapilas sakṛṣṇatāmras tamasmadhye
ABa sūryainduparidhiyoge arkaaṣṭamabhāgas bhavati anādeśyas
ABc bhānos bhāsvarabhāvāt suacchatanutvāt ca śaśiparidhes
ABa kṣitiraviyogāt dinakṛt raviinduyogāt prasādhayet ca indum
ABc śaśitārāgrahayogāt tathā eva tārāgrahās sarve
ABa satasatjñānasamudrāt samuddhṛtam brahmaṇas prasādena
ABc satjñānauttamaratnam mayā nimagnam svamatināvā
ABa āryabhaṭīyam nāmnā pūrvam svāyambhuavam sadā nityam
ABc sukṛtaāyuṣos praṇāśam kurute pratikañcukam yas asya
