Navaślokī
āryāṣṭasāhaśrikāyāḥ prajñāpāramitāyāḥ piṇḍārthaḥ
prajñāpāramitāmbodhau śubharatnākare svayam 
sarvā pāramitās tatra tādātmyena vyavasthitāḥ  Nś 
niṣprapañcā nirābhāsā nirvikalpā nirālayā 
niḥsvabhāvā parā sūkṣmā bindunādavivarjitā  Nś 
prajñāpāramitā mātā sarvabuddhodayā parā 
trayānupalabdhirūpā sarvajñajñānagocarā  Nś 
prajñāpāramitāṃ saṃyag yo bhāvayitum icchati 
tenārthato navaślokāś cintanīyāḥ samāsataḥ  Nś 
karmaprabhāvasaṃbhūtaṃ ṣaḍāyatanalakṣaṇam 
punarbhavam iti khyātaṃ pratibhāsopamaṃ hi tat  Nś 
nirmitaṃ nagaraṃ yadvad vilokayati nirmitaḥ 
tadvat paśyati rūpāṇi karmabhir nirmitaṃ jagat  Nś 
dharmaṃ deśayataḥ śabdā ye kecit śrutigocarāḥ 
pratiśrutkopamāḥ sarve prodbhūtaśrutinaḥ śrutāḥ  Nś 
āghrataṃ svāditaṃ tathā spraṣṭaṃ viṣayalālāsaiḥ 
svapnatulyam idaṃ sarvam upalabdhaṃ na vidyate  Nś 
māyāyantro naro yadvad bhinnāṃ ceṣṭāṃ karoti vai 
tadvad ceṣṭāṃ karoty eva dehayantro nirātmakaḥ  Nś 
nānopalabdhayo yāś ca pratikṣaṇasamudbhavāḥ 
marīcisadṛśāś caite dṛṣṭanaṣṭāḥ vilakṣaṇāḥ  Nś 
pratibimbanibhaṃ grāhyam anādicittasaṃbhavam 
tadākāraṃ ca vijñānam anyonyapratibimbavat  Nś 
dhyāyināṃ svacchasaṃtāne yaj jnanendusamudbhavam 
udakacandropamaṃ tad dhi pratyakṣaṃ na vidyate  Nś 
yoginām api yaj jñānaṃ tad apy ākāśalakṣaṇam 
tasmāj jñānaṃ ca jñeyaṃ ca sarvam ākāśalakṣaṇam  Nś 
iti cintayataḥ tattvaṃ sarvabhāveṣv anāśritam 
bodhipraṇidhicittena jñānaṃ agraṃ bhaviṣyati  Nś 
āryāṣṭasāhasrikāyāḥ prajñāpāramitāyāḥ piṇḍārthaḥ samāptaḥ
kṛtir iyaṃ śrīkambalāmbarapādanām
