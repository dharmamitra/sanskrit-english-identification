Veṇukātyāyanasagotrīsūtra
Wal pṛccha bhagini śrutvā te vedayiṣyāmi 
Wal sati śramaṇa eke śramaṇa brāhmaṇāḥ ye svayakṛta sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati  saty
eke parakṛta prajñapayati saty eke svayakṛta ca parakṛta ca  saty eke svayakāraaparakārahetusamutpanna
sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati  yathā katha samutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati 
Wal anyathāsamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati 
Wal yathā katha śramaṇa anyathāsamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati 
Wal pratītyasamutpanna bhagini arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati 
Wal yathā katha śramaṇa pratītyasamutpanna arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñpayati 
Wal tena hi bhagini tvām eva prakṣyāmi yathā te kṣamati tathaita vyākuru 
Wal ki manyase bhagini  asti cakṣuḥ  asti śramaṇa  asti rūpam  asti śramaṇa 
Wal cakṣuś ced bhagini utpadyeta api nu asti cakṣuḥsasparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayita sukha vā duḥkha vā aduḥkhāsukha vā  
 asti śramaṇa  
Wal asti śrotram asti ghāṇam asti jihvā asti kāyaḥ asti manaḥ 
asti śramaṇa  sati dharmāḥ 
Wal asti śrotra ghrāṇa jihvā kāyo manaś ced utpadyeta api nu śrotraghrāṇajihvākāyamanaḥsasparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayita  
sukha vā duḥkha vā aduḥkhāsukha vā  
asti śramaṇa 
Wal eva hi bhagini pratīyasamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati 
Wal eva hi śramaṇa pratītyasamutpannam arhantaḥ sukha prajñapya yathā katha śramaṇa arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhasya nirodha prajñapayati 
Wal tena hi bhagini tvām eva prakṣyāmi yathā te kṣamati tathaita vyākuru 
Wal ki manyase bhagini  cakṣuś ced bhagini sarveṇa sarva sarvathā apariśeṣa nirudhyeta
apariśeṣan adhyasta parikṣayam paryādāna gacched api nu cakṣuḥsasparśapratyayam
ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayita sukha vā duḥkha vā aduḥkhāsukha vā 
no śramaṇa 
Wal śrotra ghrāṇa jihvā kāyo manaś ced bhagini sarveṇa sarva sarvathā apariśeṣa nirudhyeta
apariśeṣam adhyasta parikṣaya paryādāna gacchet api nu manaḥsasparśapratyayam
ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayita sukha vā duḥkha vā aduḥkhāsukha vā 
no śramaṇa 
Wal eva hi bhagini ārhantaḥ pratītyasamutpannasya sukhaduḥkhasya nirodha prajñapayati 
Wal asmi khalu dharmaparyāye bhāṣyamāne veṇukātyāyanasagotryā virajo vigatamala dharmeṣu dharmacakṣur utpannam 
Wal atha veṇukātyāyanasagotrī dṛṣṭadharmā prāptadharmā viditadharmā paryavagāḍhadharmā
tīrṇakākṣā tīrṇavicikitsā aparapratyayā ananyaneyā śāstuḥ śāsane dharmeṣu vaiśaradyaprāptā
utthāyāsanād ekāsam uttarāsaga kṛtvā yenāyuṣmān udāyī tenājali praṇamya āyuṣmantam
udāyinam idam avocat 
Wal abhikrāntāha śramaṇa abhikrāntā eṣāha bhagavanta śaraṇa gacchāmi dharma ca bhikṣusagha
ca  upāsikā ca mān dhāraya śramaṇa adyāgreṇa yāvajjīva prāṇopeta śaraṇagatām abhiprasannām 
Wal atha udāyī veṇukātyāyanasagotrī brāhmaṇī dhārmyā kathayāsadarśayitvā samādapayitvā samuttejayitvā sapraharṣayitvā utthāyāsanāt prakrāntaḥ
