VeṇukātyāyanasagotrīsūtraWal pṛccha bhagini śrutvā te vedayiṣyāmi Wal sati śramaṇa eke śramaṇa brāhmaṇāḥ ye svayakṛta sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati  satyeke parakṛta prajñapayati saty eke svayakṛta ca parakṛta ca  saty eke svayakāraaparakārahetusamutpannasukhaduḥkha prajñapayati  yathā katha samutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati Wal anyathāsamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati Wal yathā katha śramaṇa anyathāsamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati Wal pratītyasamutpanna bhagini arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati Wal yathā katha śramaṇa pratītyasamutpanna arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñpayati Wal tena hi bhagini tvām eva prakṣyāmi yathā te kṣamati tathaita vyākuru Wal ki manyase bhagini  asti cakṣuḥ  asti śramaṇa  asti rūpam  asti śramaṇa Wal cakṣuś ced bhagini utpadyeta api nu asti cakṣuḥsasparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayita sukha vā duḥkha vā aduḥkhāsukha vā   asti śramaṇa  Wal asti śrotram asti ghāṇam asti jihvā asti kāyaḥ asti manaḥ asti śramaṇa  sati dharmāḥ Wal asti śrotra ghrāṇa jihvā kāyo manaś ced utpadyeta api nu śrotraghrāṇajihvākāyamanaḥsasparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayita  sukha vā duḥkha vā aduḥkhāsukha vā  asti śramaṇa Wal eva hi bhagini pratīyasamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkha prajñapayati Wal eva hi śramaṇa pratītyasamutpannam arhantaḥ sukha prajñapya yathā katha śramaṇa arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhasya nirodha prajñapayati Wal tena hi bhagini tvām eva prakṣyāmi yathā te kṣamati tathaita vyākuru Wal ki manyase bhagini  cakṣuś ced bhagini sarveṇa sarva sarvathā apariśeṣa nirudhyetaapariśeṣan adhyasta parikṣayam paryādāna gacched api nu cakṣuḥsasparśapratyayamādhyātmam utpadyate vedayita sukha vā duḥkha vā aduḥkhāsukha vā no śramaṇa Wal śrotra ghrāṇa jihvā kāyo manaś ced bhagini sarveṇa sarva sarvathā apariśeṣa nirudhyetaapariśeṣam adhyasta parikṣaya paryādāna gacchet api nu manaḥsasparśapratyayamādhyātmam utpadyate vedayita sukha vā duḥkha vā aduḥkhāsukha vā no śramaṇa Wal eva hi bhagini ārhantaḥ pratītyasamutpannasya sukhaduḥkhasya nirodha prajñapayati Wal asmi khalu dharmaparyāye bhāṣyamāne veṇukātyāyanasagotryā virajo vigatamala dharmeṣu dharmacakṣur utpannam Wal atha veṇukātyāyanasagotrī dṛṣṭadharmā prāptadharmā viditadharmā paryavagāḍhadharmātīrṇakākṣā tīrṇavicikitsā aparapratyayā ananyaneyā śāstuḥ śāsane dharmeṣu vaiśaradyaprāptāutthāyāsanād ekāsam uttarāsaga kṛtvā yenāyuṣmān udāyī tenājali praṇamya āyuṣmantamudāyinam idam avocat Wal abhikrāntāha śramaṇa abhikrāntā eṣāha bhagavanta śaraṇa gacchāmi dharma ca bhikṣusaghaca  upāsikā ca mān dhāraya śramaṇa adyāgreṇa yāvajjīva prāṇopeta śaraṇagatām abhiprasannām Wal atha udāyī veṇukātyāyanasagotrī brāhmaṇī dhārmyā kathayāsadarśayitvā samādapayitvā samuttejayitvā sapraharṣayitvā utthāyāsanāt prakrāntaḥ
