Nāgārjuna Bhavasaṅkrāntiparikathānamaḥ kumārabhūtāya mañjuśriye bhāvānna jāyate bhāvo nābhāvādapi jāyate bhāva utpaddyate nityaṃ bhābo bhrāntiḥ khapuṣpavat   sati dharme nabhastulye khatulyaṃ jāyate param pratītya sarva khasamaṃ bhāvastasmādabhāvavān   sbabhāvataḥ karma nāsti heturnāsti phalaṃ na ca na viddyata idaṃ sarbaṃ loko nāsti na bhañjanam   anutpannaśca yo bhāvaḥ paraṃ sajanayetkatham lokaḥ prathamato jātaḥ kenāpi na hi nirmitaḥ somasiṃhapurītulyo loko bhramyatyanarthake   loko vikalpadutpanno vikalpaścittasaṃbhavaḥ cittaṃ hi kāyāśrayakaṃ tasmātkāyo vicāryate   rūpaṃ śūnyaṃ vedanā niḥsvabhāvā saṃjñā nāste nāsti saṃskārabhāvaḥ bhūtaṃ hitvā cittacaitte ca nastastasmātkāyaḥ kalpahīnasvabhāvaḥ   citta nāsti na dharmāste na kāyo nāpi dhātavaḥ advayīkaraṇaṃ hīdaṃ tattvaṃ vidvadbhirucyate   anālambamidaṃ sarvamanālambaṃ prabhāṣitam kṛtvā matimanālambāmanālambaṃ samuddhitaṃ   dānaśīlakṣamāvīryadhyānādau suniṣevite acireṇaiva kālena paramāṃ bodhimāpsyati   upāyaprajñayosthitvā sattvāṃśca karuṇāpayet sarvajñānaṃ śighrameva lapsyate nahi saṃśayaḥ   nāmamātramidaṃ sarvaṃ saṃjñāmātre pratiṣṭhitam nābhidhānātpṛthagbhūtamabhidheyaṃ na vidyate   anāmakāḥ sarvadharmā nirātmānaḥ prakīrtitāḥ ime dharmā abhūtāśca kalpanāyāḥ samuddhitāḥ kalpanā sāpi śūnyeyaṃ yayā śūnyeti kalpitāḥ   cakṣu paśyati rūpāṇi tattvavaktrā yaducyate mithyābhimānalokasya sāṃvṛtaṃ satyamīritam   sāmagryā darśanaṃ yatra prakāśayati nāyakaḥ prāhopacārabhūmiṃ tāṃ paramārthasya buddhimān   na cakṣuḥ prekṣate rūpaṃ mano dharmānnvetti ca etattu paraṃ satyaṃ lokasya viṣayo na yat   cakṣurnāsti na rūpañca dṛśyaṃ nāsti na manaskriyā cittaṃ svapnasamaṃ bhrāntiḥ sarvaṃ na sadasanna ca   ācārya āryanāgārjunakṛta bhavasaṅkrāntiparikathā sampūrṇā
