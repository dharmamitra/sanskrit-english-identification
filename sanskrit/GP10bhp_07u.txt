BhP vidharmaḥ paradharmaśca ābhāsa upamā chalaḥ
BhP adharmaśākhāḥ pañcemā dharmajño 'dharmavat tyajet
BhP dharmabādho vidharmaḥ syāt paradharmo 'nyacoditaḥ
BhP upadharmastu pākhaṇḍo dambho vā śabdabhic chalaḥ
BhP yastvicchayā kṛtaḥ pumbhirābhāso hyāśramāt pṛthak
BhP svabhāvavihito dharmaḥ kasya neṣṭaḥ praśāntaye
BhP dharmārthamapi neheta yātrārthaṃ vādhano dhanam
BhP anīhānīhamānasya mahāheriva vṛttidā
BhP santuṣṭasya nirīhasya svātmārāmasya yat sukham
BhP kutastat kāmalobhena dhāvato 'rthehayā diśaḥ
BhP sadā santuṣṭamanasaḥ sarvāḥ śivamayā diśaḥ
BhP śarkarākaṇṭakādibhyo yathopānatpadaḥ śivam
BhP santuṣṭaḥ kena vā rājan na vartetāpi vāriṇā
BhP aupasthyajaihvyakārpaṇyādgṛhapālāyate janaḥ
BhP asantuṣṭasya viprasya tejo vidyā tapo yaśaḥ
BhP sravantīndriyalaulyena jñānaṃ caivāvakīryate
BhP kāmasyāntaṃ hi kṣuttṛḍbhyāṃ krodhasyaitat phalodayāt
BhP jano yāti na lobhasya jitvā bhuktvā diśo bhuvaḥ
BhP paṇḍitā bahavo rājan bahujñāḥ saṃśayacchidaḥ
BhP sadasas patayo 'pyeke asantoṣāt patantyadhaḥ
BhP asaṅkalpāj jayet kāmaṃ krodhaṃ kāmavivarjanāt
BhP arthānarthekṣayā lobhaṃ bhayaṃ tattvāvamarśanāt
BhP ānvīkṣikyā śokamohau dambhaṃ mahadupāsayā
BhP yogāntarāyān maunena hiṃsāṃ kāmādyanīhayā
BhP kṛpayā bhūtajaṃ duḥkhaṃ daivaṃ jahyāt samādhinā
BhP ātmajaṃ yogavīryeṇa nidrāṃ sattvaniṣevayā
BhP rajastamaśca sattvena sattvaṃ copaśamena ca
BhP etat sarvaṃ gurau bhaktyā puruṣo hyañjasā jayet
BhP yasya sākṣādbhagavati jñānadīpaprade gurau
BhP martyāsaddhīḥ śrutaṃ tasya sarvaṃ kuñjaraśaucavat
BhP eṣa vai bhagavān sākṣāt pradhānapuruṣeśvaraḥ
BhP yogeśvarairvimṛgyāṅghrirloko yaṃ manyate naram
BhP ṣaḍvargasaṃyamaikāntāḥ sarvā niyamacodanāḥ
BhP tadantā yadi no yogān āvaheyuḥ śramāvahāḥ
BhP yathā vārtādayo hyarthā yogasyārthaṃ na bibhrati
BhP anarthāya bhaveyuḥ sma pūrtamiṣṭaṃ tathāsataḥ
BhP yaścittavijaye yattaḥ syān niḥsaṅgo 'parigrahaḥ
BhP eko viviktaśaraṇo bhikṣurbhaikṣyamitāśanaḥ
BhP deśe śucau same rājan saṃsthāpyāsanamātmanaḥ
BhP sthiraṃ sukhaṃ samaṃ tasminnāsītarjvaṅga omiti
BhP prāṇāpānau sannirundhyāt pūrakumbhakarecakaiḥ
BhP yāvan manastyajet kāmān svanāsāgranirīkṣaṇaḥ
BhP yato yato niḥsarati manaḥ kāmahataṃ bhramat
BhP tatastata upāhṛtya hṛdi rundhyāc chanairbudhaḥ
BhP evamabhyasyataścittaṃ kālenālpīyasā yateḥ
BhP aniśaṃ tasya nirvāṇaṃ yātyanindhanavahnivat
BhP kāmādibhiranāviddhaṃ praśāntākhilavṛtti yat
BhP cittaṃ brahmasukhaspṛṣṭaṃ naivottiṣṭheta karhicit
BhP yaḥ pravrajya gṛhāt pūrvaṃ trivargāvapanāt punaḥ
BhP yadi seveta tān bhikṣuḥ sa vai vāntāśyapatrapaḥ
BhP yaiḥ svadehaḥ smṛto 'nātmā martyo viṭkṛmibhasmavat
BhP ta enamātmasāt kṛtvā ślāghayanti hyasattamāḥ
BhP gṛhasthasya kriyātyāgo vratatyāgo vaṭorapi
BhP tapasvino grāmasevā bhikṣorindriyalolatā
BhP āśramāpasadā hyete khalvāśramaviḍambanāḥ
BhP devamāyāvimūḍhāṃstān upekṣetānukampayā
BhP ātmānaṃ cedvijānīyāt paraṃ jñānadhutāśayaḥ
BhP kimicchan kasya vā hetordehaṃ puṣṇāti lampaṭaḥ
BhP āhuḥ śarīraṃ rathamindriyāṇi hayān abhīṣūn mana indriyeśam
BhP vartmāni mātrā dhiṣaṇāṃ ca sūtaṃ sattvaṃ bṛhadbandhuramīśasṛṣṭam
BhP akṣaṃ daśaprāṇamadharmadharmau cakre 'bhimānaṃ rathinaṃ ca jīvam
BhP dhanurhi tasya praṇavaṃ paṭhanti śaraṃ tu jīvaṃ parameva lakṣyam
BhP rāgo dveṣaśca lobhaśca śokamohau bhayaṃ madaḥ
BhP māno 'vamāno 'sūyā ca māyā hiṃsā ca matsaraḥ
BhP rajaḥ pramādaḥ kṣunnidrā śatravastvevamādayaḥ
BhP rajastamaḥprakṛtayaḥ sattvaprakṛtayaḥ kvacit
BhP yāvan nṛkāyarathamātmavaśopakalpaṃ
BhP dhatte gariṣṭhacaraṇārcanayā niśātam
BhP jñānāsimacyutabalo dadhadastaśatruḥ
BhP svānandatuṣṭa upaśānta idaṃ vijahyāt
BhP nocet pramattamasadindriyavājisūtā
BhP nītvotpathaṃ viṣayadasyuṣu nikṣipanti
BhP te dasyavaḥ sahayasūtamamuṃ tamo 'ndhe
BhP saṃsārakūpa urumṛtyubhaye kṣipanti
BhP pravṛttaṃ ca nivṛttaṃ ca dvividhaṃ karma vaidikam
BhP āvartate pravṛttena nivṛttenāśnute 'mṛtam
BhP hiṃsraṃ dravyamayaṃ kāmyamagnihotrādyaśāntidam
BhP darśaśca pūrṇamāsaśca cāturmāsyaṃ paśuḥ sutaḥ
BhP etadiṣṭaṃ pravṛttākhyaṃ hutaṃ prahutameva ca
BhP pūrtaṃ surālayārāma kūpājīvyādilakṣaṇam
BhP dravyasūkṣmavipākaśca dhūmo rātrirapakṣayaḥ
BhP ayanaṃ dakṣiṇaṃ somo darśa oṣadhivīrudhaḥ
BhP annaṃ reta iti kṣmeśa pitṛyānaṃ punarbhavaḥ
BhP ekaikaśyenānupūrvaṃ bhūtvā bhūtveha jāyate
BhP niṣekādiśmaśānāntaiḥ saṃskāraiḥ saṃskṛto dvijaḥ
BhP indriyeṣu kriyāyajñān jñānadīpeṣu juhvati
BhP indriyāṇi manasyūrmau vāci vaikārikaṃ manaḥ
BhP vācaṃ varṇasamāmnāye tamoṃkāre svare nyaset
BhP oṃkāraṃ bindau nāde taṃ taṃ tu prāṇe mahatyamum
BhP agniḥ sūryo divā prāhṇaḥ śuklo rākottaraṃ svarāṭ
BhP viśvo 'tha taijasaḥ prājñasturya ātmā samanvayāt
BhP devayānamidaṃ prāhurbhūtvā bhūtvānupūrvaśaḥ
BhP ātmayājyupaśāntātmā hyātmastho na nivartate
BhP ya ete pitṛdevānāmayane vedanirmite
BhP śāstreṇa cakṣuṣā veda janastho 'pi na muhyati
BhP ādāvante janānāṃ sadbahirantaḥ parāvaram
BhP jñānaṃ jñeyaṃ vaco vācyaṃ tamo jyotistvayaṃ svayam
BhP ābādhito 'pi hyābhāso yathā vastutayā smṛtaḥ
BhP durghaṭatvādaindriyakaṃ tadvadarthavikalpitam
BhP kṣityādīnāmihārthānāṃ chāyā na katamāpi hi
BhP na saṅghāto vikāro 'pi na pṛthaṅ nānvito mṛṣā
BhP dhātavo 'vayavitvāc ca tanmātrāvayavairvinā
BhP na syurhyasatyavayavinyasannavayavo 'ntataḥ
BhP syāt sādṛśyabhramastāvadvikalpe sati vastunaḥ

BhP bhāvādvaitaṃ kriyādvaitaṃ dravyādvaitaṃ tathātmanaḥ

BhP kāryakāraṇavastvaikya darśanaṃ paṭatantuvat

BhP sa teneheta kāryāṇi naro nānyairanāpadi
