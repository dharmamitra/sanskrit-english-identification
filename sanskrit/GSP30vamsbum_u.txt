Vāmanadatta
Svabodhodayamañjarī
samyagbodhavicāreṇa bhāvanām asvabhāvataḥ 
labdhabodhadayānandaṃ vande saṃsthānam ātmanaḥ   
rūpādipañcavargo 'yaṃ viśvam etāvad eva hi 
gṛhyate pañcabhis tac ca cakṣurādibhir indriyaiḥ   
cakṣuḥ sarvāsv avasthāsu dehināṃ manasi sthitam 
tat pralīnaṃ bhaved yasya tasya sarvaṃ pralīyate   
tasyaiva vilayopāyaḥ pradiṣṭo gurubhiḥ purā 
tadāgamaparibhraṃśabhayāt spaṣṭīkṛto mayā   
jātyādikalpanārūpavyavahāro manaḥsthitaḥ 
vastuto na tu tāḥ santi niścityaitac chamaṃ vrajet   
śukraśonitasaṃśleṣakāle nāsti manaḥ kvacit 
na budbude na peṣyāṃ vā na śarīre kvacit sthitam   
śarīraṃ garbhavāsāntaḥ kaumāre yauvane tathā 
kṣaṇakṣayi vināśāntaṃ tac ca naivopalabhyate   
pṛthag nāma pṛthag rūpaṃ lomādīnāṃ pṛthak sthitiḥ 
pṛthak sarvaṃ yathā dṛṣṭaṃ kva śarīraṃ tadā sthitam   
evam eva vicāryoktā jātis tathaiva bādhyate 
na ca sā vastuto yuktā kasyacid vāvabhāsate   
nāma pitrā kṛtaṃ mithyā kriyā na niyatā yataḥ 
guṇī yadi bhavet kaścid bhavanti bahavo guṇāḥ   
itthaṃ mithyāvikalpotthavāsanābhir abhiplutam 
cañcalaṃ manaso rūpaṃ niścityaitan nirodhayet   
pūrvair nirodho kathito vairāgyābhyāsaypogataḥ 
ayatnena nirodho 'yam asmābhir upadiśyate   
grāhyaṃ yac ca bhavet kiñcit tat tal līnaṃ tadā bhavet 
anyasyāgrahaṇāc cittaṃ svātmany eva praśāmyati   
yathā ghanasvane sāndre krameṇa vilayaṃ gate 
tadāśrayavaśāc cittaṃ tasmin kṣīṇe praśāmyati   
yad yan manoharaṃ kiṃcic chrutigocaram āgatam 
ekāgraṃ bhāvayet tāvad yāval līnaṃ nirodhakṛt   
rūpādīnāṃ tathaivetthaṃ bhāvayed ramaṇīyatām 
vilīnāni smaret paścād ātamabhāvopabṛṃhitaḥ   
evaṃ grāhyasamāveśān nirodhaḥ kathito mayā 
grahaṇād eva pūrvo 'yam idānīṃ sampradṛśyate   
grahaṇānīndriyāṇīha samānīti prabodhayet 
samatvaṃ rāgahāneḥ syād dveṣasyopakṣayāt tathā   
sarvarāgāt sahāniḥ syāt sarvadveṣāt tathaiva ca 
baddhavat sarvarāgī syāt sarvadveṣṭā ca bhairavaḥ   
agrāhyam indriyaṃ śūnyaṃ svātmany eva pralīyate 
pralīnendriyavṛttes tu kaivalyābhyudayodayaḥ   
tasmāc cittaṃ samādāya śūnye saṅkalpavarjite 
nistabdhendriyavṛttes tu nirodhaḥ samprajāyate   
jṛmbhamānasya satataṃ kṣudhāviṣṭasya yoginaḥ 
dhyāyato vā kim apy antaḥ pratyante kevalaṃ bhavet   
apralīnamanovṛtter nidrām āśrayataḥ śanaiḥ 
viṣayāgrahaṇāt sarvanirodhaḥ samprajāyate   
dhāvataḥ padavikṣepaprayatnānavadhāraṇāt 
niḥsaṃkalpamanovṛtteḥ paramātmā prakāśate   
āsane 'py upaviṣṭasya kva me cittam avasthitam 
vicāryaivaṃ prayatnena nirādhāre praśāmyati   
vayunā spandanādīni śarīrasya karomy aham 
na ca citte sthito vāyur na vāyau cittam āsthitam   
evaṃ vimṛśato bhāvān na kvacit saṃsthitaṃ manaḥ 
mithaiva vṛttayas tasya sarvathā kevalaṃ sthitam   
yatra yatra bhaved vāñchā bhojanādiṣu vastuṣu 
pūrayet tām yathāśakti bhavet pūrṇo nirāśrayaḥ   
vāñchitaṃ gaditaṃ kiñcid akasmād yadi vismṛtam 
punas tasyānusandhānāt kṣaṇāt kaivalyam āpnuyāt   
sthānuḥ syāt puruṣo veti dūrād dṛśye vikalpite 
suniścitamateḥ kṣipraṃ nirodhaḥ samprajāyate   
dṛśyaiḥ padārthair draṣṭāraṃ paśyed buddhyā yato dṛśaḥ 
taṃ dṛṣṭvā mokṣam āpnoti yo na tadvat sa badhyate   
ālambya saṃvidaṃ yatnāt saṃvedyaṃ na svabhāvataḥ 
tasmāt saṃviditaṃ sarvam iti saṃvinmayo bhavet   
punar viśed apānena hṛdayaṃ pravikāsayet 
tathaiva kṣīṇavṛttiḥ syād apāne vilayaṃ gate   
suṣumṇāpatham āśritya prāsādadhvanibodhatah 
binduṣaṭkaparityāgāc chāntabodhaḥ kṣaṇād bhavet   
vāmadakṣiṇasañcārabindudvayanigharṣaṇāt 
dvādaśānte mahāśāntiḥ siddhair uktā mukhāgame   
ūrdhvam ākramato vāyor udgatānte śamo bhavet 
puryaṣṭakavibhede 'pi tathaiva manaso layaḥ   
vivāryāsyaṃ kṣaṇam sthitvā niḥsandigdham anākulam 
stambhitaprāṇavṛttes tu nirodhaḥ samprajāyate   
nābhimedhrāntare cittaṃ suratānte vinikṣipet 
līyamāne ratānande nistaraṅgaḥ kṣaṇaṃ bhavet   
dūrāgatasuhṛdbandhupariṣvaṅganiṣevitam 
ānandanirbharaṃ cittaṃ nivṛttiṃ labhate kṣaṇāt   
dūrād uccarite śabde śabdārthānavadhāraṇāt 
sāvadhānasya tajjñānaṃ kṣīṇarodhaḥ prajāyate    
pādābhyaṅgapariṣvaṅgāc cittarodhaḥ kṣaṇaṃ bhavet   
rucyānāṃ ṣāḍavādīnāṃ svalolāgre sthitiṃ kuru 
kṣiyamāṇe rasānande kaivalyam upajāyate   
mālatyādiṣu gandhāṃś ca tathaiva paribhāvayet 
tadāśrayavaśāc cittaṃ teṣu līneṣu līyate   
itthaṃ pratikṣaṇaṃ yasya cittam ātmani līyate 
sa labdhabodhasadbhāvo jīvanmukto 'bhidhīyate   
mīmāṃsāvanasiṃhasya harṣadattasya sūnunā 
kṛtā vāmanadattena svabodhodayamañjarī
