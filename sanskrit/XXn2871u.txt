 Bhagavatstutiḥ praṇidhāya manaḥ sahendriyairvidhivad vāk ca śarīrameva ca  guṇabhūta mahāguṇodadhestava vakṣyāmi guṇaikadeśatām   paramapravarosmi vādināmanavadyaḥ susamāhitendriyaḥ  paramārthavidaprakampitaḥ prayataiḥ sarvaparapravādibhiḥ   caraṇa susamāptameva te susamāptavrata sādhitavrataḥ  balavāśca samādhiravyayastava nārāyaṇaśailarājavat   puruṣaṣabha nāsti te vyathā na viṣādo na bhaya na ca klamaḥ  na ca te vyasana kutaḥ kalirna ca bhūteṣu kadācidakṣamā   na ca dhāvasi nātilīyase na ca santapyasi nāpi hṛṣyase  satata śubhameva te manaḥ satata merurivācalādhipaḥ   munipugava sarvadhātubhirvipula jñānamapāvṛta tava  aparāhatamakṣayavyaya vividheṣvāyataneṣu vartase   na ca testi mune katha kathā vimatirnāsti na saśayaḥ kvacit  svayameva na te parāpara vidita sarvamavedi vidyayā   priyadarśanasādhudarśanapriyasādhupriyapaṇḍitapriyaḥ samameva hi te priyāpriya satata prītikarastatheryase   madhurapratibhānavānasi smitavākyaḥ smṛtamān viśāradaḥ  vividānumata prabhāṣase triṣu lokeṣu ca te stṛta yaśaḥ   nṛsurāsurayakṣarākṣasā bahavastvāmiha lokapaṇḍitāḥ upagamya mune punaḥ punaḥ paripṛcchānti na cābhiṣūyase   svavacaḥ paritoṣitāstvayā naradevāḥ surayakṣarākṣasāḥ pratiyānti vinītasaśayāścaraṇau vandya ca te mahāmuni   sthitamāsitamāgata gata śayita maunamathābhyudīritam  atha cīvarapātradhāraṇa rucira gautama sarvameva te   avilambitamadruta sama svaramādhuryaguṇaiḥ samanvitam  vacana punaruktavarjita samaye vyāharase narottama   balavānasi lokaviśrutaḥ puruṣajñaḥ puruṣarṣabhaḥ prabhuḥ  na ca manyasi nāvamanyase sakala lokamima sadaivatam   na ca vismayase kadācana prakṛtistheṣu calācaleṣu ca  suhṛdeṣu  ca  durhṛdeṣu ca pratikūleṣvanulomavatsu ca   paridevyamadīnanisvana srutalālārpitasannināditam  paridāhavidāhasayuta bhayarogajvaraśokakarṣitam   prasamīkṣya jagat samākula vividhairduḥkhaśatairupadrutam cirarātramanāthamutsuka bhavatṛṣṇāprasṛta tamovṛtam   avatārya munermahākṛpā vividhā cātmagatā prabhāvatām  svayameva hi śākyapuṅgava vyathitānmocayitu tvayocyate   ativīrya mahāvināyakapravarāṇāmanuvādinā vara na ca te sadṛśaḥ kutodhikastriṣu lokeṣvapi naiva vidyate   pratighānunayā na santi te na ca te santi muni parisravāḥ  anurodhavirodhavigrahāḥ satata suvrata naiva naiva te   apahāya mune priyāpriye sukhaduḥkhe vi  ṣama sama tathā  arati ca rati viparyayannupaśāntaścarasīha sayatah   vyasane na ca nāma nirmanā na ca nāmonnamase praśasayā  ayaśaśca yaśaśca te sama samamākruṣṭamathāpi vanditam   ativākyamatho titikṣase paruṣa pāpajanairudīritam  samarāgragato viṣāṇavān bhṛśamuktāniva kuñjaraḥ śarān   suvacastvamṛṣi vacaḥ kṣamaḥ sudurukteṣvapi nābhiṣūyase  samameva ca varttase mune paribhāṣāsu śubhāsu vākṣu ca   satata ca varārha pūjyase naradevāsurayakṣarākṣasaiḥ ṛṣibhiśca sadā mahātmabhirna ca te vikriyate sthira manaḥ   pravarohamito na manyasetyavarohamito na manyase  sadṛśohamito na manyase trividhā mānavidhā na santi te   dhṛtimān samaloṣṭakāñcanaḥ samavaiḍuryakaṭhallaśarkaraḥ tṛṇakāṣthasama mahāmune carasīda hi sadevaka jagat   himabhāskararaśmisagamācchiśiroṣṇa pavana samudvahan  adhivāsayase nagendravanna ca te pravyathate sthira manaḥ   śayanāsanapānabhojana vividha cīvaramuttarādharam  adhivāsayase nagendravanna ca te pravyathate mune nirāsravam   na ha śocāsi nātha nāsti me na ca te santi mune parigrahāḥ  asitosi suvākyanirmamḥ parimukto vividhairupadravaiḥ   na ca lābhamavāpya hṛṣyase tadalābhācca laya na gacchasi  avamānamatho titikṣase na ca sammānamihabhinandasi   kṣatajopamamagracandana surabhūmandarasānusambhavam  asayo niśitāḥ śarāśca te na vikurvanti manaḥ kadācana   na kathā kathayasyanarthikā na ca yā durjanasevitā kathā  na  ca lābhakathā na  sāmiṣā na ca yā nānumatā mahātmabhiḥ   pravivekakathāḥ sukhāvahāḥ praśama pravadanti kevalam  kathayasyatidevatāḥ kathāḥ kathitā yā vinayanti kilviṣam   madhurāṇi ca saṅgatāni ca svabhinītāni ca sāravanti ca  vacanāni mune prabhāṣase jagadarthāya viniṣcitāni ca   abudhā viparītadarśanāścapalāḥ sāhasikāḥ priyanvitāḥ  piśunāḥ paruṣā śaṭhāśca ye bhagavastaiḥ saha saṅgata na te   aśaṭhā ṛjavaśca ye narāḥ śucayaḥ satyaratā jitendriyāḥ  satata ca samīkṣyakāriṇo bhagavastaiḥ saha saṅgata tava   dharmajña nayajña pudralajña tvā vande ṣaḍabhijña sarvadaiva  kṣetrajña mune parāparajña tvā vande śirasā nayānayajña   pṛthumapi samīkṣate guṇa  tava  parikathito hi māa guṇaikadeśaḥ  na tava guṇamahārṇavasya pāra jagati pumānadhigantumārya śaktaḥ    iti bhagavatstutiḥ
