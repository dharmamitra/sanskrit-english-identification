 buddhastotram
 om namo buddhāya
 namo'stu buddhāya viśuddhabodhaye
 viśuddhadharmapratibhāsabuddhaye 
 saddharmapuṇyopagatānubuddhaye
 bhavāgraśūnyāya viśuddhabuddhaye   
 aho aho buddhamanantatejasaṃ
 aho aho sāgaramerugulmam 
 aho aho buddhamanantagocaram
 audumbaraṃ puṣpamivātidurlabham   
 aho mahākāruṇikastathāgataḥ
 aho aho śākyakulasya ketuḥ 
 aho praṇamyaḥ sa narendrasūryo
 yenedṛśaṃ bhāṣitasūtramuttamam   
 anugrahārthaṃ sa hi sarvasattvān
 saddharmacaryāmupadiśya nūnam 
 śānteśvaraḥ śākyamunistathāgataḥ
 sattvottamaḥ śāntapuraṃ praviṣṭaḥ   
 gambhīraśāntaṃ virajāsamādhi
 padaṃ praviṣṭe jinabuddhagocare 
 śūnyāśca kāyāstvatha śrāvakāṇāṃ
 śūnyā vihārā dvipadottamānām   
 te sarvadharmā prakṛtiśca śūnyā
 sattvā hi śūnyā nahi jātu vidyate   
 nityaṃ ca nityaṃ ca jinaṃ smarāmi
 nityaṃ ca śocāmi jinasya darśanam 
 nityaṃ ca nityaṃ praṇidhiṃ karomi
 saṃbuddhapūjyasya ca darśanārtham   
 sthāpyeha nityaṃ dharaṇīṣu jānu
 śokābhitapto jinadarśanāya 
 odantakāruṇyavināyakastva
 matīva tṛṣṇā sugatasya darśane   
 satāṃ varo yadvijahāra nityaṃ
 dadāhi me darśanatoyaśītalam 
 sattvāḥ satṛṣṇāstava rūpadarśane
 prahlādayainān karuṇājalena   
 kāruṇyabhāvaṃ kuru nātha nāyaka
 dadāhi me darśanaṃ saumyarūpam 
 tvaṃ pāhi trātarjagadekadeśitā
 śūnyāśca kāyāstvatha śrāvakāṇām   
 ākāśatulyā gaganasvabhāvā
 māyāmarīcyodakacandrakāyāḥ 
 sarve ca sattvāḥ sukhitasvabhāvā
 bhavantu bho nāyaka tvatprasādāt   
 bodhisattvasamuccayānāma kuladevatāviracitaṃ
 buddhastotraṃ samāptam
