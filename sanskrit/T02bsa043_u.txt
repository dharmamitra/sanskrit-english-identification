Samādhisaṃbhāraparivartaḥ
lokanāthāya namaḥ
bodhicittaṃ dṛḍhaṃ kuryāt pūrva kāruṇyaśaktijam 
bhavabhogasukhe 'saktaḥ parigrahaparāṅmukhaḥ   
śraddhādidhanasampanno guruṃ buddhasamaṃ bhajet 
taddiṣṭasamayācāraṃ paripālitumudyataḥ   
kumbhaguhyābheṣekaṃ tu prasādāllabhate guroḥ 
kāyavāk cittasaṃśuddhaḥ siddhipātraṃ sa sādhakaḥ   
samādhyaṅgasamudbhūtasaṃbhārapāripūritaḥ 
śīghraṃ siddhyāptirevaṃ ca guhyamantranayasthitiḥ   
samādhyaṅgacyutau tasya vipakṣasthititastathā 
janmakoṭisahasraiśca samādhirna prasidhyati   
tatsamādhiprasidhyartha guhyavṛttasthayogavit 
tad bhinnaṃ cintanaṃ tyaktvā dṛḍhavīryeṇa codyamet   
samādheryāni cāṅgāni vipakṣastadviniścaye 
samādhyaṅgaṃ vipakṣaśca saṃkṣepeṇa samucyate   
śīlasampattiyuktatvaṃ bhogeṣu nirapekṣatā 
kṣāntirdṛḍhapratijñā ca janasaṃsargavarjanam   
kāyavāk cittakāryeṣu samprajanyasamanvayaḥ 
buddhakāyastutismṛtyā kāyānusmṛtisaṃyutiḥ   
yasmin kāle tu yatkārya dārḍhrya tasya hyanusmṛtau 
pañcāvaraṇahāniśca yuktakṛd bhaktamānatā   
samastalokadharmeṣu cittopekṣā sadā tathā 
saṃlekho 'ṅgāni cemāni vipakṣastadviparyayaḥ   
samādhisambhārakṛtairhi puṇyaiḥ samādhiṃ vajropamametu lokaḥ   a ba 
samādhisambhāraparivarto mahāpaṇḍitācāryadīpaṅkaraśrījñānapādaviracitaḥ samāptaḥ 
tenaiva bhāratīyopādhyāyena mahāsaṃśodhakalokacakṣuṣā bhikṣuśākyamatinā ca anūdya nirṇītaḥ
