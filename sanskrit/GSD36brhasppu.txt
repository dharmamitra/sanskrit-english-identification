śrī brhaspatismṛtiḥ
dharmapradhānāḥ puruṣāḥ pūrvam āsann ahiṃsakāḥ 
lobhadveṣābhibhūtānāṃ vyavahāraḥ pravartitaḥ  Brh 
prayacchec ced bhṛtiṃ svāmī bhṛtyānāṃ karma kurvatām 
na kurvanti ca bhṛtyāś cet tatra vādaḥ pravartate  Brh 
hiṃsāṃ vā kurute kaś cid deyaṃ vā na prayacchati 
dve hi sthāne vivādasya tayor bahutarā gatiḥ  Brh 
yato dravyaṃ vinikrīya ṛṇārthaṃ caiva gṛhyate 
tanmūlyam uttamarṇena vyavahāra iti smṛtaḥ  Brh 
Brh rājaguṇāḥ
guṇadharmān ato rājñaḥ kathayāmy anupūrvaśaḥ 
dhanikarṇikasaṃdigdhau pratibhūlekhyasākṣiṇaḥ  Brh 
vicārayati yaḥ samyak tasyotpattiṃ nibodhata 
somāgnyarkānilendrāṇāṃ vittāpattyor yamasya ca  Brh 
tejomātraṃ samuddhṛtya rājño mūrtir hi nirmitā 
tasya savāṇi bhūtāni carāṇi sthāvarāṇi ca  Brh 
bhayād bhogāya kalpante svadharmān na calanti ca 
nārājake kṛṣivaṇik kusīdaparipālanam  Brh 
tasmād varṇāśramāṇāṃ tu netāsau nirmitaḥ purā 
Brh vyavahārapadāni
dvipado vyavahāraḥ syāt dhanahiṃsāsamudbhavaḥ  Brh 
dvisaptako'rthamūlas tu hiṃsāmūlaś caturvidhaḥ 
pāruṣye dve vadhaś caiva parastrīsaṃgrahas tathā  Brh 
kusīdanidhideyād yaṃ saṃbhūyotthānam eva ca 
bhṛtyadānam aśuśrūṣā bhūvādo 'svāmivikriyaḥ  Brh 
krayavikrayānuśayaḥ samayātikramas tathā 
strīpuṃsayogaḥ steyaṃ ca dāyabhāgo'kṣadevanam  Brh 
etāny arthasamutthāni padāni tu caturdaśa 
punar evaṃ prabhinnāni kriyābhedād anekadahā  Brh 
pāruṣye dve sāhasaṃ ca parastrīsaṃgrahas tathā 
hiṃsodbhavapadāny evaṃ catvāry āha bṛhaspatiḥ  Brh 
hīnamadhyottamatvena prabhinnāni pṛthak pṛthak 
viśeṣa eṣāṃ nirdiṣṭaś caturṇām apy anukramāt  Brh 
padāny aṣṭādaśaitāni dharmaśāstroditāni tu 
mūlaṃ sarvavivādānāṃ ye vidus te parīkṣakāḥ  Brh 
pūrvapakṣaḥ smṛtaḥ pādo dvitīyas tūttaras tathā 
kriyāpādas tathā vācyaś caturtho nirṇayas tathā  Brh 
Brh dharmādicatuṣṭayabalābalam
dharmeṇa vyavahāreṇa cāritreṇa nṛpājñayā 
catuṣprakāro'bhihitaḥ saṃdigdhe 'rthe vinirṇayaḥ  Brh 
śāstraṃ kevalam āśritya kriyate yatra nirṇayaḥ 
vyavahāraḥ sa vijñeyo dharmas tenāpi vardhate  Brh 
deśasthityānumānena naigamānumatena ca 
kriyate nirṇayas tatra vyavahāras tu bādhyate  Brh 
vihāya caritācāraṃ yatra kuryāt punar nṛpaḥ 
nirṇayaṃ sā tu rājājñā caritaṃ bādhyate tayā  Brh 
dharmaśāstrānusāreṇa sāmātyaḥ sa purohitaḥ 
vyavahārān nṛpaḥ paśyet prajāsaṃrakṣaṇāya ca 
krodhalobhavihīnas tu satyavādī jitendriyaḥ  Brh 
Brh dharmādhikaraṇam
saptaprakṛtikaṃ yat tu vijigīṣor areś ca yat 
caturdaśakam evedaṃ maṇḍalaṃ paricakṣate  Brh 
catvāraḥ pṛthivīpālāḥ pṛthaṅmitraiḥ sahāṣṭakam 
amātyādibhir ete ca jagaty akṣarasaṃhitāḥ  Brh 
prātar utthāya nṛpatiḥ śaucaṃ kṛtvā vidhānataḥ 
gurūn jyotirvido vaidyān devān viprān purohitān  Brh 
yathārham etān saṃpūjya supuṣpābharaṇāmbaraiḥ 
abhinandya ca gurvādīn sumukhaḥ praviśet sabhām  Brh 
rājā kāryāṇi saṃpaśyet sadbhir eva tribhir vṛtaḥ 
sabhām eva praviśyāgryām āsīnaḥ sthita eva vā  Brh 
Brh durgalakṣaṇam
ātmadārārthalokānāṃ saṃcitānāṃ tu guptaye 
nṛpatiḥ kārayed durgaṃ prākāradvayasaṃyutam  Brh 
bhūpānām indhanarasair vetraśaṣpānnavāhanaiḥ 
yantrāyudhaiś ca vividhaiḥ snigdhaiḥ śūrair narair yutam  Brh 
vedavidyāvido viprān kṣatriyān agnihotriṇaḥ 
āhṛtya sthāpayet tatra teṣāṃ vṛttiṃ prakalpayet  Brh 
anācchedyāḥ karās tebhyaḥ pradeyā gṛhabhūmayaḥ 
muktā bhāvyāś ca nṛpater lekhayitvā svaśāsane  Brh 
nityaṃ naimittikaṃ kāmyaṃ śāntikaṃ pauṣṭikaṃ tathā 
paurāṇāṃ karma kuryus te saṃdigdhavinayaṃ tathā  Brh 
samā nimnonnatā vāpi yatra bhūmir yathāvidhā 
śālāṭṭaparikhādyāś ca kartavyāś ca tathāvidhāḥ  Brh 
samantāt tatra veśmāni kuryuḥ prakṛtayas tataḥ 
dvijavaiśyavaṇicchilpi kārukā rakṣakās tathā  Brh 
shalāvasthānaniṣkāśa bhramaśvabhracatuṣpathān 
samājavikrayasthāna govrajāṃś caiva kalpayet  Brh 
guṇavān iti yaḥ proktaḥ khyāpito janasaṃsadi 
kathaṃ tenaiva vaktreṇa nirguṇaḥ parikathyate  Brh 
tasmāt prabhutvaṃ vṛttiṃ ca nirdoṣasya na cālayet 
anavasthāprasaṅgaḥ syān naśyetopagrahas tathā  Brh 
samyaṅ niviṣṭadeśas tu kṛtadurgas tu śāstrataḥ 
kaṇṭakoddharaṇe nityam ātiṣṭhed balam uttamam  Brh 
tat prajāpālanaṃ proktaṃ trividhaṃ nyāyavedibhiḥ 
paracakrāc caurabhayād balino'nyāyavartinaḥ  Brh 
parānīkastenabhayam upāyaiḥ śamayen nṛpaḥ 
balavat paribhūtānāṃ pratyahaṃ nyāyadarśanaiḥ  Brh 
yad adhīte yad yajate yaj juhoti yad arcati 
tasya ṣaḍbhāgabhāg rājā samyag bhavati rakṣaṇāt  Brh 
rakṣan dharmeṇa bhūtāni rājā vadhyāṃś ca ghātayan 
yajate 'harahar yajñaiḥ sahasraśatadakṣiṇaiḥ  Brh 
daśāṣṭaṣaṣṭhaṃ nṛpater bhāgaṃ dadyāt kṛṣīvalam 
khilād varṣāvasantāc ca kṛṣyamāṇād yathākramam  Brh 
deśasthityā baliṃ dadyur bhūtaṃ ṣaṇmāsavārṣikam 
eṣa dharmaḥ samākhyātaḥ kīnāśānāṃ purātanaḥ  Brh 
Brh sabhāniveśanaprakāraḥ
audakaṃ pārvataṃ vārkṣyam airaṇaṃ dhānvanaṃ tathā 
durgamadhye gṛhaṃ kuryāj jalavṛkṣāvṛtaṃ pṛthak  Brh 
prāgdiśi prāṅmukhīṃ tasya lakṣaṇyāṃ kalpayet sabhām 
mālyadhūpāsanopetāṃ bījaratnasamanvitām  Brh 
pratimālekhyadevaiś ca yuktām agnyambunā tathā 
lakṣaṇyāṃ vāstuśāstrokta lakṣaṇena tu lakṣitām  Brh 
bhadrāsanam adhiṣṭhāya saṃvītāṅgaḥ samāhitaḥ 
praṇamya lokapālebhyaḥ kāryadarśanam ārabhet  Brh 
vipro dharmadrumasyādiḥ skandhaśākhe mahīpatiḥ 
sacivāḥ patrapuṣpāṇi phalaṃ nyāyena pālanam  Brh 
yaśo vittaṃ phalaraso bhogopagrahapūjanam 
ajeyatvaṃ lokapaṅktiḥ svarge sthānaṃ ca śāśvatam  Brh 
vodotvaitān nyāyarasān samo bhūtvā vivādanam 
tyaktalobhādikaṃ rājā dharmaṃ kuryād vinirṇayam  Brh 
rājā vṛttivivādānāṃ svayam eva pradarśanam 
śāstradṛṣṭena mārgeṇa sa vidvadbhiḥ prasevyate  Brh 
tasmān nyāyena rājā tu samyag yatnena pālayet 
tasmād arthaṃ ca rājyaṃ ca yaśaś ca vipulaṃ labhet  Brh 
satyaṃ devāḥ samāsena manuṣyās tv anṛtaṃ viduḥ 
ihaiva tasya devatvaṃ yasya satye sthitā matiḥ  Brh 
paśvājyyartvigādīnāṃ saṃyogāj jāyate 'dhvaraḥ 
yathā saṃbadhyate tena vyavahāras tathocyate  Brh 
prāḍvivākasadasyānām upajīvya matāni tu 
tadyuktiyogād yo 'rtheṣu nirṇaye na sa daṇḍabhāk  Brh 
Brh sabhāprabhedāḥ
pratiṣṭhitāpratiṣṭhā ca mudritā śāsitā tathā 
caturvidhā sabhā proktā sabhyāś caiva tathāvidhāḥ  Brh 
pratiṣṭhitā pure grāme calā nāmāpratiṣṭhitā 
mudritā adhyakṣasaṃyuktā rājayuktā ca śāsitā  Brh 
nyāyān paśyet kṛtamatiḥ sā sabhādhvarasaṃmitā 
Brh sabhyāḥ
lokavedāṅgadharmajñāḥ sapta pañca trayo 'pi vā 
yatropaviṣṭā viprāgryāḥ sā yajñasadṛśī sabhā  Brh 
kuryād alagnakau rakṣed arthiprathyarthinau sadā 
etad daśāṅgaṃ karaṇaṃ yasyām adhyāsta pārthivaḥ  Brh 
dvisasyāṣṭamaṃ bhāgaṃ muktvā kālaṃ susaṃviśet 
sa kālo vyavahārāṇāṃ śāstradṛṣṭaḥ paraḥ smṛtaḥ  Brh 
sādhukarmakriyāyuktāḥ satyadharmaparāyaṇāḥ 
akrodhalobhāḥ śāstrajñāḥ sabhyāḥ kāryā mahībhujā  Brh 
sapta pañca trayo vā sabhāsado bhavanti  Brh 
deśācārānabhijñā ye nāstikāḥ śāstravarjitāḥ 
unmattakruddhalubdhārtā na praṣṭavyā vinirṇaye  Brh 
rājā kāryāṇi saṃpaśyet prāḍvivāko 'tha vā dvijaḥ 
nyāyāṅgāny agrataḥ kṛtvā sabhyaśāstramate sthitaḥ  Brh 
balena caturaṅgena yato rañjayate prajāḥ 
dīpyamānaḥ svavapuṣā tena rājābhidhīyate  Brh 
ekas tv anekadhā prokto vyavahāro manīṣibhiḥ 
tasya nirṇayakṛd rājā brāhmaṇaś ca bahuśrutaḥ  Brh 
vyavahārāśritaṃ praśnaṃ pṛcchati prāḍ iti śrutiḥ 
vivadet tatra yas tasmin prāḍvivākas tu sa smṛtaḥ  Brh 
vivāde pṛcchati praśnaṃ pratipraśnaṃ tathaiva ca 
priyapūrvaṃ prāg vadati prāḍvivākas tataḥ smṛtaḥ  Brh 
saprāḍvivākaḥ sāmātyaḥ sabrāhmaṇapurohitaḥ 
sasabhyaḥ prekṣako rājā svarge tiṣṭhati dharmataḥ  Brh 
sarvaśāstrārthavettāram alubdhaṃ nyāyabhāṣiṇam 
vipraṃ prājñaṃ kramāyātam amātyaṃ sthāpayed dvijam  Brh 
dvijān vihāya yaḥ paśyet kāryāṇi vṛṣalaiḥ saha 
tasya prakṣarate rāṣṭraṃ balaṃ kośaṃ ca naśyati  Brh 
ye cāraṇyacarās teṣām araṇye karaṇaṃ bhavet 
senāyāṃ sainikānāṃ tu sārtheṣu baṇijāṃ tathā  Brh 
kīnāśāḥ kārukā mallāḥ kusīdaśreṇivartakāḥ 
liṅginas taskarāś caiva svena dharmeṇa nirṇayaḥ  Brh 
kulāni śreṇayaś caiva gaṇās tv adhikṛto nṛpaḥ 
pratiṣṭhā vyavahārāṇāṃ gurvebhyas tūttarottaram  Brh 
tapasvināṃ tu kāryāṇi traividyair eva kārayet 
māyāyogavidāṃ caiva na svayaṃ kopakāraṇāt  Brh 
adaṇḍyān daṇḍayan rājā daṇḍyāṃś caivāpy adaṇḍayan 
ayaśo mahad āpnoti narakaṃ caiva gacchati  Brh 
api bhrātā suto 'rghyo vā śvaśuro māturo 'pi vā 
nādaṇḍyo nāma rājño 'sti dharmād vicalitaḥ svakāt  Brh 
yatra vipro na vidvān syāt kṣatriyaṃ tatra yojayet 
vaiśyaṃ vā dharmaśāstrajñaṃ śūdraṃ yatnena varjayet  Brh 
dharmakarmavihīnas tu brāhmair liṅgair vivarjitaḥ 
bravīti brāhmaṇo 'smīti tam āhur brāhmaṇabruvam  Brh 
śabdābhidhānatattvajñau gaṇanākuśalau śucī 
nānālipijñau kartavyau rājñā gaṇakalekhakau  Brh 
akāraṇe rakṣaṇe ca sākṣyarthipravādinām 
sabhyādhīnaḥ satyavādī kartavyas tu sa pūruṣaḥ  Brh 
etad daśāṅgakaraṇaṃ yasyām adhyāsya pārthivaḥ 
nyhāyaṃ paśyet kṛtamatiḥ sā sabhādhvarasaṃmitā  Brh 
eṣāṃ mūrdhā nṛpo 'ṅgānāṃ mukhaṃ cādhikṛtaḥ smṛtaḥ 
bāhū sabhyāḥ smṛtir hastau jaṅghe gaṇakalekhakau  Brh 
hemāgnyambudṛśau hṛc ca pādau svapuruṣas tathā  Brh 
hiraṇyam agnim udakaṃ dharmaśāstrāṇi caiva hi 
tanmadhye sthāpayed rājā puṇyāni ca hitāni ca  Brh 
ādityacandradevādi dikpālān tatra kalpayet 
hemāgnyambusvapuruṣāḥ sādhanāṅgāni vai daśa  Brh 
daśānām api caiteṣāṃ karma proktaṃ pṛthak pṛthak 
vaktādhyakṣo nṛpaḥ śāstā sabhyaḥ kāryaparīkṣakaḥ  Brh 
smṛtir vinirṇayaṃ brūte jayadānaṃ damaṃ tathā 
śapathārthe hiraṇyāgnī ambu tṛṣitajantuṣu  Brh 
gaṇako gaṇayed arthaṃ likhen nyāyaṃ ca lekhakaḥ 
partyarthisabhyānayanaṃ sākṣiṇam ca svapūruṣaḥ  Brh 
vāgdaṇḍaś caiva dhigdaṇḍo viprādhīnau tu tāūbhau 
arthadaṇḍavadhāv uktau rājāyatāv ubhāv api  Brh 
rājñā ye viditāḥ samyak kulaśreṇigaṇādayaḥ 
sāhasanyāyavarjyāni kuryuḥ kāryāṇi te nṛṇām  Brh 
kulaśreṇigaṇādhyakṣāḥ proktā nirṇayakārakāḥ 
vicārya śreṇibhiḥ kāryaṃ kulair yan na vicāritam  Brh 
gaṇaiś ca śreṇy avijñātaṃ gaṇājñātaṃ niyuktakaiḥ 
kulādibhyo 'dhikāḥ sabhyās tebhyo 'dhyakṣaḥ smṛto 'dhikaḥ  Brh 
sarveṣām adhiko rājā dharmaṃ yatnena niścitam 
uttamādhamamadhyānāṃ vivādānāṃ vicāraṇāt  Brh 
uparyupari buddhīnāṃ carantīśvarabuddhayaḥ 
ajñānatimiropetān saṃdehapaṭalānvitān  Brh 
nirāmayān yaḥ kurute śāstrāñjanaśalākayā 
iha kīrtiṃ rājapūjāṃ labhate svargatiṃ ca saḥ  Brh 
lobhadveṣādikaṃ tyaktvā yaḥ kuryāt kāryanirṇayam 
śāstroditena vidhinā tasya yajñaphalaṃ bhavet  Brh 
adharmataḥ pravṛttaṃ tu nopekṣeran sabhāsadaḥ 
upekṣamāṇās te bhūpā narakaṃ yānty adhomukhāḥ  Brh 
nyāyamārgād apetaṃ tu jñātvā cittaṃ mahīpateḥ 
vaktavyaṃ tv apriyaṃ tatra na sabhyaḥ kilbiṣī tataḥ  Brh 
sabhyena tāvad vaktavyaṃ dharmārthasahitaṃ vacaḥ 
śṛṇoti yadi no rājā syāt tu sabhyas tato 'naghaḥ  Brh 
anirṇīteṣu yady evaṃ saṃbhāṣeta raho 'rthinā 
prāḍvivāko 'pi daṇḍyaḥ syāt sabhyāś caiva viśeṣataḥ  Brh 
snetāc cājñānato vāpi mohād vā lobhato 'pi vā 
yatra sabhyo 'nyathāvādī daṇḍyo 'sabhyaḥ smṛto hi saḥ  Brh 
lekhyaṃ yatra na vidyeta na sākṣī na ca bhuktayaḥ 
pramāṇāni na santy ekaṃ pramāṇaṃ tatra pārthivaḥ  Brh 
niścetuṃ ye na śakyāḥ syur vādāḥ saṃdigdharūpiṇaḥ 
teṣāṃ nṛpaḥ pramāṇ aṃ syāt sa sarvasya prabhur yataḥ  Brh 
vyavahārān svayaṃ paśyet sabhyaiḥ parivṛto 'nvaham  Brh 
anyāyavādinaḥ sabhyās tathaivotkocajīvinaḥ 
viśvaste vañcakāś caiva nirvāsyāḥ sarva eva te  Brh 
niyukto vāniyukto vā śāstrajño vaktum arhati 
yat tena sadasi proktaṃ sa dharmo nātra saṃśayaḥ  Brh 
pūrvāmukhas tūpaviśed rājā sabhyā udaṅmukhāḥ 
gaṇakaḥ paścimā yas tu lekhako dakṣiṇāmukhaḥ  Brh 
yathā yamaḥ priyadveṣyau prāpte kāle niyacchati 
tathā rājñā niyantavyāḥ prajās tad dhi yamavratam  Brh 
dharmaśāstrārthaśāstrābhyām avirodhena pārthivaḥ 
samīkṣamāṇo nipuṇaṃ vyavahāragatiṃ nayet  Brh 
nyāyaśāstram atikramya sabhyair atra tu niścitam 
tatra dharmo hato hanti sarvān eva na saṃśayaḥ  Brh 
dhāryaṃ manvādikaṃ śāstraṃ nārthaśāstraṃ kathaṃcana 
dvayor virodhe kartavyaṃ dharmaśāstroditaṃ vacaḥ  Brh 
kevalaṃ śāstram āśritya na kartavyo vinirṇayaḥ 
yuktihīne vicāre tu dharmahāniḥ prajāyate  Brh 
pūrvāhṇe tām adhiṣṭhāya vṛddhāmātyānujīvibhiḥ 
paśyet purāṇadharmārtha śāstrāṇi śṛṇuyāt tathā  Brh 
cauro 'cauraḥ sādhv asādhu jāyate vyavahārataḥ 
yuktiṃ vinā vicāreṇa māṇḍavyaś coratāṃ gataḥ  Brh 
asatyāḥ satyasadṛśāḥ satyāś cāsatyasaṃnibhāḥ 
dṛśyante bhrāntijanakās tasmād yuktyā vicārayet Brh 
yajñe saṃpūjyate viṣṇur vyavahāre mahīpatiḥ 
jayī tu yajamāno 'tra jitaḥ paśur udāhṛtaḥ  Brh 
pūrvapakṣottarāv ādyaṃ pratijñā ca haviḥ smṛtaḥ 
trayī śāstrāṇi sabhyās tu ṛtvijo dakṣiṇā damaḥ  Brh 
tathā caivopadṛṣṭārau jñeyau gaṇakalekhakau 
eṣo 'dhvarasamaḥ prokto vyavahāraḥ samāhṛtaḥ  Brh 
smṛtyācāravyapetena mārgenādharṣitaḥ paraiḥ 
āvedayati ced rājñe vyavahārapadaṃ hi tat  Brh 
patitādikṛtaś caiva yaś ca na prakṛtiṃ gataḥ 
asvatantrakṛtaś caiva pūrvapakṣo na sidhyati  Brh 
mattonmattārtavyasani bālavṛddhaprayojitaḥ 
asaṃbandhakṛtaś caiva vyavahāro na sidhyati  Brh 
guruśiṣyau pitāputrau dampatī svāmibhṛtyakau 
eteṣāṃ samavetānāṃ vyavahāro na sidhyati  Brh 
evaṃ parīkṣitaṃ sabhyaiḥ pūrvpakṣaṃ tu lekhayet 
aprasiddhaṃ puradviṣṭaṃ vivādaṃ na vicārayet  Brh

pratilomaprasūtānāṃ tathā durganivāsinām 
deśajātikulādīnāṃ ye dharmās tatpravartitāḥ  Brh

madhyadeśe karmakarāḥ śilpinaś ca gavāśinaḥ  Brh 
matsyādāś ca narāḥ pūrve vyabhicāraratāḥ striyaḥ 
uttare madyapā nāryaḥ spṛśyā nṝṇāṃ rajasvalāḥ  Brh
