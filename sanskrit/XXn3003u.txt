 (67) (10) sacen mañjuśrīr bodhisattvaḥ sarvasattvān avaivartikatve pratiṣṭhāpayet |
 yo vānyo bodhisattva imaṁ dharmaparyāyam adhimuktaḥ likhāpayitvā parebhyo vistareṇa saṁprakāśayet,
 antaśa ekaṁ sattvam apy asmin dharmaparyāye 'vatārayet |
 ayaṁ tato bahutaraṁ puṇyaṁ prasavet ||
 atha khalu bhagavāṁs tasyāṁ velāyām imā gāthā abhāṣat |
 yo bodhisattvo daśabuddhakoṭināṁ saddharmaṁ dhārayet kṣayāntakāle |
 sūtraṁ ca yo 'nyaḥ śṛṇuyāt sagauravād idaṁ tataḥ puṇyamahāntaṁ viśeṣayet || 1 ||
 yo buddhakoṭīdaśa pūjayed ṛddhyā samākramya daśāsu dikṣu |
 vandāpayet sa puruṣāṁś ca sarvān kṛtvā kṛpāṁ sarvasukheṣv agṛddhaḥ || 2 ||
 (68) yaś cedaṁ sūtraṁ jinadharmasūcakaṁ parasya deśeta muhūrtakaṁ pi |
