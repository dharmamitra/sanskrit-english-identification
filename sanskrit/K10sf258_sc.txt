Upasenasūtra
Wal siddham  rājagṛhe nidānam 
Wal tena khalu samayenāyuṣmān upaseno rājagṛhe viharati śītavane śmaśāne sarpaśauṇḍikaprāgbhāre kalikopavicārāntarikāyām 
Wal athāyuṣmata upasenasya ekākino rahasigatasya pratisalīnasya āśīviṣaḥ kāye nipatita uggratejā ghoraviṣaḥ parittas tadyathā ājanaśalākā 
Wal tatrāyuṣmān upaseno bhikṣūn āmatrayati  āśīviṣo me kāye nipatitaḥ tadyathā ājanaśalākā 
aga tāvan me āyuṣmantaḥ kāya samatato nuparigṛhya bahir abhinirharata mā ihaiva
viśariṣyati tadyathā busamuṣṭiḥ 
Wal tena khalu samayenāyuṣmāñ śāriputra āyuṣmata upasenasya nātidūre niṣaṇṇo bhūd anyataram vṛkṣamūla niśritya 
Wal aśrauṣīd āyuṣmāñ śāriputra āyuṣmata upasenasya vāgvyāhāram  śrutvā ca punar yenāyuṣmān upasenas tenopajagāma  upetyāyuṣmantam upasenam  
idam avocat  
Wal na khalu vayam āyuṣmata upasenasya paśyāmo mukhasya vā anyathātvam indriyāṇā vā
nānākaraṇam  atha ca punar āyuṣmān upasena evam āha  āśīviṣo me kāye nipatitaḥ
pūrvavad yāvat tadyathā busamuṣṭiḥ 
Wal yasyāyuṣmāñ śariputra eva syāt  aha cakṣur mama cakṣuḥ  aha śrotta ghrāṇa
jihvā kāyaḥ  aha manaḥ  mama manaḥ  aha rūpa mama rūpam  aha śabdagandharasaspṛṣṭavyadharmā
mama dharmāḥ  aha pṛthivīdhātur mama pṛthivīdhātuḥ  aham abdhātus tejodhātur vāyudhātur
ākāśadhātur vijñānadhātuḥ  aha rūpaskandho mama rūpaskandhaḥ  aha vedanāsajñāsaskāravijñānaskandho
mama vijñānaskandhaḥ  tasya syād mukhasya vā anyathātvam indriyāṇā vā nānākaraṇam 
Wal mama tu naiva bhavati  aha cakṣur mama cakṣuḥ  tadyathā aha vijñānaskandho mama vijñānaskandhaḥ  tasya me ki bhaviṣyati mukhasya  
vā anyathātvam indriyāṇā vā nānākaraṇam  
Wal dīragharāttra khalv āyuṣmata upasenasya ahakaramamakarāsmimānābhiniveśānuśayaḥ
prahīṇāḥ parijñātā ucchinnamūlās tālamastakavad anābhavagatikā āyatyām anutpādadharmāṇaḥ 
tasya te ki bhaviṣyati mukhasya vā anyathātvam indriyāṇā vā nānākaraṇam 
Wal athāyuṣmāñ śariputtraḥ āyuṣmata upasenasya kāya sāmantakenānuparigṛhya bahir abhinirharati 
Wal samanantarābhinirhṛta āyuṣmata upasenasya kāyaḥ śataśo viśirṇas tadyathā busamuṣṭiḥ 
Wal athāyuṣmāñ śāriputro syā velāyā gāthā babhāse 
Wal sucīrṇe brahmacarye smin mārge caiva subhāvite 
tuṣṭa āyukṣayād bhavati viṣapāttra ivojjhite 
Wal sucīrṇe brahmacarye smin mārge caiva subhāvite 
tuṣṭa āyukṣayād bhavati rogasyāpagame yathā 
Wal sucīrṇe brahmacarye smin mārge caiva subhāvite 
ādīptād vā gṛhād mukto maraṇānte na śocate 
Wal sucīrṇe brahmacarye smin mārge caiva subhāvite 
tṛṣṇakāṣṭhasama loka paśyati prajñayā yadā 
Wal athāyuṣmāñ śāriputra āyuṣmata upasenasya śarīre śāripūjā kṛtvā yena bhagavās tenopajagāma upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvā ekāte  
nyaṣīdat  
Wal ekātaniṣaṇṇa āyuṣmāñ śāriputtro bhagavatam idam avocat 
Wal āyuṣmato bhadata upasenasya āśīviṣaḥ kāye nipatita ugratejā ghoraviṣaḥ parittas tadyathā janaśalākā pūrvavad yāvat tasya kāyo viśīrṇas  
tadyathā busamuṣṭiḥ  
Wal sacec chāriputra upasenasya kulaputtrasya tasmi samaye imā gāthāḥ pratyabhāsyata imāni ca matrapadāni nāsyāśīviṣaḥ kāye kramiṣyan  
nāsya kāyo viśariṣyat tadyathā busamuṣṭiḥ  
Wal yā bhadata katamā gāthāḥ katamāni matrapadāni 
Wal maitrī me dhṛtirāṣṭreṣu maitrī airāvaṇeṣu ca 
chibbāputtreṣu me maitrī kabalāśvatareṣu ca 
Wal karkoṭakeṣu me maitrī kṛṣṇagautamakeṣu ca 
nandopandau ye nāgā teṣu maitrī ca me sadā 
Wal apādakeṣu me maitrī maitrī me dvipadeṣu ca 
catuṣpadeṣu me maitrī maitrī bahupadeṣu ca 
Wal sarvvanāgeṣu me maitrī ye nāgā udadhiniśritāḥ 
sarvvasatveṣu me maitrī ye ttrasāḥ sthāvarāś ca ye 
Wal sukhinaḥ sarvve satvā hi sarvve satvā niramayāḥ 
sarvve bhadrāṇi paśyatu mā caiśā pāpam āgamat 
Wal sarpaśauṇḍikaprāgbhāre nitya viharato mama 
āśīviṣo ghoraviṣo jīvitam uparundhati 
Wal yena me satyavākyena śāstā lokeṣv anuttaraḥ 
tena me satyavākyena mā me kāye viṣa kramet 
Wal rāgo dveṣaś ca mohaś ca ete loke viṣattrayam 
nirvviṣo bhagavān buddhaḥ satyadharmahata viṣam 
Wal hata viṣa ghoraviṣa kṛto rakṣāparikaraḥ 
sarvve hataviṣa biddhās tad uraga hata viṣam 
Wal tatremāni mantrapadāni bhavati tadyathā  otubile  tubile  tube  pratube  naṭṭe  sunaṭṭe  kevaṭṭe  munaye  samaye  datte  
 nīlakeśe  vālakupe  ole  oṅko  svāhā  
Wal sacec chāriputtra upasenasya kulaputrasya tasmi samaye imā gāthāḥ pratyabhāyata imāni ca matrapadāni nāsyāśīviṣa kāye kramiṣyat  
 nāsya kāyo vyaśariṣyat tadyathā busamuṣṭiḥ  
Wal tat kuta etad bhadata labhyam āyuṣmata upasenena yat tasmi samaye aśrutapūrvvā gāthāḥ pratibhāṣyate aśrutapūrvvāṇi vā matrapadāni  
tadyathā etarhi bhagavataḥ  
Wal athāyuṣmāñ śāriputtro bhagavato bhāṣitam abhinandyānumodya bhagavatpādau śirasā bhagavato ntikāt prakrātaḥ
