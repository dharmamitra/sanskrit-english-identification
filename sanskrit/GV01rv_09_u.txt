RV ayā rucā hariṇyā punāno viśvā dveṣāṃsi tarati svayugvabhiḥ sūro na svayugvabhiḥ dhārā sutasya rocate punāno aruṣo hariḥ
RV viśvā yad rūpā pariyāty ṛkvabhiḥ saptāsyebhirṛkvabhiḥ
 RV tvaṃ tyat paṇīnāṃ vido vasu saṃ mātṛbhirmarjayasi sva ā dama ṛtasya dhītibhirdame parāvato na sāma tad yatrāraṇanti dhītayaḥ 
RV tridhātubhiraruṣībhirvayo dadhe rocamāno vayo dadhe
 RV pūrvāmanu pradiśaṃ yāti cekitat saṃ raśmibhiryatate darśato ratho daivyo darśato rathaḥ agmannukthāni pauṃsyendraṃ jaitrāya 
RV vajraśca yad bhavatho anapacyutāsamatsvanapacyutā
RV nānānaṃ vā u no dhiyo vi vratāni janānām
RV takṣā riṣṭaṃ rutaṃ bhiṣag brahmā sunvantamichatīndrāyendo pari srava
RV jaratībhiroṣadhībhiḥ parṇebhiḥ śakunānām
RV kārmāro aśmabhirdyubhirhiraṇyavantamichatīndrāyendo pari srava
RV kārurahaṃ tato bhiṣagupalaprakṣiṇī nanā
RV nānādhiyovasūyavo 'nu gā iva tasthimendrāyendo pari srava
RV aśvo voḷhā sukhaṃ rathaṃ hasanāmupamantriṇaḥ
RV śepo romaṇvantau bhedau vārin maṇḍūka ichatīndrāyendo pari srava
RV śaryaṇāvati somamindraḥ pibatu vṛtrahā
RV balaṃ dadhāna ātmani kariṣyan vīryaṃ mahadindrāyendo pari srava
RV ā pavasva diśāṃ pata ārjīkāt soma mīḍhvaḥ
RV ṛtavākena satyena śraddhayā tapasā suta indrāyendo pari srava
RV parjanyavṛddhaṃ mahiṣaṃ taṃ sūryasya duhitābharat
RV taṃ gandharvāḥ pratyagṛbhṇan taṃ some rasamādadhurindrāyendopari srava
RV ṛtaṃ vadannṛtadyumna satyaṃ vadan satyakarman
RV śraddhāṃ vadan soma rājan dhātrā soma pariṣkṛta indrāyendo pari srava
RV satyamugrasya bṛhataḥ saṃ sravanti saṃsravāḥ
RV saṃ yanti rasino rasāḥ punāno brahmaṇā hara indrāyendo pari srava
RV yatra brahmā pavamāna chandasyāṃ vācaṃ vadan
RV grāvṇā some mahīyate somenānandaṃ janayannindrāyendo pari srava
RV yatra jyotirajasraṃ yasmin loke svarhitam
RV tasmin māṃ dhehi pavamānāmṛte loke akṣita indrāyendo pari srava
RV yatra rājā vaivasvato yatrāvarodhanaṃ divaḥ
RV yatrāmūryahvatīrāpastatra māmamṛtaṃ kṛdhīndrāyendo pari srava
RV yatrānukāmaṃ caraṇaṃ trināke tridive divaḥ
RV lokā yatra jyotiṣmantastatra māmamṛtaṃ kṛdhīndrāyendo pari srava
RV yatra kāmā nikāmāśca yatra bradhnasya viṣṭapam
RV svadhā ca yatra tṛptiśca tatra māmamṛtaṃ kṛdhīndrāyendo parisrava
RV yatrānandāśca modāśca mudaḥ pramuda āsate
RV kāmasya yatrāptāḥ kāmāstatra māmamṛtaṃ kṛdhīndrāyendo pari srava
RV ya indoḥ pavamānasyānu dhāmānyakramīt
RV tamāhuḥ suprajā iti yaste somāvidhan mana indrāyendo pari srava
RV ṛṣe mantrakṛtāṃ stomaiḥ kaśyapodvardhayan giraḥ
RV somaṃnamasya rājānaṃ yo jajñe vīrudhāṃ patirindrāyendo parisrava
RV sapta diśo nānāsūryāḥ sapta hotāra ṛtvijaḥ
RV devā ādityā ye sapta tebhiḥ somābhi rakṣa na indrayendo pari srava
RV yat te rājañchṛtaṃ havistena somābhi rakṣa naḥ
RV arātīvā mā nastārīn mo ca naḥ kiṃ canāmamadindrāyendo parisrava
