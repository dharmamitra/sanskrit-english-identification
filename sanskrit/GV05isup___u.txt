ĪśaUpaniṣad Īśopaniṣad or Īśāvāsyopanisad
 Kāṇva recension
īśā vāsyam idaṃ sarvaṃ yat kiñca jagatyāṃ jagat 
tena tyaktena bhuñjīthā mā gṛdhaḥ kasya sviddhanam  IsUp 
kurvann eveha karmāṇi jijīviṣecchataṃ samāḥ 
evaṃ tvayi nānyatheto 'sti na karma lipyate nare  IsUp 
asuryā nāma te lokā andhena tamasāvṛtāḥ 
tāṃste pretyābhigacchanti ye ke cātmahano janāḥ  IsUp 
anejad ekaṃ manaso javīyo nainaddevā āpnuvanpūrvamarṣat 
taddhāvato 'nyānatyeti tiṣṭhat tasminn apo mātariśvā dadhāti  IsUp 
tad ejati tan naijati tad dūre tad v antike 
tad antar asya sarvasya tad u sarvasyāsya bāhyataḥ  IsUp 
yas tu sarvāṇi bhūtāny ātmany evānupaśyati 
sarvabhūteṣu cātmānaṃ tato na vijugupsate  IsUp 
yasmin sarvāṇi bhūtāny ātmaivābhūd vijānataḥ 
tatra ko mohaḥ kaḥ śoka ekatvam anupaśyataḥ  IsUp 
sa paryagāc chukram akāyam avraṇam asnāviraṃ śuddham apāpaviddham 
kavir manīṣī paribhūḥ svayambhūr yāthātathyator 'thān vyadadhāc chāśvatībhyaḥ samābhyaḥ  IsUp 
andhaṃ tamaḥ praviśanti ye 'vidyām upāsate 
tato bhūya iva te tamo ya uvidyāyāṃ ratāḥ  IsUp 
anyad evāhur vidyayān yad āhur avidyayā 
iti śuśruma dhīrāṇāṃ ye nas tad vicacakṣire  IsUp 
vidyāṃ cāvidyāṃ ca yas tad vedobhayaṃ saha 
avidyayā mṛtyuṃ tīrtvā vidyayāmṛtam aśnute  IsUp 
andhaṃ tamaḥ praviśanti ye 'sambhūtim upāsate 
tato bhūya iva te tamo ya u sambhūtyāṃ ratāḥ  IsUp 
anyad evāhuḥ saṃbhavād anyad āhur asaṃbhavāt 
iti śuśruma dhīrāṇāṃ ye nas tad vicacakṣire  IsUp 
saṃbhūtiṃ ca vināśaṃ ca yas tad vedobhayaṃ saha 
vināśena mṛtyuṃ tīrtvā saṃbhūtyāmṛtam aśnute  IsUp 
hiraṇmayena pātreṇa satyasyāpihitaṃ mukham 
tat tvaṃ pūṣann apāvṛṇu satyadharmāya dṛṣṭaye  IsUp 
pūṣann ekarṣe yama sūrya prājāpatya vyūha raśmīn samūha tejaḥ 
yat te rūpaṃ kalyāṇatamaṃ tat te paśyāmi yo 'sāv asau puruṣaḥ so 'ham asmi  IsUp 
vāyur anilam amṛtam athedaṃ bhasmāntaṃ śarīram 
oṃ krato smara kṛtaṃ smara krato smara kṛtaṃ smara  IsUp 
agne naya supathā rāye asmān viśvāni deva vayunāni vidvān 
yuyodhy asmaj juhurāṇam eno bhūyiṣṭhāṃ te nama uktiṃ vidhema  IsUp
