kuśalas tva pitrye śilpasthānakarmasthāne tadyathāhastigrīvāyām aśvapṛṣṭhe rathe

Hart tathyam aha bhadanta kuśalaḥ pitrye śilpasthānakarmasthāne tadyathāhastigrīvāyām
aśvapṛṣṭhe rathe sarau dhanuṣy apayāne niryāṇe akuśagrahetomaragrahe lipigaṇananyasanasakhyāmudrāyām
Hart atha puruṣa āgacchet tasyaiva syāt kuśalo bata Bodho rājakumāraḥ pitryeśilpasthānakarmasthāne
tadyathāhastigrīvāyām aśvapṛṣṭhe rathe saraudhanuṣy apayāne niryāṇe akuśagrahe pāśagrahe
tomaragrahe lipigaṇananyasanasakhyāmudrāyā yanv aham asyāntikād anyatamānyatamaśilpasthānakarmasthānam anvāgamayeyam iti
Hart sa ca syād aśrāddhaḥ ki manyase Bodha yat tac chrāddhena prāptavyaprāpnuyāt saḥ
no bhadata
Hart sa ca syāc chaṭhaḥ ki manyase Bodha yat tadṛ śaṭhīna prāptavyanprāpnuyāt sa
no bhadanta
Hart sa ca syād bahvābādhaḥ ki manyase Bodha yat tad alpābādhena prāptavya prāpnuyāt saḥ
no bhadanta
Hart sa ca syād anārabdhavīryaḥ ki manyase Bodha yat tad ārabdhavīryeṇaprāptavya prāpnuyāt sa
no bhadanta
Hart sa ca syād duṣprajñaḥ ki manyase Bodha yat tat prājñena prāptavyaprāpnuyāt sa
no bhadanta ekaikena tāvad bhadanta ito ṅgena samanvāgatena tena puruṣeṇa na sukara
mamāntikād anyatamānyatama śilpasthānakarmasthānam anvāgamayitu kaḥ punar vādaḥ sarvaiḥ
Hart dvitīyo āpuruṣa āgacchet tasyaiva syāt kuśalo bata Bodho rājakumāraḥ pitrye
śilpasthānakarmmasthāne tadyathāhastigrīvāyā aśvapṛṣṭhe rathe sarau dhanuṣy apayāne
niryāṇe akuśagrahe pāśagrahe tomaragrahelipigaṇananyasanasakhyāmudrāyā yanv
aham asyāntikād anyatamānyatamac chilpasthānakarmmasthānam anvāgamayeyam iti

Hart sa ca syāt prājñaḥ ki manyase Bodha yat tat prājñena prāptavya prāpnuyātsa
eva bhadanta ekaikena tāvad bhadanta ito ṅgena samanvāgatena tenapuruṣeṇa sukara
mamāntikād anyatamānyatamac chilpasthānakarmasthānasamanvāgamayitu kaḥ punar vādaḥ sarvair
Hart evam eva Bodha pacabhiḥ prādhānikāgais samanvāgata āryaśrāvakaḥkṣipram evāsmi dharmavinaye āsravakṣayam anuprāpnoti katamaiḥ pacabhir 
Hart iha Bodha āryaśrāvakasya tathāgatasyāntike śraddhābhiniviṣṭābhavatimū lajātāpratiṣṭhitāasahāryāśramaṇena
vā brāhmaṇena vā devena vā māreṇa vā brahmaṇā vā kenacid vā punar loke sahadharmatam
anena prathamenaprādhānikenāgena samanvāgato bhavaty āryaśrāvakaḥ
Hart punar apara  Bodha āryaśrāvako śaṭhībhavaty amāyāvīṛjukoṛjukajātīyaḥ sa yathābhū
tam ātmānam āviṣkaroti śāstur antike vijñānā casabrahmacārīṇām anena dvitīyena prādhānikenāgena
samanvāgatobhavaty āryaśrāvakaḥ
Hart punar apara āryaśrāvaka alpābādho bhavaty arogajātīyaḥ samayāpācanyāgrahaṇyāsamanvāgato
nātyuṣṇayā nātiśītayāavyābādhayāṛtusukhāyāyayāpy aśitapītakhaditāsvāditāni samyaksukhena
paripāka gacchanti anenatṛtīyena prādhānikāgena samanvāgato bhavaty ābhavaty āryaśrāvakaḥ
Hart punar apara Bodha āryaśrāvakaḥ ārabdhavīryo viharati sthāmavā vīryavānutsāhīdṛḍhaparākramo
nikṣiptadhuraḥ kuśaleṣu dharmeṣu kāma tvak snāyvasthi cāvatiṣṭhatā pariśuṣyatu
śarīrān māsaśoṇitam atha ca punar yattad ārabdhavīryeṇa prāptavya sthāmavatā vīryavatā
utsāhinā dṛḍhaparākrameṇānikṣiptadhureṇa kuśaleṣu dharmeṣu tad vata nāprāpyāntarādvīryasya
srasana bhaviṣyaty anena caturthena prādhānikenāgena samanavāgato bhavaty āryaśrāvakaḥ
Hart punar apara Bodhāryaśrāvakaḥ prajñāvān viharati lokasyodayāstagaminyāprajñayāsamanvāgataḥ
āryayānairyāṇikayānairvedhikayāniryātitatkaraḥ sya hetoḥ samyagduḥkhakṣayāya duḥkhasyāntakriyāyai
anena pacamena prādhānikenāgena samanvāgato bhavaty āryaśrāvakaḥ
Hart ebhir Bodha pacabhiḥ prādhānikāgais samanvāgataḥ āryaśrāvakaḥ kṣipramevāsmi dharmavinaye āsravakṣayam anuprāpnoti
Hart atha Bodho rājakumāraḥ pū rva kāyam abhyunnamayya dakṣiṇa bāhumabhiprasāryāttamanāttamanāudānam
udānayati aho buddha aho dharma ahosagha aho dharmasya svākhyātatā yatredānī
