jaṅghā navāṅgulā jñeyā gulphamarddhāṅgulaṃ bhavet  
adho bhāgā prakartavyā ekāṅgulaprakīrtitā 
catuḥkalaṃ ca vijñeyā hikkā cāsyācāṃsāgrameva ca  
bāhū trigolakaṃ caiva prabāhū aṣṭamaṅgulam 
saptāṅgulamitaṃ jñeyaṃ ucchritaṃ karapallavam  
yathāśobhena vijñeyā kartavyā māṃsavartanamnī 
vāmanasya pramāṇaṃ tu kathitaṃ munisattama  
 iti ātreyatilake catustālasya lakṣaṇam 
mahāpratimāvinyāsaṃ pravakṣyāmyadhunā śṛṇu 
daśapañcādhikairhastaiḥ pratimā kanyasī smṛtāḥ  
dviguṇā madhyamā jñeyā jyeṣṭhā tu triguṇā smṛtāḥ 
ataḥ paraṃ na kurvīta yadicchet śreyamātmanaḥ   
dagdhā jīrṇā ca bhagnā ca sphuṭitā cāpi devatāḥ 
sthitāvasthāpyamānā vā sadā doṣakarā bhavet  
dagdhārcayā anāvṛṣṭirjīrṇārcayā dhanakṣayaḥ 
bhagnārcayā kule nāśaḥ sphuṭitā yuddhamādiśet  
arccā vā yadi vā liṅgaṃ devī mātṛgaṇastathā 
śīghramutpāṭayedeva vidhidṛṣṭena karmaṇā  
puṣpārghaṃ ca tathā dhūpaṃ naivedyaṃ balimeva ca 
dattvā ca vāsasī caiva homakarmasamanvitaḥ  
vipraśāntyudakaṃ caiva vedadeva
mantreṇa kārayet 
bālarajju tathā mauñjaṃ dukūlakṣomakastathā  
vidhirevaṃ samuddiṣṭaṃ rajju cātra vidhīyate 
vṛṣasya kakude baddhvā ākarṣejjīrṇadevatām  
śailīmayī bhavedarccā tīrthe bahūdakeṣu ca 
nadīsaṅgamasaṃsthāne tasmiṃścaiva tu nikṣipet  
sauvarṇaṃ rajataṃ caiva tāmraṃ raityamayīmapi 
drāvayedagninā sarvaṃ yadicchecchreyamātmani  
dārumayī bhavedarcā navavastreṇa veṣṭayet 
ghṛtena madhunā snigdhaṃ dīptamagnau pradāpayet  
pārthivī ca bhavedarccā yadi syānmṛṇmayīmapi 
bhūkhanitvā śiro mātre nyasettasmin prapūrayet  
arccā vāyadi vā liṅgaṃ punaḥ śīghraṃ tu sthāpayet 
sarvalakṣaṇasampannā vidhidṛṣṭena sthāpayet  
dvijaśca jānāṃ
bālavṛddhānāṃ mānuṣāṇāṃ śubhāya ca 
rājā jayamavāpnoti śasyavṛddhikaraṃ bhavet
