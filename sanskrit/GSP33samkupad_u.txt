Śaṃkara Upadeśasāhasrī
caitanyaṃ sarvagaṃ sarvaṃ sarvabhūtaguhāśayam 
yat sarvaviṣayātītaṃ tasmai sarvavide namaḥ  SamUpadI 
samāpayya kriyāḥ sarvā dārāgnyādhānapūrvikāḥ 
brahmavidyām athedānīṃ vaktuṃ vedaḥ pracakrame  SamUpadI 
karmāṇi dehayogārthaṃ dehayoge priyāpriye 
dhruve syātāṃ tato rāgo dveṣaś caiva tataḥ kriyāḥ  SamUpadI 
dharmādharmau tato 'jñasya dehayogas tathā punaḥ
evaṃ nityapravṛtto 'yaṃ saṃsāraś cakravad bhṛśam  SamUpadI 
ajñānaṃ tasya mūlaṃ syād iti taddhānam iṣyate 
brahmavidyāta ārabdhā tato niḥśreyasaṃ bhavet  SamUpadI 
vidyaivājñānahānāya na karmāpratikūlataḥ 
nājñānasyāprahāṇe hi rāgadveṣakṣayo bhavet  SamUpadI 
rāgadveṣakṣayābhāve karma doṣodbhavaṃ dhruvam 
tasmān niḥśreyasārthāya vidyaivātra vidhīyate  SamUpadI 
nanu karma tathā nityaṃ kartavyaṃ jīvane sati 
vidyāyāḥ sahakāritvaṃ mokṣaṃ prati hi tad vrajet  SamUpadI 
yathā vidyā tathā karma coditatvāviśeṣataḥ 
pratyavāyasmṛteś caiva kāryaṃ karma mumukṣubhiḥ  SamUpadI 
nanu dhruvaphalā vidyā nānyat kiṃcid apekṣate 
nāgniṣṭomo yathaivānyad dhruvakāryo 'py apekṣate  SamUpadI 
tathā dhruvaphalā vidyā karma nityam apekṣate 
ity evaṃ kecid icchanti na karma pratikūlataḥ  SamUpadI 
vidyāyāḥ pratikūlaṃ hi karma syāt sābhimānataḥ 
nirvikārātmabuddhiś ca vidyetīha prakīrtitā  SamUpadI 
ahaṃ kartā mamedaṃ syād iti karma pravartate 
vastvadhīnā bhaved vidyā kartradhīno bhaved vidhiḥ  SamUpadI 
kārakāṇy upamṛdnāti vidyābbuddhim ivoṣare 
tatsatyamatim ādāya karma kartuṃ vyavasyati  SamUpadI 
viruddhatvād ataḥ śakyaṃ karma kartuṃ na vidyayā 
sahaivaṃ viduṣā tasmāt karma heyaṃ mumukṣuṇā  SamUpadI 
dehādyair aviśeṣeṇa dehino grahaṇaṃ nijam 
prāṇināṃ tad avidyotthaṃ tāvat karmavidhir bhavet  SamUpadI 
neti netīti dehādīn apohyātmāvaśeṣitaḥ 
aviśeṣātmabodhārthaṃ tenāvidyā nivartitā  SamUpadI 
nivṛttā sā kathaṃ bhūyaḥ prasūyeta pramāṇataḥ 
asaty evāviśeṣe hi pratyagātmani kevale  SamUpadI 
na ced bhūyaḥ prasūyeta kartā bhokteti dhīḥ katham 
sad asmīti ca vijñāne tasmād vidyāsahāyikā  SamUpadI 
atyarecayad ity ukto nyāsaḥ śrutyāta eva hi 
karmabhyo mānasāntebhya etāvad iti vājinām  SamUpadI 
amṛtatvaṃ śrutaṃ tasmāt tyājyaṃ karma mumukṣubhiḥ 
agniṣṭomavad ity uktaṃ tatredam abhidhīyate  SamUpadI 
naikakārakasādhyatvāt phalānyatvāc ca karmaṇaḥ 
vidyā tadviparītāto dṛṣṭānto viṣamo bhavet  SamUpadI 
kṛṣyādivat phalārthatvād anyakarmopabṛṃhaṇam 
agniṣṭomas tv apekṣeta vidyānyat kim apekṣate  SamUpadI 
pratyavāyas tu tasyaiva yasyāhaṃkāra iṣyate 
ahaṃkāraphalārthitve vidyete nātmavedinaḥ  SamUpadI 
tasmād ajñānahānāya saṃsāravinivṛttaye 
brahmavidyāvidhānāya prārabdhopaniṣat tv iyam  SamUpadI 
sader upanipūrvasya kvipi copaniṣad bhavet 
mandīkaraṇabhāvāc ca garbhādeḥ śātanāt tathā  SamUpadI 
iti caitanyaprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
pratiṣeddhum aśakyatvān neti netīti śeṣitam 
idaṃ nāham idaṃ nāham ity addhā pratipadyate  SamUpadI 
idaṃdhīr idamātmotthā vācārambhaṇagocarā 
niṣiddhātmodbhavatvāt sā na punar mānatāṃ vrajet  SamUpadI 
pūrvabuddhim abādhitvā nottarā jāyate matiḥ 
dṛśir ekaḥ svayaṃsiddhaḥ phalatvāt sa na bādhyate  SamUpadI 
idaṃvanam atikramya śokamohādidūṣitam 
vanād gāndhārako yadvat svam ātmānaṃ prapadyate  SamUpadI 
iti pratiṣedhaprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
īśvaraś ced anātmā syān nāsāv asmīti dhārayet 
ātmā ced īśvaro 'smīti vidyā sānyanivartikā  SamUpadI 
ātmano 'nyasya ced dharmā asthūlatvādayo matāḥ 
ajñeyatve 'sya kiṃ taiḥ syād ātmatve tv anyadhīhnutiḥ  SamUpadI 
mithyādhyāsaniṣedhārthaṃ tato 'sthūlādi gṛhyatām 
paratra cen niṣedhārthaṃ śūnyatāvarṇanaṃ hi tat  SamUpadI 
bubhutsor yadi cānyatra pratyagātmana iṣyate 
aprāṇo hy amanāḥ śubhra iti cānarthakaṃ vacaḥ  SamUpadI 
itīśvaraprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
ahaṃpratyayabījaṃ yad ahaṃpratyayavatsthitam 
nāhaṃpratyayavahnyuṣṭaṃ kathaṃ karma prarohati  SamUpadI 
dṛṣṭavac cet prarohaḥ syān nānyakarmā sa iṣyate 
tannirodhe kathaṃ tat syāt pṛcchāmo vas tad ucyatām  SamUpadI 
dehādyārambhasāmarthyāj jñānaṃ sadviṣayaṃ tvayi 
abhibhūya phalaṃ kuryāt karmānte jñānam udbhavet  SamUpadI 
ārabdhasya phale hy ete bhogo jñānaṃ ca karmaṇaḥ 
avirodhas tayor yukto vaidharmyaṃ cetarasya tu  SamUpadI 
dehātmajñānavaj jñānaṃ dehātmajñānabādhakam 
ātmany eya bhaved yasya sa necchann api mucyate  SamUpadI 
tataḥ sarvam idaṃ siddhaṃ prayogo 'smābhir īritaḥ  SamUpadI 
ity ahaṃpratyayaprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
mūtrāśaṅko yathodaṅko nāgrahīd amṛtaṃ muniḥ 
karmanāśabhayāj jantor ātmajñānāgrahas tathā  SamUpadI 
buddhisthaś calatīvātmā dhyāyatīva ca dṛśyate 
naugatasya yathā vṛkṣās tadvat saṃsāravibhramaḥ  SamUpadI 
nausthasya prātilomyena nagānāṃ gamanaṃ yathā 
ātmanaḥ saṃsṛtis tadvad dhyāyatīveti hi śrutiḥ  SamUpadI 
caitanyapratibimbena vyāpto bodho hi jāyate 
buddheḥ śabdādinirbhāsas tena momuhyate jagat  SamUpadI 
caitanyābhāsatāhamas tādarthyaṃ ca tad asya yat 
idamaṃśaprahāṇe na paraḥ so 'nubhavo bhavet  SamUpadI 
iti mūtrāśaṅkaprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
chittvā tyaktena hastena svayaṃ nātmā viśeṣyate 
tathā śiṣṭena sarveṇa yena yena viśeṣyate  SamUpadI 
tasmāt tyaktena hastena tulyaṃ sarvaṃ viśeṣaṇam 
anātmatvena tasmāj jño muktaḥ sarvaviśeṣaṇaiḥ  SamUpadI 
viśeṣaṇam idaṃ sarvaṃ sādhvalaṃkaraṇaṃ yathā 
avidyāstam ataḥ sarvaṃ jñāta ātmany asad bhavet  SamUpadI 
jñātaivātmā sadā grāhyo jñeyam utsṛjya kevalaḥ 
aham ity api yad grāhyaṃ vyapetāṅgasamaṃ hi tat  SamUpadI 
yāvān syād idamaṃśo yaḥ sa svato 'nyo viśeṣaṇam 
viśeṣaprakṣayo yatra siddho jñaś citragur yathā  SamUpadI 
idam aṃśo 'ham ity atra tyājyo nātmeti paṇḍitaiḥ 
ahaṃ brahmeti śiṣṭo 'ṃśo bhūtapūrvagater bhavet  SamUpadI 
iti chittvāprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
buddhyārūḍhaṃ sadā sarvaṃ dṛśyate yatra tatra vā 
mayā tasmāt paraṃ brahma sarvajñaś cāsmi sarvagaḥ  SamUpadI 
yathātmabuddhicārāṇāṃ sākṣī tadvat pareṣv api 
naivāpoḍhuṃ na vādātuṃ śakyas tasmāt paro hy aham  SamUpadI 
vikāritvam aśuddhatvaṃ bhautikatvaṃ na cātmanaḥ 
aśeṣabuddhisākṣitvād buddhivac cālpavedanā  SamUpadI 
maṇau prakāśyate yadvad raktādyākāratātape 
mayi saṃdṛśyate sarvam ātapeneva tan mayā  SamUpadI 
buddhau dṛśyaṃ bhaved buddhau satyāṃ nāsti viparyaye 
draṣṭā yasmāt sadā draṣṭā tasmād dvaitaṃ na vidyate  SamUpadI 
avivekāt parābhāvaṃ yathā buddhir avet tathā 
vivekāt tu parād anyaḥ svayaṃ cāpi na vidyate  SamUpadI 
iti buddhyārūḍhaprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
citisvarūpaṃ svata eva me mate rasādiyogas tava mohakāritaḥ 
ato na kiṃcit tava ceṣṭitena me phalaṃ bhavet sarvaviśeṣahānataḥ  SamUpadI 
vimucya māyāmayakāryatām iha praśāntim āyāhy asadīhitāt sadā 
ahaṃ paraṃ brahma sadā vimuktavat tathājam ekaṃ dvayavarjitaṃ yataḥ  SamUpadI 
sadā ca bhūteṣu samo 'smi kevalo yathā ca khaṃ sarvagam akṣaraṃ śivam 
nirantaraṃ niṣkalam akriyaṃ paraṃ tato na me 'stīha phalaṃ tavehitaiḥ  SamUpadI 
ahaṃ mamaiko na tadanyad iṣyate tathā na kasyāpy aham asmy asaṅgataḥ 
asaṅgarūpo 'ham ato na me tvayā kṛtena kāryaṃ tava cādvayatvataḥ  SamUpadI 
phale ca hetau ca jano viṣaktavān iti pracintyāham ato vimokṣaṇe 
janasya saṃvādam imaṃ prakḷptavān svarūpatattvārthavibodhakāraṇam  SamUpadI 
saṃvādam etaṃ yadi cintayen naro vimucyate 'jñānamahābhayāgamāt 
vimuktakāmaś ca tathā janaḥ sadā caraty aśokaḥ sama ātmavit sukhī  SamUpadI 
iti citisvarūpaprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
sūkṣmatāvyāpite jñeye gandhāder uttarottaram 
pratyagātmāvasāneṣu pūrvapūrvaprahāṇataḥ  SamUpadI 
śārīrā pṛthivī tāvad yāvad bāhyā pramāṇataḥ 
abādīni ca tattvāni tāvaj jñeyāni kṛtsnaśaḥ  SamUpadI 
vāyvādīnāṃ yathotpatteḥ pūrvaṃ khaṃ sarvagaṃ tathā 
aham ekaḥ sadā sarvaś cinmātraḥ sarvago 'dvayaḥ  SamUpadI 
brahmādyāḥ sthāvarāntā ye prāṇino mama pūḥ smṛtāḥ 
kāmakrodhādayo doṣā jāyeran me kuto 'nyataḥ  SamUpadI 
bhūtadoṣaiḥ sadāspṛṣṭaṃ sarvabhūtastham īśvaram 
nīlaṃ vyoma yathā bālo duṣṭaṃ māṃ vīkṣate janaḥ  SamUpadI 
maccaitanyāvabhāsyatvāt sarvaprāṇidhiyāṃ sadā 
pūr mama prāṇinaḥ sarve sarvajñasya vipāpmanaḥ  SamUpadI 
janimaj jñānavijñeyaṃ svapnajñānavad iṣyate 
nityaṃ nirviṣayaṃ jñānaṃ tasmād dvaitaṃ na vidyate  SamUpadI 
jñātur jñātir hi nityoktā suṣupte tv anyaśūnyataḥ 
jāgrajjñātis tv avidyātas tad grāhyaṃ cāsad iṣyatām  SamUpadI 
rūpavattvādyasattvān na dṛṣṭyādeḥ karmatā yathā 
evaṃ vijñānakarmatvaṃ bhūmno nāstīti gamyate  SamUpadI 
iti sūkṣmatāprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
dṛśisvarūpaṃ gaganopamaṃ paraṃ sakṛdvibhātaṃ tv ajam ekam akṣaram 
alepakaṃ sarvagataṃ yad advayaṃ tad eva cāhaṃ satataṃ vimukta om  SamUpadI 
dṛśis tu śuddho 'ham avikriyātmako na me 'sti kaścid viṣayaḥ svabhāvataḥ 
puras tiraś cordhvam adhaś ca sarvataḥ supūrṇabhūmā tv aja ātmani sthitaḥ  SamUpadI 
ajo 'maraś caiva tathājaro 'mṛtaḥ svayaṃprabhaḥ sarvagato 'ham advayaḥ 
na kāraṇaṃ kāryam atīva nirmalaḥ sadaiva tṛptaś ca tato vimukta om  SamUpadI 
suṣuptajāgratsvapataś ca darśanaṃ na me 'sti kiṃcit svam iveha mohanam 
svataś ca teṣāṃ parato 'py asattvatas turīya evāsmi sadā dṛg advayaḥ  SamUpadI 
śarīrabuddhīndriyaduḥkhasaṃtatir na me na cāhaṃ mama nirvikārataḥ 
asattvahetoś ca tathaiva saṃtater asattvam asyāḥ svapato hi dṛśyavat  SamUpadI 
idaṃ tu satyaṃ mama nāsti vikriyā vikārahetur na hi me 'dvayatvataḥ 
na puṇyapāpe na ca mokṣabandhane na cāsti varṇāśramatāśarīrataḥ  SamUpadI 
anādito nirguṇato na karma me phalaṃ ca tasmāt paramo 'ham advayaḥ 
yathā nabhaḥ sarvagataṃ na lipyate tathā hy ahaṃ dehagato 'pi sūkṣmataḥ  SamUpadI 
sadā ca bhūteṣu samo 'ham īśvaraḥ kṣarākṣarābhyāṃ paramo hy athottamaḥ 
parātmatattvaś ca tathādvayo 'pi san viparyayeṇābhivṛtas tv avidyayā  SamUpadI 
avidyayā bhāvanayā ca karmabhir vivikta ātmāvyavadhiḥ sunirmalaḥ 
dṛgādiśaktipracito 'ham advayaḥ sthitaḥ svarūpe gaganaṃ yathācalam  SamUpadI 
ahaṃ paraṃ brahma viniścayātmadṛṅ na jāyate bhūya iti śruter vacaḥ 
na caiva bīje tv asati prajāyate phalaṃ na janmāsti tato hy amohatā  SamUpadI 
mamedam itthaṃ ca tavāda īdṛśaṃ tathāham evaṃ na paro 'pi cānyathā 
vimūḍhataivāsya janasya kalpanā sadā same brahmaṇi vādvaye śive  SamUpadI 
yad advayaṃ jñānam atīva nirmalaṃ mahātmanāṃ tatra na śokamohatā 
tayor abhāve na hi karma janma vā bhaved ayaṃ vedavidāṃ viniścayaḥ  SamUpadI 
suṣuptavaj jāgrati yo na paśyati dvayaṃ tu paśyann api cādvayatvataḥ 
tathā ca kurvann api niṣkriyaś ca yaḥ sa ātmavin nānya itīha niścayaḥ  SamUpadI 
itīdam uktaṃ paramārthadarśanaṃ mayā hi vedāntaviniścitaṃ param 
vimucyate 'smin yadi niścito bhaven na lipyate vyomavad eva karmabhiḥ  SamUpadI 
iti dṛśiprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
īkṣitṛtvaṃ svataḥsiddhaṃ jantūnāṃ ca tato 'nyatā 
ajñānād ity ato 'nyatvaṃ sad asīti nivartyate  SamUpadI 
etāvad dhy amṛtatvaṃ na kiṃcid anyat sahāyakam 
jñānasyeti bruvac chāstraṃ saliṅgaṃ karma bādhate  SamUpadI 
sarveṣāṃ manaso vṛttam aviśeṣeṇa paśyataḥ 
tasya me nirvikārasya viśeṣaḥ syāt kathaṃcana  SamUpadI 
manovṛttaṃ manaś caiva svapnavaj jāgratīkṣituḥ 
saṃprasāde dvayāsattvāc cinmātraḥ sarvago 'dvayaḥ  SamUpadI 
svapnaḥ satyo yathābodhād dehātmatvaṃ tathaiva ca 
pratyakṣādeḥ pramāṇatvaṃ jāgrat syād ātmavedanāt  SamUpadI 
vyomavat sarvabhūtastho bhūtadoṣair vivarjitaḥ 
sākṣī cetāguṇaḥ śuddho brahmaivāsmīti kevalaḥ  SamUpadI 
nāmarūpakriyābhyo 'nyo nityamuktasvarūpavān 
aham ātmā paraṃ brahma cinmātro 'haṃ sadādvayaḥ  SamUpadI 
ahaṃ brahmāsmi kartā ca bhoktā cāsmīti ye viduḥ 
te naṣṭā jñanakarmabhyāṃ nāstikāḥ syur na saṃśayaḥ  SamUpadI 
dharmādharmaphalair yoga iṣṭo 'dṛṣṭo yathātmanaḥ 
śāstrād brahmatvam apy asya mokṣo jñānāt tatheṣyatām  SamUpadI 
yā māhārajanādyās tā vāsanāḥ svapnadarśibhiḥ 
anubhūyanta eveha tato 'nyaḥ kevalo dṛśiḥ  SamUpadI 
kośād iva viniṣkṛṣṭaḥ kāryakāraṇavarjitaḥ 
yathāsir dṛśyate svapne tadvad boddhā svayaṃprabhaḥ  SamUpadI 
āpeṣāt pratibuddhasya jñasya svābhāvikaṃ padam 
uktaṃ netyādivākyena kalpitasyāpanetṛṇā  SamUpadI 
mahārājādayo lokā mayi yadvat prakalpitāḥ 
svapne tadvad dvayaṃ vidyād rūpaṃ vāsanayā saha  SamUpadI 
dehaliṅgātmanā kāryā vāsanārūpiṇā kriyā 
netinetyātmarūpatvān na me kāryā kriyā kvacit  SamUpadI 
na tato 'mṛtatāśāsti karmaṇo 'jñānahetutaḥ 
mokṣasya jñānahetutvān na tadanyad apekṣate  SamUpadI 
amṛtaṃ cābhayaṃ nārtaṃ netītyātmā priyo mama 
viparītam ato 'nyad yat tyajet tat sakriyaṃ tataḥ  SamUpadI 
itīkṣitṛtvaprakaraṇam  SamUpadI 
prakāśasthaṃ yathā dehaṃ sālokam abhimanyate 
draṣṭrābhāsaṃ tathā cittaṃ draṣṭāham iti manyate  SamUpadI 
yad eva dṛṣyate loke tenābhinnatvam ātmanaḥ 
prapadyate tato mūḍhas tenātmānaṃ na vindati  SamUpadI 
daśamasya navātmatvapratipattivad ātmanaḥ 
dṛśyeṣu tadvad evāyaṃ mūḍho loko na cānyathā  SamUpadI 
tvaṃ kuru tvaṃ tad eveti pratyayāv ekakālikau 
ekanīḍau kathaṃ syātāṃ viruddhau nyāyato vada  SamUpadI 
dehābhimānino duḥkhaṃ nādehasya svabhāvataḥ 
svāpavat tatprahāṇāya tat tvam ity ucyate dṛśeḥ  SamUpadI 
dṛśeś chāyā yadārūḍhā mukhacchāyeva darśane 
paśyaṃs taṃ pratyayaṃ yogī dṛṣṭa atmeti manyate  SamUpadI 
taṃ ca mūḍhaṃ ca yady anyaṃ pratyayaṃ vetti no dṛśeḥ 
sa eva yogināṃ śreṣṭho netaraḥ syān na saṃśayaḥ  SamUpadI 
vijñāter yas tu vijñātā sa tvam ity ucyate yataḥ 
sa syād anubhavas tasya tato 'nyo 'nubhavo mṛṣā  SamUpadI 
dṛśirūpe sadā nitye darśanādarśane mayi 
kathaṃ syātāṃ tato nānya iṣyate 'nubhavas tataḥ  SamUpadI 
yatsthas tāpo raver dehe dṛśeḥ sa viṣayo yathā 
sattvasthas tadvad eveha dṛśeḥ sa viṣayas tathā  SamUpadI 
pratiṣiddhedamaṃśo jñaḥ kham ivaikaraso 'dvayaḥ 
nityamuktas tathā śuddhaḥ so 'haṃ brahmāsmi kevalaḥ  SamUpadI 
vijñātur naiva vijñātā paro 'nyaḥ saṃbhavaty ataḥ 
vijñātāhaṃ paro muktaḥ sarvabhūteṣu sarvadā  SamUpadI 
yo vedāluptadṛṣṭitvam ātmano 'kartṛtāṃ tathā 
brahmavittvaṃ tathā muktvā sa ātmajño na cetaraḥ  SamUpadI 
jñātaivāham avijñeyaḥ śuddho muktaḥ sadety api 
vivekī pratyayo buddher dṛśyatvān nāśavattvataḥ  SamUpadI 
aluptā tv ātmano dṛṣṭir notpādyā kārakair yataḥ 
dṛśyayā cānyayā dṛṣṭyā janyatāsyāḥ prakalpitā  SamUpadI 
dehātmabuddhyapekṣatvād ātmanaḥ kartṛtā mṛṣā 
naiva kiṃcit karomīti satyā buddhiḥ pramāṇajā  SamUpadI 
kartṛtvaṃ kārakāpekṣam akartṛtvaṃ svabhāvataḥ 
kartā bhokteti vijñānaṃ mṛṣaiveti suniścitam  SamUpadI 
evaṃ śāstrānumānābhyāṃ svarūpe 'vagate sati 
niyojyo 'ham iti hy eṣā satyā buddhiḥ kathaṃ bhavet  SamUpadI 
yathā sarvāntaraṃ vyoma vyomno 'py abhyantaro hy aham 
nirvikāro 'calaḥ śuddho 'jaro muktaḥ sadādvayaḥ  SamUpadI
