karṇaparva
nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamam 
devīṃ sarasvatīṃ vyāsaṃ tato jayamudīrayet  Bhmj 
hate droṇe kurukṣetre punarabhyetya saṃjayaḥ 
rājñe nyavedayadyuddhamāścaryaṃ karṇapārthayoḥ  Bhmj 
tato duryodhanaḥ karṇaṃ cittasaṃkalpaśālinam 
cakre drauṇigirā hṛṣṭaḥ śūraṃ senāsu nāyakam  Bhmj 
abhiṣikto maṇimayaiḥ kalaśairyaśasāṃ nidhiḥ 
manorathasudhāvarṣī so 'bhavatkurubhūpateḥ  Bhmj 
vidhāya makaravyūhaṃ sthito vairikuleṣu saḥ 
cakrire pāṇḍavā vyūhamardhacandramatandritam  Bhmj 
tataḥ śastrāstrasaṃgharṣakīrṇavahnikaṇākule 
ghore pravṛtte samare nirdhoṣastumulo 'bhavat  Bhmj 
bhīmo 'tha kuñjarārūḍho mattamātaṅgamāsthitam 
caturdantaṃ jaghānograṃ kulūtanṛpatiṃ raṇe  Bhmj 
tataḥ karṇāntanirmuktaiḥ karṇacāpacyutaiḥ śaraiḥ 
nirbhinnāḥ kuñjarāḥ petuśchinnapakṣā ivācalāḥ  Bhmj 
pārthasātyakipāñcāladraupadeyaśikhaṇḍinaḥ 
kṛpadrauṇimukhaiścakruḥ paraṃ navamivāharam  Bhmj 
pārthaḥ saṃśaptakānhatvā nināya bhrātarau nṛpau 
daṇḍādhāraṃ sadaṇḍaṃ ca daṇḍādhārapuraṃ śaraiḥ  Bhmj 
atha pāṇḍyaḥ kurucamūṃ dārayañjagatībhujām 
bhallaiḥ śirāṃsi ciccheda labdhalakṣyaḥ prahāriṇām  Bhmj 
bhīṣmadroṇārjunaspardhāṃ darpajvarabhavāṃ nayaḥ 
sahate nityamātmānaṃ manyamāno balādhikam  Bhmj 
dakṣiṇāpathanāthena karṇāṭīsmitakīrtinā 
lāṭīnetraśitāstreṇa tena bāṇairnipīḍitā  Bhmj 
drauṇinā sūtapūtreṇa saubalena kṛpeṇa ca 
vāryamāṇāpi dudrāva kururājavarūthinī  Bhmj 
tamabhyetya javāddrauṇirviśikhāśanivarṣiṇam 
ayodhayadasaṃbhrānto jambhāririva śambaram  Bhmj 
tasya līlāvatīlolalocanabhramarāmbujam 
drauṇiḥ śiro jahārātha bhallenāndelikuṇḍalam  Bhmj 
pāṇḍyasaṃjñe hate vīre mauliratne mahībhujām 
pāṇḍusenā na śuśubhe śaśihineva śarvarī  Bhmj 
 pāṇḍyavadhaḥ    
tato gāḍhaniṣakteṣu śastravṛṣṭiṃ pibatsiva 
āviṣṭeṣviva vīreṣu yudhi māneṣvavāritam  Bhmj 
miśrībhūteṣu bhūpānāṃ ratheṣu ca gajeṣu ca 
naṣṭālokeṣu lokeṣu vapurgāḍhena pāṃsunā  Bhmj 
mleccharājaṃ mahānīkaṃ nakulaḥ krūrayodhinam 
jaghāna rākṣasācāraṃ sarvāyudhaviśāradam  Bhmj 
vimarde nibiḍe tasminnakulaṃ vadhagocaram 
smarankuntīvacaḥ karṇo nāvadhītsatyavikramaḥ  Bhmj 
tataḥ karṇamukhairvīraiḥ kīrṇāḥ pāñcālasṝñjayāḥ 
na lebhire paritrāṇaṃ kuravaścārjunādibhiḥ  Bhmj 
hatavīreṣu bhagneṣu cchinnadhvajaratheṣu ca 
gāḍhaviddhasravaddantighaṭāniṣpiṣṭapattiṣu  Bhmj 
akāṇḍatāṇḍavāviddhakabandhākulavartmasu 
viśālaśoṇitanadīmañjatkuñjaravājiṣu  Bhmj 
hataśeṣeṣu sainyeṣu śrānteṣu kurupāṇḍavāḥ 
avahāraṃ dināpāye cakruḥ śithilakārmukāḥ  Bhmj 
 prathamo yuddhadivasaḥ    
atha prabhāte saṃnaddhe saṃnikṛṣṭe baladvaye 
ūce duryodhanaṃ karṇaḥ pāṇḍusenāṃ vyalokayan  Bhmj 
tvatprasādapraṇayino mānasya vibhavasya ca 
adyāhamanṛṇo bhūtvā raṇe gacchāmi nirvṛtim  Bhmj 
adya gāṇḍīvadhanvānaṃ hatvā sāraṃ bhavaddviṣām 
saphalaṃ te karomyeṣa saṃkalpaṃ cirasaṃbhṛtam  Bhmj 
nindyaṃ sarvātmanā loke satāṃ svaguṇakīrtanam 
kiṃtu kartavyakāleṣu na praśaṃsanti mūkatām  Bhmj 
ahamabhyadhikaḥ pārthādvidyayā vikrameṇa ca 
etāvadadhikaṃ kṛṣṇo yadanargho 'sya sārathiḥ  Bhmj 
kṛṣṇādabhyadhikaḥ śalyo viditaḥ sarvabhūbhujām 
tena sārathinā jiṣṇuṃ vijetumahamutsahe  Bhmj 
iti vaikartanenokte hṛṣṭo rājā suyodhanaḥ 
tadevābhyetya vinayānmadrarājamabhāṣata  Bhmj 
tato madrādhipaḥ kruddhaḥ saṃrambhalulitāṃśukaḥ 
sāsūyaṃ vīkṣya rādheyaṃ kauravādhipamabhyadhāt  Bhmj 
aho saralatā rājannavamānāya kevalam 
yadahaṃ dīrghasaṃghābhyāṃ sārathye gaditastvayā  Bhmj 
kimahaṃ pāṇḍavānsarvānhantuṃ śakte na saṃgare 
nṛpaṃ māṃ sūtaputrasya sūtaṃ kartuṃ yadicchasi  Bhmj 
uktveti sahasā śalye sānuge hantumudyate 
prasādya taṃ kurupatirvaktuṃ samupacakrame  Bhmj 
madrarāja ja saṃrambhamasthāne kartumarhasi 
adhikastvaṃ hi govindādato 'smābhirihārthyate  Bhmj 
arjunādadhikaḥ kṛṣṇaḥ sārathirvihitaḥ paraiḥ 
tvaṃ karṇādadhikastasmātsārathye mā haṭhaṃ kṛthāḥ  Bhmj 
trailokyopaplave daityaiḥ prajāpativarorjitaiḥ 
rājatāyasasauvarṇasvacchandapuravāsibhiḥ  Bhmj 
vadhyamāneṣu lokeṣu devāḥ śakrapurogamāḥ 
purā pinākinaṃ devaṃ śaṃkaraṃ śaraṇaṃ yayuḥ  Bhmj 
viśvatrāṇāya viśveśastridaśairarthitastataḥ 
sarvadevamayaṃ kṛtvā rathaṃ viṣṇuṃ ca sāyakam  Bhmj 
uvāca devānsaṃnaddhaḥ sārathiḥ kalpyatāṃ mama 
matprabhāvādhikaḥ kaścidyena hantāsmi dānavān  Bhmj 
tataḥ śakramukhā devā vicārya suciraṃ dhiyā 
brahmāṇaṃ viśvakartāraṃ sārathye paryakalpayan  Bhmj 
tenoṅkārapratodena gṛhīte syandane haraḥ 
ekībhūtānpure daityāndadāha viśikhāgninā  Bhmj 
 tripuravadhopākhyānam    
iti rudrasya dhātāpi svayaṃ sūto 'bhavatpurā 
tathā tvamapi karmasya nāvajñāṃ kartumarhasi  Bhmj 
aiśvaryasyābhijanyasya guṇānāṃ vikramasya ca 
santaḥ phalaṃ hi manyante mitrapraṇayapūraṇam  Bhmj 
ukte duryodhaneneti madrarājaḥ smitānanaḥ 
svacchandavādī sūto 'haṃ bhaviṣyāmītyabhāṣata  Bhmj 
 śalyasārathyasvīkāraḥ    
tataḥ svakalpitaiḥ śvetaisturaṅgairiva mātaliḥ 
taptakāñcanasaṃnāhe karṇasya rucire rathe  Bhmj 
jagrāha helayā śalyo raśmimamālāṃ suśikṣitām 
pratāpadhāmnaḥ sūryasya divīva garuḍāgrajaḥ  Bhmj 
yuyutsuṃ karṇamālokya dhanvinaṃ śalyasārathim 
jayaṃ hastagataṃ mene kururājaḥ sahānugaḥ  Bhmj 
nadastu kurusainyeṣu śaṅkhadundubhiniḥsvanaiḥ 
maurvīmāsphālayankarṇo madrarājamabhāṣata  Bhmj 
adya śakraḥ sutaṃ dṛṣṭvā mayā nihatamarjunam 
divyāstreṣu dṛśaḥ śaṅke sāsūyaṃ kṣipati kṣaṇāt  Bhmj 
adya madbāṇadalitaṃ pārthaṃ vīkṣya yudhiṣṭhiraḥ 
bhīṣmadroṇavadhāyāsavaiphalyātparitapyatām  Bhmj 
iti bruvāṇaṃ rādheyaṃ karṇa madrādhipo 'bravīt 
mā sūtaputra mithyaiva pralāpaṃ śiśuvatkṛthāḥ  Bhmj 
gandharvasamare pūrvaṃ tvayi dhīmati vidrute 
yena trailokyavīreṇa kauravāḥ parirakṣitāḥ  Bhmj 
mānināmāttaśastrabhāṇāṃ tasmingograhavigrahe 
yuṣmadvidhānāṃ sahasā hṛtā yenāṃśukāvalī  Bhmj 
ākhaṇḍalaḥ sānucaraḥ khāṇḍave yena nirjitaḥ 
taṃ samutsahase jetuṃ karṇa karṇo 'si kiṃ na vā  Bhmj 
iti śalyena sahasā śalyenevārdito muhuḥ 
saṃstambhya kopaṃ rādheyo drakṣyasīti tamabhyadhāt  Bhmj 
tato vyūheṣvanīkeṣu savyasāciraṇotsukaḥ 
agresaro rathāgryāṇāṃ karṇaḥ papraccha sainikān  Bhmj 
kvāsau jayadrathārātirvīro vānaraketanaḥ 
taṃ vinā rabhasādete tāmyantyeva mameṣavaḥ  Bhmj 
vidhatte locanapathaṃ mama yastūrṇamarjunam 
dadāmi tasmai vāsāṃsi citrāṇyābharaṇāni ca  Bhmj 
rathānsamattamātaṅgāngāḥ suvarṇaṃ purāṇi ca 
kāntāśca vividhaṃ cānyaddraviṇaṃ yāvadicchati  Bhmj 
etadākarṇya sotprāsaṃ babhāṣe madrabhūpatiḥ 
hāso duḥkhāya jāyeta rādheya tvadviceṣṭitaiḥ  Bhmj 
mūmūrṣuriva niṣputro draviṇaṃ cirasaṃbhṛtam 
avicāryaiva kiṃ mohātpareṣu tyaktumicchasi  Bhmj 
svayamarjunamāyāntaṃ vaśaḥ sitasaṭābharam 
draṣṭāsi bāṇanakharaiḥ kuraṅga iva kātaraḥ  Bhmj 
śrutvaitatkupitaḥ karṇo jagāda bhrukuṭīmukhaḥ 
nirguṇastvaṃ na jānīṣe na māṃ na ca dhanaṃjayam  Bhmj 
ahamevārjunaṃ jāne pratimallaṃ pinākinaḥ 
sa ca māṃ vetti tattvena tvaṃ mithyā tu pragalbhase  Bhmj 
ayaṃ mitropadhiḥ śatruḥ kenāpi preṣito 'si naḥ 
kudeśajasya vā naitattava kauṭilyamadbhutam  Bhmj 
madrakāḥ kutsitācārāḥ satyaśīlārjavojjhitāḥ 
mānaṃ pracakṣate loke sarvavedavido janāḥ  Bhmj 
nirlajjāḥ sarvagāminyo lobhamohamadākulāḥ 
anāryā madrikāyāstāḥ svasāro mātaraśca te  Bhmj 
rādheyeneti saṃrambhādbhāṣito madrabhūpatiḥ 
uvāca mūḍha bahuśo hitamuktaṃ na budhyase  Bhmj 
smartāsi vigaladdarpo nirjitaḥ savyasācinā 
hitopadeśavākyānāṃ śocannanuśayākulaḥ  Bhmj 
samudrakūlanilayaḥ purā vaiśyo mahādhanaḥ 
bahvapatyo 'bhavadrājñā rakṣito nirupadravaḥ  Bhmj 
tatputrāṇāmabhūtkākaḥ satatocciṣṭabhojanaḥ 
puṣṭāṅgo yo dhanāḍhyānāṃ praṇayī putravatpriyaḥ  Bhmj 
tataḥ kadācidāyātā haṃsā mānasagāminaḥ 
so 'vadajjātasaṃgharṣastulyā yūyaṃ na me jave  Bhmj 
jāne pātaśataṃ pūrṇaṃ viḍīnoḍḍīnabhedataḥ 
jñāyate tulyapātānme yuṣmākaṃ ca tarasvitā  Bhmj 
ityucchiṣṭabhujā tena rājahaṃsā vivalgitāḥ 
āhūtā vihasanto 'ntarvīrā no kiṃcidūcire  Bhmj 
ekastu haṃso jaladhau vrajāva iti saṃvidā 
kākena sārdhaṃ śaravannipapāta smitānanaḥ  Bhmj 
tena vātajavenābdhau vāyasaḥ khe vrajansamam 
sa ca krośārdhamātre 'bhūddhastapakṣo 'tivihvalaḥ  Bhmj 
tenaiva bhagnadarpo 'tha tīre nyastaḥ kṛpāvatā 
raktaṣṭhīvī cirātprāpa saṃjñāṃ jñātakhagāntaraḥ  Bhmj 
 haṃsakākīyam    
evaṃ tvamapi rādheya kauravaiḥ parivāritaḥ 
arjunaṃ samare prāpya hīnadarpo bhaviṣyasi  Bhmj 
śrutvaitanmadrarājena kathitaṃ marmadāraṇam 
ūce vaikartano vīraḥ pṛthukopo 'pyavikriyaḥ  Bhmj 
yuṣmadvidhānāṃ vacasā jāyate na bhayaṃ mama 
paśyārjunaṃ mayā śalya samare vinipātitam  Bhmj 
lakṣyābhyāse mayā vatsaḥ purā bāṇena dāritaḥ 
pracchannastarukhaṇḍena homadhenordvijanmanaḥ  Bhmj 
tacchāpādrathacakraṃ me paryantaṃ sādhayiṣyati 
ityetacchidramekaṃ me durjayo 'hamato 'nyathā  Bhmj 
tvaṃ tu kevalayānena nṛpeṇa vāhinā vṛtaḥ 
mayā svayaṃ saṃnikarṣe dvijihvaḥ kuṭilo vṛtaḥ  Bhmj 
madrakāṇāṃ kuvṛttānāṃ pāpadeśanivāsinām 
tīrthācāravihīnānāmadhipo 'si kimucyate  Bhmj 
abhakṣyaṃ bhuñjate nityamapātavyaṃ pibanti ye 
agamyāsu ramante ca te madrāstava bāndhavāḥ  Bhmj 
sarvasādhāraṇā yoṣāḥ sarvabhakṣyā dvijātayaḥ 
sarvapātakasaṃpṛktā madrā yeṣu bhavānnṛpaḥ  Bhmj 
 madrakutsanam    
iti karṇena gadite madrarājo 'pyabhāṣata 
karṇa rājā tvamaṅgānāṃ mātulyatyāgapāpinām  Bhmj 
vikrīyante sadā yatra dārāḥ putrāśca mānavaiḥ 
tasminpraṇetā viṣaye naivaṃ me vaktumarhasi  Bhmj 
 aṅgakutsanam    
ityukte madrarājena coditāste turaṅgamāḥ 
dārayanta iva kṣoṇīṃ jātapakṣā ivodyayuḥ  Bhmj 
athādṛśyata śubhrāśvo jiṣṇuracyutasārathiḥ 
agre samagrasainyānāṃ vyagraḥ śatrunibarhaṇe  Bhmj 
tataḥ pravṛtte vīrāṇāṃ samare prāṇahāriṇi 
dhanuṣāṃ kūjatāṃ śabde chādite tūryaniḥsvanaiḥ  Bhmj 
uvāca śalyo rādheyaṃ dhanaṃjayaraṇotsukam 
paśya gāṇḍīvadhanvānaṃ bhindānaṃ kuruvāhinīm  Bhmj 
ete saṃśaptakāḥ pārthaśaraśreṇīśatārditāḥ 
hatadvipaghaṭākūṭakuṭīṣu nibhṛtaṃ sthitāḥ  Bhmj 
paśyārjunaśarotkṛttakaṇṭhānāṃ mauktikāvalī 
patitā bhūmipālānāmaśrumālā iva śriyaḥ  Bhmj 
iti madrādhipenokte kva yāsyati dhanaṃjayaḥ 
madbāṇagocaraṃ yāto harṣādityavadannṛpaḥ  Bhmj 
tato vaikartanaśaraiḥ pūrite bhuvanodare 
cakampe pāṇḍavānīkaṃ vātairiva mahadvanam  Bhmj 
dhṛṣṭadyumnamukhānvīrānkurvāṇo virathānmuhuḥ 
ayutāni kṣaṇātkarṇo vīrāṇāmavadhīddaśa  Bhmj 
cedimāgadhapāñcālasainye bhagne yudhiṣṭhiraḥ 
mahārathairanugataḥ svaṃya rādheyamādravat  Bhmj 
rājavallabha vāgyuddhaṃ nedaṃ yatra pragalbhase 
ityuktvā dharmatanayaḥ śaraiḥ karṇamapūrayat  Bhmj 
atha karṇo narapateścakrarakṣau mahārathau 
hatvā śarairunmamātha dhvajaṃ cāpaṃ cakarta ca  Bhmj 
tataḥ śaktiṃ niśātāgrāṃ kāladaṃṣṭrāmivotkaṭām 
prāhiṇotsūtavṛtrāya sa nirhnādāṃ yudhiṣṭhiraḥ  Bhmj 
bhittvā vaikartanaḥ śaktiṃ tāṃ ghaṇṭākrandinīṃ muhuḥ 
jātarūpamayaṃ varma cakarta nṛpateḥ śaraiḥ  Bhmj 
muhūrtaṃ taṃ samāpūrya cchinnavarmāṇamāśugaiḥ 
dantavarṇāñjaghānāsya kṛṣṇabālāṃsturaṅgamān  Bhmj 
vidrutaṃ dharmatanayaṃ dṛṣṭvā krodhī vṛkodaraḥ 
karṇaṃ parāṅmukhaṃ cakre śarairaśanidāruṇaiḥ  Bhmj 
duryodhanānujānvīrānāyātānbhrāturajñayā 
vidadhe vipulairbāṇairbhīmaḥ pretapurāśrayān  Bhmj 
nandopanandau durdharṣaṃ dhanurgrāhaṃ mahābhujam 
jarāsaṃdhaṃ śrutarvāṇaṃ durdinaṃ saniṣaṅgiṇam  Bhmj 
pāśahastaṃ kavacinaṃ krodhaṃ ca sa mahābhujam 
hatvā praviśya pṛtanāṃ nāgānīkamapothayat  Bhmj 
tataḥ sātyakipāñcālamādrīsutayudhiṣṭhirāḥ 
ayodhayankarṇamukhyāṃstasminsubhaṭasaṃkṣaye  Bhmj 
vṛṣasenamukhairvīrairbhidyamāne balārṇave 
udabhūccaṇḍagāṇḍīvadhvanirāghaṭṭitāmbaraḥ  Bhmj 
hatvā saṃśaptakānsarvānpratyāvṛtte dhanaṃjaye 
āvartanartitevābhūtsahasā kuruvāhinī  Bhmj 
bhagne suśarmapramukhe pārthāstrairnṛpamaṇḍale 
karṇaḥ pāṇḍavasainyeṣu kālalīlāyitaṃ vyadhāt  Bhmj 
ayutāni gajendrāṇāṃ sapta hatvā tarasvinām 
tāvantyeva rathānāṃ ca cakre bhūmibhujāṃ kṣayam  Bhmj 
vyāghraketuṃ jayaṃ śaṅkuṃ rudramugrāyudhaṃ param 
siṃhasenaṃ rocamānaṃ śuktaṃ citrāyudhaṃ harim  Bhmj 
devāpiṃ jiṣṇukarmāṇaṃ śalabhaṃ citradaṇḍakau 
anyāṃśca bhūmipānhatvā pāñcālakadanaṃ vyadhāt  Bhmj 
vadhyamāne 'tha karṇena vidīrṇe ca sahasradhā 
babhūva pāṇḍave sainye ghoro vyatikaro navaḥ  Bhmj 
karṇena droṇaputreṇa kṛpeṇa kṛtavarmaṇā 
kirīṭinā pārṣatena śaineyena śikhaṇḍinā  Bhmj 
kupitena ca bhīmena te sene pravidārite 
babhūvaturviprakīrṇe vidhvastakavacāyudhe  Bhmj 
divyāstravarṣiṇaṃ drauṇiṃ divyāstreṇaiva phalguṇaḥ 
jitvā dadarśa karṇena vadhyamānānmahārathān  Bhmj 
tamabravīnmadhuripurvismitaḥ karṇāvikramāt 
paśya kaunteya karṇāstravahnidagdhāṃ varūthinīm  Bhmj 
paśya kālakaṭākṣeṇa lakṣitaḥ sūtanandanaḥ 
kaṭākṣeṇa madādhmātaḥ sārathye māṃ yadaikṣata  Bhmj 
kṣaṇena nikhilāṃ syūtāṃ tāṃ senāṃ kurute nṛpaḥ 
paśyāsya śarajālena nīrandhreṇāvṛtā diśaḥ  Bhmj 
ityuktvā keśavastūrṇaṃ rathena ghananādinā 
nināya śakratanayaṃ bhīmasenarathāntikam  Bhmj 
tato bhīmagirā dhyātvā dharmarājaṃ dhanaṃjayaḥ 
karṇasāyakanirbhinnaṃ prayātaṃ śibiraṃ śanaiḥ  Bhmj 
niḥśvasanbhṛśasaṃtaptaḥ kesarīva talāhataḥ 
prayayau bhrātaraṃ draṣṭuṃ saha kāliyavidviṣā  Bhmj 
sa hemapuṅkhairnirbhinnaṃ karṇanāmāṅkitaiḥ śaraiḥ 
śayānaṃ dharmatanayaṃ dadarśa cchinnakaṅkaṭam  Bhmj 
tasyābhivādya caraṇau niṣaṇṇau keśavārjunau 
cintāsaṃtāpitatanū tasthatuḥ kṣaṇamākulau  Bhmj 
yudhiṣṭhirastaṃ vijñāya karṇaṃ hatvā samāgatam 
utthāya nirvyatho harṣājjayādapṛthuvismayaḥ  Bhmj 
prāyo niḥśeṣitāḥ senā yenāstradahanena naḥ 
sa diṣṭyā samare pārtha tvayā karṇo nipātitaḥ  Bhmj
