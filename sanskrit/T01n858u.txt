pañcatathāgatastutigāthā
om namaḥ śrīpañcabhūtāntāya 
namaste varadarājāgra bhūtakoṭi namostu te 
namaste śūnyatāgarbha buddhabodhi namo'stu te   
buddharāga namaste'stu buddhakāma namo'stu te 
buddhaprīti namaste'stu buddhamoda namo namaḥ   
buddhasmita namastubhyaṃ buddhahāsa namo namaḥ 
buddhavāca namaste'stu buddhabhāva namo namaḥ   
abhavodbhava namaste'stu namaste buddhasaṃbhava 
gaganodbhava namaste'stu namaste jñānasaṃbhava   
māyājāla namastubhyaṃ namaste buddhanāyaka 
namaste sarvasarvebhyo jñānakāya namo'stu te 
śrī pañcatathāgatastutigāthā samāptā
