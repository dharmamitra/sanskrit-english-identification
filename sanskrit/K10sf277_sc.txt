Agrajñasūtra
Wal bhavati sa samayo yad aya lokaḥ savartate  savartamāne loke yadbhūyasā satvā
ābhāsvare devanikāye upapadyante  te tatra bhavanti rūpiṇo manomayā avikalā ahīnendriyāḥ
sarvāṅgapratya gopetāḥ śubhā varṇasthāyinaḥ svayaprabhā vaihāyasagāminaḥ prītibhakṣāḥ
prītyāhārā dīrghāyuṣo dīrgham adhvāna tiṣṭhanti 
Wal tena khalu samayena mahāpṛthivī ekodikā bhavati ekārṇavā  tasyā ekodikāyā mahāpṛthivyā
ekārṇavāyā vāyunā śaraḥ samūrcchitaḥ satanoti tadyathā pakvasya kṣīrasya śaraḥ
samūrcchitaḥ satanoty evam eva tasyā ekodikāyā mahāpṛthivyā ekārṇavāyā vayunā
śaraḥ samūrcchitaḥ satanoti  sa bhavati pṛthivīraso varṇasapanno gandhasapanno
rasasapannaḥ  tadyathā navanītam evavarṇaḥ  tadyathā kṣaudra madhv aneḍakam evamāsvādaḥ 
Wal bhavati sa samayo yad aya loko vivartate  vivartamāne loke satvā āyuḥkṣayāt karmakṣayāt
puṇyakṣayād ābhāsvarād devanikāyāc cavitvā ihatye manuṣyasabhāgatām āgaccanti  te
pi rūpiṇo pūrvavad yāvad dīrgham adhvānam tiṣṭhanti 
Wal tena khalu samayena na sūryacandramasau prajñāyete na rātridivasa na māsa ardhamāsa
na ṛtu na kṣaṇalavamuhūrta prajñāyante  na strī na pumān  satvaḥ satva ity ekasakhyā gacchati 
Wal athānyatamo lolupajātīyaḥ satvaḥ agulyagreṇa pṛthivīrasam āsvādayati  yathā yathāsvādayati
tathā tathā pratikākṣati  yathā yathā pratikākṣati tathā tathā kabaḍikāropakrameṇa praparibhuktaḥ 
Wal adrākṣur anyatame satvās ta satva kabaḍiṅkāropakrameṇa pṛthivīrasa paribhuñjānam 
dṛṣṭvā ca te pi satvā agulyāgreṇa pṛthivīrasam āsvādayanti  yathā yathāsvādayanti
pūrvavad yāvad kabaḍiṅkāropakrameṇa praparibhuktāḥ  yataś ca te satvā ta pṛthivīrasa
kabaḍiṅkāropakrameṇa praparibhuktās teṣā kāye dṛḍhatva gurutva prajñāyete śubhavarṇo
ntarhitaḥ  andhakāro loke prādurbhūtaḥ 
Wal dharmatā khalu vāsiṣṭha andhakārasya loke prādurbhāvāt sūryacandramasau prajñāyete etc as in
