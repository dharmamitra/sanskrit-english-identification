AgrajñasūtraWal bhavati sa samayo yad aya lokaḥ savartate  savartamāne loke yadbhūyasā satvāābhāsvare devanikāye upapadyante  te tatra bhavanti rūpiṇo manomayā avikalā ahīnendriyāḥsarvāṅgapratya gopetāḥ śubhā varṇasthāyinaḥ svayaprabhā vaihāyasagāminaḥ prītibhakṣāḥprītyāhārā dīrghāyuṣo dīrgham adhvāna tiṣṭhanti Wal tena khalu samayena mahāpṛthivī ekodikā bhavati ekārṇavā  tasyā ekodikāyā mahāpṛthivyāekārṇavāyā vāyunā śaraḥ samūrcchitaḥ satanoti tadyathā pakvasya kṣīrasya śaraḥsamūrcchitaḥ satanoty evam eva tasyā ekodikāyā mahāpṛthivyā ekārṇavāyā vayunāśaraḥ samūrcchitaḥ satanoti  sa bhavati pṛthivīraso varṇasapanno gandhasapannorasasapannaḥ  tadyathā navanītam evavarṇaḥ  tadyathā kṣaudra madhv aneḍakam evamāsvādaḥ Wal bhavati sa samayo yad aya loko vivartate  vivartamāne loke satvā āyuḥkṣayāt karmakṣayātpuṇyakṣayād ābhāsvarād devanikāyāc cavitvā ihatye manuṣyasabhāgatām āgaccanti  tepi rūpiṇo pūrvavad yāvad dīrgham adhvānam tiṣṭhanti Wal tena khalu samayena na sūryacandramasau prajñāyete na rātridivasa na māsa ardhamāsana ṛtu na kṣaṇalavamuhūrta prajñāyante  na strī na pumān  satvaḥ satva ity ekasakhyā gacchati Wal athānyatamo lolupajātīyaḥ satvaḥ agulyagreṇa pṛthivīrasam āsvādayati  yathā yathāsvādayatitathā tathā pratikākṣati  yathā yathā pratikākṣati tathā tathā kabaḍikāropakrameṇa praparibhuktaḥ Wal adrākṣur anyatame satvās ta satva kabaḍiṅkāropakrameṇa pṛthivīrasa paribhuñjānam dṛṣṭvā ca te pi satvā agulyāgreṇa pṛthivīrasam āsvādayanti  yathā yathāsvādayantipūrvavad yāvad kabaḍiṅkāropakrameṇa praparibhuktāḥ  yataś ca te satvā ta pṛthivīrasakabaḍiṅkāropakrameṇa praparibhuktās teṣā kāye dṛḍhatva gurutva prajñāyete śubhavarṇontarhitaḥ  andhakāro loke prādurbhūtaḥ Wal dharmatā khalu vāsiṣṭha andhakārasya loke prādurbhāvāt sūryacandramasau prajñāyete etc as in
