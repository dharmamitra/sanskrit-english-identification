rya Maitreyavyākaraṇaṃnamaḥ āryamaitreyāya śāriputro mahāprajñaḥ dharmasenāpatibibhuḥ lokānāmanukampāya śāstāraṃ paryapṛcchata  Mvyā sūtrāntare purā khyātaṃ yaṃ lokanāyakasya ca buddhasyānāgatasya hi maitreyanāma śāsanaṃ  Mvyā vyākhyāhi tadvalaṃ cāpi ṛddhiṃ sarvārthavardhanaṃ śrotumicchāma eva ca nāyakasya narottama  Mvyā tacchrutvā bhagavānāha śṛṇu naravarasya tvaṃ tasya maitreyabuddhasya vibhavaṃ vyākṛtaṃ mayā  Mvyā śukṣyanti ca tadārnavāḥ samantāt bahuyojanāḥ pratipādyā bhaviṣyanti mārgāśca cakravartinaḥ  Mvyā jambudvīpaṃ samantataḥ āyatanaṃ tadāhi ca āvāsaṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ daśasahasrayojanam  Mvyā narāstaddeśikāśca hi bhaviṣyanti śubhaṅkarāḥ ahiṃsakāśca nirdaṇḍyāḥ susamṛddhāśca subhagāḥ  Mvyā niṣkaṇṭakaśca bhūsthalaṃ samatalaṃ hi śyāmalaṃ unnamāvanamaśritaṃ mṛdutūla picopamaṃ  Mvyā gandhaśāli janiṣyate kṛṣṭimṛte ca madhuraṃ nānāvarṇābhilaṅkṛtā bhaviṣyanti cailadrumāḥ  Mvyā drumāśca krośavistṛtā patrapuṣpaphalānatāḥ sahasrāśītimātraśca āyustadā bhaviṣyati  Mvyā varṇavantaḥ bhaviṣyanti balavanto mahākāyāḥ sattvāḥ dakṣāśca niṣkleśā nirdoṣā dirghajīvinaḥ  Mvyā rogatrayaṃ bhaviṣyati kāmojarā gnimāndyañca pañcaśatatame varṣe pariṇītā ca dārikā  Mvyā tadā ketumatī nāma purī tatra bhaviṣyati sattvānāñca nivāsanaṃ prāṇināṃ hitakāriṇāṃ  Mvyā dīrghā dvādaśayaujana sapta vistāraśo hyasau nagaraṃ puṇyavacca tat viśuddhañca manoramaṃ  Mvyā saptaratnamayāḥ prāṃśuprākārāḥ krośavistṛtāḥ nānāratnavibhūṣitagopuratoraṇānyapi  Mvyā iṣṭakairnimitāśca te ratnamayairbhaviṣyanti padmotpalasamācchanāḥ parikhā haṃsaśobhitā  Mvyā mālairhi pariveṣṭitaṃ saptatālaismamantataḥ catūratnamayāstālāḥ kiṅkinījālaśobhitāḥ  Mvyā tattālebhyonilājjātaḥ śabdaścaiva manoramaḥ sumadhuro yathā tūryaṃ tacca pañcāṅgasaṃyutaṃ  Mvyā nagare smin narāśca ye viśrāmasukhakāminaḥ prahṛṣṭābhirabhiṣyante tālaśabdaiśca te tadā  Mvyā nagaramapi tatkṛtamutpalakumudākīrṇaṃ taḍāgopavanodyānaṃ trayametadbhaviṣyati  Mvyā śaṅkhonāma nṛpastatra mahātejā bhaviṣyati caturdvīrpādhipeśvaraścakravarttī mahābalaḥ  Mvyā saptaratnasamanvitaścaturaṅgabalādhipaḥ sahasraṃ hi janiṣyante putrāstadāsya bhūpateḥ  Mvyā pṛthivīṃ sāgarāntāñca sa paripālayiṣyati yathādharmadaṇḍeṇa narādhipo hi tadyathā  Mvyā caturmahānidhistadā śaṅkhākhyasya ca bhūpateḥ ratnānāṃ śatakoṭīnāṃ rājñastadā hi lokyate  Mvyā piṅgalaśca kaliṅgeṣu mithilāyām ca pāṇḍukaḥ elapatraśca gāndhāre śaṃkho vārāṇasīpure  Mvyā caturbhirebhinidhibhissa rājā susamanvitaḥ bhaviṣyati mahāvīraḥ śatapuṇyabaloditaḥ  Mvyā brāhmaṇastasya rājñāśca subrahmaṇaḥ purohitaḥ bahuśrutaścaturvedastasyopādhyāyo bhaviṣyati  Mvyā adhyāpako mantradharaḥ smṛtimān vedapāragaḥ kaiṭabhe sanighaṇṭe ca padavyākaraṇe tathā  Mvyā tadā brahmāvatī nāma tasya bhāryā bhaviṣyati darśanīyā prāsādikā abhirūpā yaśasvinī  Mvyā tuṣitebhyaścayavitvā tu maitreyo hyagrapugdalaḥ tasyāḥ kukṣau sa niyataṃ pratisandhiṃ grahīṣyati  Mvyā daśamāsāṃśca nikhilāṃ dhārayitvā mahādyūtiṃ supuṣpitesminnudyāne maitreyajananī tataḥ  Mvyā na niṣaṇṇā nipannā vā sthitā sā dharmacāriṇī drumasya śākhāmālamvya maitreyaṃ janayiṣyati  Mvyā niṣkramiṣyati pārśvena dakṣiṇena narottamaḥ abhrakūṭādyathā sūryo nirgataśca prabhāvyate  Mvyā alipto garbhapaṅkena kuśeśayamivāmvunā traidhātukamidaṃ sarvaṃ prabhayā pūrayiṣyati  Mvyā prīto tha taṃ sahasrākṣo devarājā śacīpatiḥ jāyamānaṃ grahītā ca maitreyaṃ dvipadottamam  Mvyā padāni jātamātraśca saptāsau prakamiṣyati pade pade nidhānañca padmaṃ padmaṃ bhaviṣyati  Mvyā diśaścatasraścodvīkṣya vācaṃ pravyāhariṣyati iyaṃ me paścimā jāti nāsti bhūyaḥ punarbhavaḥ na punarabhā gamiṣyāmi nirvāsyāmi nirāsravaḥ  Mvyā saṃsārārṇavamagnānāṃ sattvānāṃ duḥkhabhāgināṃ tṛṣṇābandhanabaddhānāṃ kariṣyāmi vimocanam  Mvyā śvetaṃ cāsya surāśchatraṃ dhārayiṣyanti mūrdhani śītoṣṇavāridhārābhyāṃ nāgendrau snāpayiṣyataḥ  Mvyā pratigṛhya ca taṃ dhātrī dvātriṃśadvaralakṣaṇam śriyā jvalantaṃ maitreyaṃ mātre samupaneṣyati  Mvyā manoramāṃ ca śivikāṃ nānāratnavibhūṣitāṃ ārūḍhāṃ putrasahitāṃ vahiṣyanti ca devatā  Mvyā tatastūrya sahasreṣu vādyamāneṣu tatpuraṃ praviṣṭamātre maitreye puṣpavarṣaṃ patiṣyati  Mvyā dṛṣṭaivaṃ putraṃ subrahmā dvātriṃśadvaralakṣaṇam pratyavīkṣātha mantreṣu tadā prīto bhaviṣyati  Mvyā gatidvayaṃ kumārasya yathā mantreṣu dṛśyate narādhipaścakravarttī buddho vā dvipadottamaḥ  Mvyā sa ca yauvanasaṃprāpto maitreyaḥ puruṣottamaḥ cintayiṣyati dharmātmā duḥkhitā khalviyaṃ prajāḥ  Mvyā brahmakharo mahāghoṣo hemavarṇo mahādyutiḥ viśālavakṣaḥ pīnāṃsaḥ padmapatranibhekṣaṇaḥ  Mvyā hastaḥ pañcāśaducchrāya tasya kāyo bhaviṣyati visṛtaśca tato rddheṇa śubhavarṇasamucchrayaḥ  Mvyā aśītibhiścaturbhiśca sahasraiḥ saṃpuraskṛtaḥ mānavānāṃ sa maitreyo mantrānadhyāpayiṣyati  Mvyā atha śaṃkho narapatiḥ yūpamucchrāpayiṣyati tiryañca ṣoḍaśavyāmaṃ ūrddhva vyāmasahasrakam  Mvyā sa taṃ yūpaṃ narapatirnānāratnavibhūṣitaṃ pradāsyati dvijātibhyo yajñaṃ kṛtvā puraḥsaraṃ  Mvyā tañca ratnamayaṃ yūpaṃ dattamātraṃ manoramaṃ brāhmaṇāṇāṃ sahasrāṇi vikiriṣyanti tatkṣaṇāt  Mvyā yūpasyatasya maitreyo dṛṣṭvā caitāmanityatāṃ kṛtasraṃ vicintya saṃsāraṃ pravrajyāṃ rocayiṣyati  Mvyā yatvahaṃ pravrajitveha spṛśeyamamṛtaṃ padaṃ vimocayeyaṃ janatāṃ vyādhimṛtyujarābhayāt  Mvyā aśītibhiḥ sahasraissa caturbhiśca puraskṛtaḥ niṣkramiṣyati maitreyaḥ pravrajyāmagrapugdalaḥ  Mvyā nāgavṛkṣastadā tasya bodhivṛkṣo bhaviṣyati pañcāśadyojanānyasya śākhā ūrddhaṃ samucchritāḥ  Mvyā niṣadya tasya cādhastānmaitreyaḥ puruṣottamaḥ anuttarāṃ śivāṃ bodhiṃ samavāpsyati nāyakaḥ  Mvyā yasyāmeva ca rātrau sa pravrajyāṃ niṣkramiṣyati tasyāṃ eva ca rātrau hi parāṃ bodhimavāpsyati  Mvyā aṣṭāṅgopetayā vācā tataḥ sa puruṣottamaḥ deśayiṣyati saddharmaṃ sarvaduḥkhāpahaṃ śivam  Mvyā prasannāṃ janatāṃ dṛṣṭvā satyāni kathayiṣyati duḥkhaṃ duḥkhasamutpādaṃ duḥkhasya samatikramaṃ  Mvyā āryaṃ cāṣṭāṅgikaṃ mārgaṃ kṣemaṃ nirvāṇagāminaṃ taṃ cāpi dharmaṃ saṃśrutya pratipatsyanti śāsane  Mvyā udyāne puṣpasaṃcchanne sannipāto bhaviṣyati pūrṇaṃ ca yojanaśataṃ parṣattasya bhaviṣyati  Mvyā śrutvā narapati rājā śaṅkho nāma mahāyaśāḥ datvā dānamasaṃkhyeyaṃ pravrajyāṃ niṣkramiṣyati  Mvyā aśītibhiścaturbhiśca sahasraiḥ parivāritaḥ narādhipo viniṣkramya pravrajyāmupayāsyati  Mvyā anenaiva pramāṇena mānavānāṃ puraskṛtaḥ maitreyasya pitā tatra pravrajyāṃ niṣkramiṣyati  Mvyā tato gṛhapatistatra sudhano nāma viśrutaḥ pravrajiṣyati śuddhātmā maitreyasyānuśāsane  Mvyā strīratnamatha śaṅkhasya viśākhā nāma viśrutā aśītibhiścaturbhiśca sahasraiḥ saṃpuraṣkṛtā nārīṇāmabhiniṣkramya pravrajyāṃ rocayiṣyati  Mvyā prāṇinaḥ tatra samaye sahasrāṇi śatāni ca pravrajyāmupayāsyanti maitreyasyānuśāsane  Mvyā supuṣpite sminnudyāne sannipāto bhaviṣyati samantato yojanaśataṃ parṣat tasya bhaviṣyati  Mvyā tataḥ kāruṇikaḥ śāstā maitreyaḥ puruṣottamaḥ samitiṃ vyavalokyātha imamarthaṃ pravakṣayati  Mvyā sarvete śākyasiṃhena guṇiśreṣṭheṇa trāyinā arthato lokanāthena dṛṣṭvā saddharmadhātunā ropitā mokṣamārgeṇa vikṣiptā mama śāsane  Mvyā chatradhvajapatākābhirgandhamālyavilepanaiḥ kṛtvā stūpeṣu satkāraṃ āgatā hi mamāntikam  Mvyā saṃghe datvā ca dānāni cīvaraṃ pānabhojanaṃ vividhaṃ glānabhaiṣajyaṃ āgatā hi mamāntikam  Mvyā kuṃkumodakasekaṃ ca candanenānulepanaṃ datvā śākyamuneḥ stūpeṣvāgatā hi mamāntikam  Mvyā śikṣāpadāni cādhāya śākyasiṃhasya śāsane paripālaya yathābhūtaṃ āgatā hi mamāntikam  Mvyā upoṣadhaṃ upoṣyeha āryamaṣṭāṅgikaṃ śubhaṃ caturdaśīṃ pañcadaśīṃ pakṣasyehāṣṭamīṃ tathā prātihārikapakṣaṃ cāṣyaṣṭāṅgaṃ susamāhitaṃ  Mvyā śīlāni ca samādāya saṃprāptāni ca śāsanam buddhaṃ dharmeṃ ca saṃghaṃ ca sattvāste śāsanaṃ gatāḥ kṛtvā ca kuśalaṃ karma macchāsanamupāgatāḥ  Mvyā tenaite preṣitāḥ sattvā pratiṣṭāśca mayāpyamī gaṇiśreṣṭheṇa muninā parītā bhūrimedhasā  Mvyā prasannāṃ janatāṃ dṛṣṭvā satyāni kathayiṣyati śrutvā ca te tato dharmaṃ prāpsyanti padamuttamam  Mvyā prātihāryatrayeṇāsau śrāvakānvinayiṣyati sarvete āsravāstatra kṣipayiṣyanti suratāḥ  Mvyā prathamaḥ sannipātosya śrāvakāṇāṃ bhaviṣyati pūrṇāḥ ṣaṇṇavatikoṭyaḥ śrāvakāṇāṃ bhavacchidāṃ  Mvyā dvitīyaḥ sannipātosya śrāvakāṇāṃ bhaviṣyati pūrṇāścaturnavati koṭyaḥ śāntānāṃ bhūrimedhasāṃ  Mvyā tṛtīyaḥ sannipātosya śrāvakāṇāṃ bhaviṣyati pūrṇāḥ dvāviṃśati koṭyaḥ śāntānāṃ śāntacetasāṃ  Mvyā dharmacakraṃ pravartyātha vinīya suramānuṣān sārdhaṃ śrāvakasaṃgheṇa pure piṇḍaṃ cariṣyati  Mvyā tataḥ praviśatastasyāṃ ramyāṃ ketumatīṃ purīṃ māndārakāṇi puṣpāṇi patiṣyanti purottame devatāḥ prakiriṣyanti tasmin puragate munau  Mvyā catvāraśca mahārājā śakraśca tridaśādhipaḥ brahmā devagaṇaiḥ sārdhaṃ pūjāṃ tasya kariṣyati  Mvyā gandharvā yakṣarākṣasāḥ śāstuḥ pūjāṃ kariṣyanti nāgāścāpi maharddhikāḥ  Mvyā te vai nūnaṃ bhaviṣyanti cyānaghācchinnasaṃśayāḥ utkṣiptaparikhāḥ dhīrā anādānā nirutsakāḥ brahmacaryañcariṣyanti
