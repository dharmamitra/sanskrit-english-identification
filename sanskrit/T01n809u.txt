uddhatadhūmradhūmadhūlidhutabaddhaghanāndhakārakaṃ
tvannatinītigatireti śamaṃ bahuśo hutāśanaḥ

bhagavati bhaktivantumupasarpati tava na vane'pi taskaraḥ   
yo'pi narendravīrahuṅkārakacagrahanigraho
grahagrasta iva rajjuhiñjīravajarjaritāṅgapañjaraḥ 
pratipadakhanakhanāyamānamukharīkṛtakharakharaśṛṅkhalāvali
stvaccaraṇāravindamabhivandya sa nandati muktabandhanaḥ

tatkṣaṇalabdhalolakiraṇamaṇikuṇḍalamaṇḍitagaṇḍamaṇḍalā
