ānandeta /
 ānandavardhanasya yo vivekaḥ vivicyatattadarthāvabodhaḥ tena vikāsī prakāśamāno yaḥ kāvyalokastasya yānyarthatatvāni sārārthāḥ teṣāṃ ghaṭanātsaṃyojanāddhetoḥ 
anumeyassāra utkarṣo yasya tat,
kiñca yat pronmiṣat sahṛdayeṣu prakāśamānamatha ca prakarṣeṇa unmiṣat sat sakalānāṃ
sarveṣāṃ sarveṣāṃ kāvyādīnāṃ sadviṣayān sārārthān sakalān sadviṣayānvā atha ca sakalānāṃ
