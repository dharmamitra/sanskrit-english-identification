śrīsiṃhabhūpālaviracito
rasārṇavasudhākaraḥ 
prathamo vilāsaḥ
rañjakollāsaḥ
śṛṅgāravīrasauhārdaṃ maugdhyavaiyātyasaurabham 
lāsyatāṇḍavasaujanyaṃ dāmpatyaṃ tad bhajāmahe SRs
vīṇāṅkitakarāṃ vande vānīm eṇīdṛśaṃ sadā 
sadānandamayīṃ devīṃ sarojāsanavallabhām SRs
asti kiñcit paraṃ vastu paramānandakandalam 
kamalākucakāṭhinyakutūhalibhujāntaram SRs
tasya pādāmbujāj jāto varṇo vigatakalmaṣaḥ 
yasya sodaratāṃ prāptaṃ bhagīrathatapaḥphalam SRs
tatra recarlavaṃśābdhiśaradrākāsudhākaraḥ 
kalānidhir udāraśrīr āsīd dācayanāyakaḥ SRs
yasyāsidhārāmārgeṇa durgeṇāpi raṇāṅgaṇe 
pāṇḍyarājagajānīkāj jayalakṣmīr upāgatā SRs
aṅganārāyaṇe yasmin bhavati śrīr atisthirā 
bhūr abhūt kariṇī vaśyā duṣṭarājagajāṅkuśe SRs
tasya bhāryā mahābhāgyā viṣṇoḥ śrīr iva viśrutā 
pocamāmbā guṇodārā jātā tāmarasānvayāt SRs
tayor abhūvan kṣitikalpavṛkṣāḥ
putrās trayas trāsitavairivīrāḥ 
siṃhaprabhur vennamanāyakaś ca
vīrāgraṇī recamahīpatiś ca SRs
kalāv ekapado dharmo yair ebhiś caraṇair iva 
sampūrṇapadatāṃ prāpya nākāṅkṣati kṛtaṃ yugam SRs
tatra siṃhamahīpāle pālayaty akhilāṃ mahīm 
namatām unnatiś citraṃ rājñām anamatāṃ natiḥ SRs
kṛṣṇaileśvarasaṃnidhau kṛtamahāsambhārameleśvare
vītāpāyam anekaśo vidadhatā brahmapratiṣṭhāpanam 
ānṛṇya samapādi yena vibhunā tattadguṇair ātmano
nirmāṇātiśayaprayāsagarimavyāsaṅgini brahmaṇi SRs
kṛtāntajihvākuṭilāṃ kṛpāṇīṃ
dṛṣṭvā yadīyāṃ trasatām arīṇām 
svedodayaś cetasi saṃcitānāṃ
mānoṣmaṇām ātanute praśāntim SRs
śrīmān recamahīpatiḥ sucarito yasyānujanmā sphuṭaṃ
prāpto vīraguruprathāṃ pṛthutarāṃ vīrasya mudrākarīm 
labdhvā labdhakaṭhārirāyavirudaṃ rāhuttarāyāṅkitaṃ
putraṃ nāgayanāyakaṃ vasumatīvīraikacūḍāmaṇim SRs
so' yaṃ siṃhamahīpālo vasudeva iti sphuṭam 
anantamādhavau yasya tanūjau lokarakṣakau SRs
tatrānujo mādhavanāyakendro
digantarālaprathitapratāpaḥ 
yasyābhavan vaṃśakarā narendrās
tanūbhavā vedagirīndramukhyāḥ SRs
tasyāgrajanmā bhuvi rājadoṣair
aprotabhāvād anapotasaṃjñām 
khyātāṃ dadhāti sma yathārthabhūtām
anantasajñāṃ ca mahīdharatvam SRs
sodaryo balabhadramūrtir aniśaṃ devī priyā rukmiṇī
pradyumnas tanayo' p pautranivaho yasyāniruddhādayaḥ 
so' yaṃ śrīpatir annapotanṛpatiḥ kiṃ cānanāmbhoruhe
dhatte cārusudarśanaśriyam asau satvātmahastāmbuje SRs
bahusomasutaṃ kṛtvā bhūlokaṃ yatra rakṣati 
ekasomasutaṃ rakṣan svarlokaṃ lajjate hariḥ SRs
somakulaparaśurāṃe
bhujabalabhīme' rigāyagobāle 
yatra ca jāgrati śāsati
jagatāṃ jāgarti nityakalyāṇam SRs
hemādridānair dharaṇīsurāṇāṃ
hemācalaṃ hastagataṃ vidhāya 
yaś cārusopānapathena cakre
śrīparvataṃ sarvajanāṅghrigamyam SRs
yo naikavīroddalano' py asaṅkhya
saṅkhyo' py abhagnātmagatikramo' pi 
ajātisāṅkaryabhavo' pi citraṃ
dadhāti somānvayabhārgavāṅkam SRs
dhāvaṃ dhāvaṃ ripunṛpatayo yuddharaṅgāpaviddhāḥ
khaḍge khaḍge phalitavapuṣaṃ yaṃ purastād vilokya 
pratyāvṛttā api tata ito vīkṣamāṇā yadīyaṃ
saṃmanyante sphuṭam avitathaṃ khaḍganārāyaṇāṅkam SRs
annamāmbeti vikhyātā tasyāsīd dharaṇīpateḥ 
devī śivā śivasyeva rājamauler mahojjvalā SRs
śatrughnaṃ śrutakīrtir yā subhadrā yaśasārjunam 
ānandayati bhartāraṃ śyāmā rājānam ujjvalam SRs
tayor abhūtāṃ putrau dvāv ādyo vedagirīśvaraḥ 
dvitīyas tv advitīyo' sau yaśasā siṃhabhūpatiḥ SRs
atha śrīsiṃhabhūpālo dīrghāyur vasudhām imām 
nijāṃsapīṭhe nirvyājaṃ kurute supratiṣṭhitām SRs
ahīnajyābandhaḥ kanakaruciraṃ kārmukavaraṃ
balidhvaṃsī bāṇaḥ parapuram anekaṃ ca viṣayaḥ 
iti prāyo lokottarasamarasaṃnāhavidhinā
maheśo' yaṃ siṃhakṣitipa iti yaṃ jalpati janaḥ SRs
yatra ca raṇasaṃnahini
tṛṇacaraṇaṃ nijapurāc ca niḥsaraṇam 
vanacaraṇaṃ taccaraṇaka
paricaraṇaṃ vā virodhināṃ śaraṇam SRs
satāṃ prītiṃ kurvan kuvalayavikāsaṃ viracayan
kalāḥ kāntāḥ puṣṇan dadhad api ca jaivātṛkakathām 
nitāntaṃ yo rājā prakaṭayati mitrodayam aho
tathā cakrānandān api ca kamalollāsasuṣamām SRs
tallabdhāni ghanāghanair atitarāṃ vārāṃ pṛṣanty ambudhau
svātyām eva hi śuktikāsu dadhate muktāni muktātmatām 
yad dānodakavipruṣas tu sudhiyāṃ haste patantyo' bhavan
māṇikyāni mahāmbarāṇi bahuśo dhāmāni hemāni ca SRs
nayanam ayaṃ guṇam aguṇaṃ
padam apadaṃ nijam avetya ripubhūpāḥ 
yasya ca nayaguṇaviduṣo
vinamanti padāravindapīṭhāntam SRs
prāṇānāṃ parirakṣaṇāya bahuśo vṛttiṃ madīyāṃ gatās
tvatsāmantamahībhujaḥ karuṇayā te rakṣaṇīyā iti 
karṇe varṇayituṃ nitāntasuhṛdo karṇāntaviśrāntayor
manye yasya dṛgantayoḥ parisaraṃ sā kāmadhenuḥ śritā SRs
yuṣmābhiḥ pratigaṇḍabhairavaraṇe prāṇāḥ kathaṃ rakṣitā
ity antaḥpurapṛcchayā yad ariṣu prāpteṣu lajjāvaśam 
śaṃsanty uttaramānanavyatikaravyāpārapāraṅgatā
gaṇḍāndolitakarṇakuṇḍalaharinmāṇikyavarṇāṅkurāḥ SRs
mandārapārijātaka
candanasantānakalpamaṇisadṛśaiḥ 
anapotadācavallabha
vedagirisvāmimādadāmayasaṃjñaiḥ SRs
ātmabhavair ativibhavair
anitarajanasulabhadānamuditair bhuvi yaḥ 
ratnākara iva rājati
rājakarāracitasukamalollāsaḥ SRs
yasyāḍhyaḥ prathamaḥ kumāratilakaḥ śrīannapoto guṇair
ekasyāgrajam ātmarūpavibhave cāpe dvayor agrajam 
ārūḍhe tritayāgrajaṃ vijayate durvāradorvikrame
satyoktau caturagrajaṃ vitaraṇe kiṃ cāpi pañcāgrajam SRs
yuddhe yasya kumāradācayavibhoḥ khaḍgāgradhārājale
majjanti pratipakṣabhūmipatayaḥ śauryoṣmasantāpitāḥ 
citraṃ tatpramadāḥ pranaṣṭatilakā vyākīrṇanīlālakāḥ
prabhraśyatkucakuṅkumāḥ parigalannetrāntakālāñjanāḥ SRs
paripoṣiṇi yasya putraratne
dayite vallabharāyapūrṇacandre 
samudeti satāṃ prabhāvaśeṣaḥ
kamalānām abhivardhanaṃ tu citram SRs
etair anyaiś ca tanayaiḥ so' yaṃ siṃhamahīpatiḥ 
ṣaḍbhiḥ pratiṣṭhām ayate svāmīvāṅgaiḥ susaṅgataiḥ SRs
rājā sa rājācalanāmadheyām
adhyāsta vaṃśakramarājadhānīm 
satāṃ ca rakṣām asatāṃ ca śikṣāṃ
nyāyānurodhād anusandadhānajñāḥ SRs
vindhyaśrīśailamadhyakṣmāmaṇḍalaṃ pālayan sutaiḥ 
vaṃśapravartakair arthān bhuṅkte bhogapurandaraḥ SRs
tasmin śāsati siṃhabhūmiramaṇe kṣmām annapotātmaje
kāṭhinyaṃ kucamaṇḍale taralatā netrāñcale subhruvām 
vaiṣamyaṃ trivalīṣu mandapadatā līlālasāyāṃ gatau
kauṭilyaṃ cikureṣu kiṃ ca kṛśatā madhye paraṃ badhyate SRs
so' haṃ kalyāṇarūpasya varṇotkarṣaikakāraṇam 
vidvatprasādanāhetor vakṣye nāṭyasya lakṣaṇam SRs
purā purandarādyās te praṇamya caturānanam 
kṛtāñjalipuṭā bhūtvā papracchuḥ sarvavedinam SRs
bhagavan śrotum icchāmaḥ śrāvyaṃ dṛśyaṃ manoharam 
dharmyaṃ yaśasyam arthyaṃ ca sarvaśilpapradarśanam SRs
paraṃ pañcamam āmnāyaṃ sarvavarṇādhikārikam 
iti pṛṣṭaḥ sa tair brahmā sarvavedān anusmaran SRs
tebhyaś ca sāram ādāya nāṭyavedam athāsṛjat 
adhyāpya bharatācāryaṃ prajāpatir abhāṣata SRs
saha putrair imaṃ vedaṃ prayogeṇa prakāśaya 
iti tena niyuktas tu bharataḥ saha sūnubhiḥ SRs
prāyojayat sudharmāyām indrasyāgre' psarogaṇaiḥ 
sarvalokopakārāya nāṭyaśāstraṃ ca nirmame SRs
tathā tadanusāreṇa śāṇḍilyaḥ kohalo' pi ca 
dattilaś ca mataṅgaś ca ye cānye tattanūdbhavāḥ SRs
granthān nānāvidhāṃś cakruḥ prakhyātās te mahītale 
teṣām atigabhīratvād viprakīrṇakramatvataḥ SRs
sampradāyasya vicchedāt tadvidāṃ viralatvataḥ 
prāyo viralasañcārā nāṭyapaddhatir asphuṭā SRs
tasmād asmatprayatno' yaṃ tatprakāśanalakṣaṇaḥ 
sāraikagrāhiṇāṃ cittam ānandayati dhīmatām SRs
nedānīntanadīpikā kim u tamaḥsaṅhātam unmūlayej
jyotsnā kiṃ na cakorapāraṇakṛte tatkālasaṃśobhinī 
bālaḥ kiṃ kamalākarān dinamaṇir nollāsayed añjasā
tat sampraty api mādṛśām api vacaḥ syād eva samprītaye SRs
svacchasvādurasādhāro vastucchāyāmanoharaḥ 
sevyaḥ suvarṇanidhivan nāṭyamārgaḥ sanāyakaḥ SRs
sāttvikādyair abhinayaiḥ prekṣakāṇāṃ yato bhavet 
naṭe nāyakatādātmyabuddhis tan nāṭyam ucyate SRs
rasotkarṣo hi nāṭyasya prāṇās tat sa nirūpyate 
vibhāvair anubhāvaiś ca sāttvikair vyabhicāribhiḥ SRs
ānīyamānaḥ svādutvaṃ sthāyī bhāvo rasaḥ smṛtaḥ 
tatra jñeyo vibhāvas tu rasajñāpanakāraṇam SRs
budhair jñeyo' yam ālamba uddīpana iti dvidhā 
ādhāraviṣayatvābhyāṃ nāyako nāyikāpi ca SRs
ālambanaṃ mataṃ tatra nāyako guṇavān pumān 
tadguṇās tu mahābhāgyam audāryaṃ sthairyadakṣate SRs
aujjvalyaṃ dhārmikatvaṃ ca kulīnatvaṃ ca vāgmitā 
kṛtajñatvaṃ nayajñatvaṃ śucitā mānaśālitā SRs
tejasvitā kalāvattvaṃ prajārañjakatādayaḥ 
ete sādhāraṇāḥ proktāḥ nāyakasya guṇā budhaiḥ SRs
sarvātiśāyirājyatvaṃ mahābhāgyam udāhṛtam 
yadviśrāṇanaśīlatvaṃ tad audāryaṃ budhā viduḥ SRs
vyāpāraṃ phalaparyantaṃ sthairym āhur manīṣiṇaḥ 
duṣkare kṣiprakāritvaṃ dakṣatāṃ paricakṣate SRs
aujjvalyaṃ nayanānandakāritvaṃ kathyate budhaiḥ 
dharmapravaṇacittatvaṃ dhārmikatvam itīryate SRs
kule mahati sambhūtiḥ kulīnatvam udāhṛtam 
vāgmitā tu budhair uktā samayocitabhāṣitā SRs
kṛtānām upakārāṇām abhjñatvaṃ kṛtajñatā 
sāmādyupāyacāturyaṃ nayajñatvam udāhṛtam SRs
antaḥkaraṇaśuddhir yā śucitā sā prakīrtitā 
akārpaṇyasahiṣṇutvaṃ kathitā mānaśālitā SRs
tejasvitvam avajñāder asahiṣṇutvam ucyate 
kalāvattvaṃ nigaditaṃ sarvavidyāsu kauśalam SRs
rañjakatvaṃ tu sakalacittāhlādanakāritā 
uktair guṇaiś ca sakalair yuktaḥ syād uttamo netā SRs
madhyaḥ katipayahīno bahuguṇahīno' dhamo nāma 
netā caturvidho' sau dhīrodāttaś ca dhīralalitaś ca SRs
dhīrapraśāntanāmā tataś ca dhīroddhataḥ khyātaḥ 
dayāvān atigambhīro vinītaḥ sattvasāravān SRs
dṛḍhavratas titikṣāvān ātmaślāghāparāṅmukhaḥ 
nigūḍhāhaṅkṛtir dhīrair dhīrodātta udāhṛtaḥ SRs
dayātiśayaśālitvaṃ dayāvattvam udāhṛtam 
gāmbhīryam avikāraḥ syāt saty api kṣobhakāraṇe SRs
niścinto dhīralalitas taruṇo vanitāvaśaḥ 
śamaprakṛtikaḥ kleśasahiṣṇuś ca vivecakaḥ SRs
lalitādiguṇopeto vipro vā sacivo vaṇik 
dhīraśāntaś cārudattamādhavādir udāhṛtaḥ SRs
mātsaryavān ahaṅkārī māyāvī roṣaṇaś calaḥ 
vikatthano bhārgavādir dhīroddhata udāhṛtaḥ SRs
ete ca nāyakāḥ sarvarasasādhāraṇāḥ smṛtāḥ 
śṛṅgārāpekṣayā teṣāṃ traividhyaṃ kathyate budhaiḥ SRs
patiś copapatiś caiva vaiśikaś ceti bhedataḥ 
patis tu vidhinā pāṇigrāhakaḥ kathyate budhaiḥ SRs
caturdhā so' pi kathito vṛttyā kāvyavicakṣaṇaiḥ 
anukūlaḥ śaṭho dhṛṣṭo dakṣiṇaś ceti bhedataḥ SRs
anukūlas tv ekajāniḥ śaṭho gūḍhāparādhakṛt 
dhṛṣṭo vyaktānyayuvatībhogalakṣmāpi nirbhayaḥ SRs
nāyikāsv apy anekāsu tulyo dakṣiṇa ucyate 
laṅghitācārayā yas tu vināpi vidhinā striyā SRs
saṅketaṃ nīyate prokto budhair upapatis tu saḥ 
dākṣiṇyam ānukūlyaṃ ca dhārṣṭyaṃ cāniyatatvataḥ SRs
nocitānyasya śāṭhyaṃ syād anyacittatvasambhavāt 
rūpavān śīlasampannaḥ śāstrajñaḥ priyadarśanaḥ SRs
kulīno matimān śūro ramyaveṣayuto yuvā 
adīnaḥ surabhis tyāgī sahanaḥ priyabhāṣaṇaḥ SRs
śaṅkavihīno mānī ca deśakālavibhāgavit 
dākṣyacāturyamādhuryasaubhāgyādibhir anvitaḥ SRs
veśyopabhogarasiko yo bhavet sa tu vaiśikaḥ 
kalakaṇṭhādiko lakṣyo bhāṇādāv eva vaiśikaḥ SRs
sa tridhā kathyate jyeṣṭhamadhyanīcavibhedataḥ 
atha śṛṅgāranetṝṇāṃ sāhāyyakaraṇocitāḥ SRs
nirūpyante pīṭhamardaviṭaceṭavidūṣakāḥ 
nāyakānucaro bhaktaḥ kiñcid ūnaś ca tadguṇaiḥ SRs
pīṭhamarda iti khyātaḥ kupitastrīprasādakaḥ 
kāmatantrakalāvedī viṭa ity abhidhīyate SRs
sandhānakuśalaś ceṭaḥ kalahaṃsādiko mataḥ 
vikṛtāṅgavacoveṣair hāsyakārī vidūṣakaḥ SRs
deśakālajñatā bhāṣāmādhuryaṃ ca vidagdhatā 
protsāhane kuśalatā yathoktakathanaṃ tathā SRs
nigūḍhamantratety ādyāḥ sahāyānāṃ guṇā matāḥ 
netṛsādhāraṇaguṇair upetā nāyikā matā SRs
svakīyā parakīyā ca sāmānyā ceti sā tridhā 
sampatkāle vipatkāle yā na muñcati vallabham SRs
śīlārjavaguṇopetā sā svakīyā kathitā budhaiḥ 
sā ca svīyā tridhā mugdhā madhyā prauḍheti kathyate SRs
mugdhā navavayaḥkāmā ratau vāmā mṛduḥ krudhi 
yatate rataceṣṭāyāṃ gūḍhaṃ lajjāmanoharam SRs
kṛtāparādhe dayite vīkṣate rudatī satī 
apriyaṃ vā priyaṃ vāpi na kiñcid api bhāṣate SRs
samānalajjāmadanā prodyattāruṇyaśālinī 
madhyā kāmayate kāntaṃ mohāntasuratakṣamā SRs
madhyā tridhā mānavṛtter dhīrādhīrobhayātmikā 
dhīrā tu vakti vakroktyā sotprāsaṃ sāgasaṃ priyam SRs
adhīrā paruṣair vākyaiḥ khedayed vallabhaṃ ruṣā 
dhīrādhīra tu vakroktyā sabāṣpaṃ vadati priyam SRs
sampūrṇayauvanonmattā pragalbhā rūḍhamanmathā 
dayitāṅge vilīneva yatate ratikeliṣu SRs
rataprārambhamātre' pi gacchaty ānandamūrcchatām 
mānavṛtteḥ pragalbhāpi tridhā dhīrādibhedataḥ SRs
udāste surate dhīrā sāvahitthā ca sādarā 
santarjya niṣṭhuraṃ roṣād adhīrā tāḍayet priyam SRs
dhīrādhīraguṇopetā dhīrādhīreti kathyate 
dvedhā jyeṣṭhā kaniṣṭheti madhyā prauḍhāpi tādṛśī SRs
dhīrādhīrādibhedena madhyāprauḍhe tridhā tridhā 
jyeṣṭhākaniṣṭḥābhedena tāḥ pratyekaṃ dvidhā dvidhā SRs
mugdhā tv ekavidhā caivaṃ sā trayodaśadhoditā 
anyāpi dvividhā kanyā paroḍhā ceti bhedataḥ SRs
tatra kanyā tv anūḍhā syāt salajjā pitṛpālitā 
sakhīkeliṣu visrabdhā prāyo mugdhāguṇānvitā SRs
pradhānam apradhānaṃ vā nāṭakādāv iyaṃ bhavet 
mālatīmādhave lakṣye mālatīmadayantike SRs
paroḍhā tu pareṇoḍhāpy anyasambhogalālasā 
lakṣyā kṣudraprabandhe sā saptaśatyādike budhaiḥ SRs
sādhāraṇastrī gaṇikā kalāprāgalbhyadhārṣṭyayuk 
eṣā syād dvividhā raktā viraktā ceti bhedataḥ SRs
tatra raktā tu varṇyā syād aprādhānyena nāṭake 
agnimitrasya vijñeyā yathā rājña irāvatī SRs
pradhānam apradhānaṃ vā nāṭaketararūpake 
sā ced divyā nāṭake tu prādhānyenaiva varṇyate SRs
viraktā tu prahasanaprabhṛtiṣv eva varṇyate 
tasyā dhauryaprabhṛtayo guāṣ tadupayoginaḥ SRs
channakāmān ratārthājñān bālapāṣaṇḍaṣaṇḍakān 
rakteva rañjayed ibhyān niḥsvān mātrā vivāsayet SRs
atra kecid āhuḥ
gaṇikāyā nānurāgo guṇavaty api nāyake 
rasābhāsaprasaṅgaḥ syād araktāyāś ca varṇane 
ataś ca nāṭakādau tu varṇyā sā na bhaved iti 
tathā cāhuḥ śṛti 
sāmānyā vanitā veśyā sā dravyaṃ param icchatā
