śrīdevī uvāca
anuttaraṃ kathaṃ deva sadyaḥkaulikasiddhidam 
yena vijñātamātreṇa khecarīsamatāṃ vrajet  ParTri 
etad guhyaṃ mahāguhyaṃ kathayasva mama prabho 
hṛdayasthā tu yā śaktiḥ kaulinī kulanāyikā  ParTri 
tāṃ me kathaya deveśa yena tṛptiṃ labhāmy aham 
śrībhairava uvāca
śṛṇu devi mahābhage uttarasyāpy anuttaram  ParTri 
yad ayaṃ kauliko vidhir mama hṛdvyomny avasthitaḥ 
kathayāmi na saṃdehaḥ sadyaḥ kaulikasiddhidam  ParTri 
athādyās tithayaḥ sarve svarā bindvavasānakāḥ 
tadantaḥ kālayogena somasūryau prakīrtitau  ParTri 
pṛthivyādīni tattvāni puruṣāntāni pañcasu 
kramāt kādiṣu vargeṣu makārānteṣu suvrate  ParTri 
vāyvagnisalilendrāṇāṃ dhāraṇānāṃ catuṣṭayam 
tadūrdhve śādivikhyātaṃ purastād brahmapañcakam  ParTri 
var cd ūrdvhaṃ
amūlā tatkramā jñeyā kṣāntā sṛṣṭir udāhṛtā 
sarveṣāṃ caiva mantrāṇāṃ vidyānāṃ ca yaśasvini  ParTri 
iyaṃ yoniḥ samākhyātā sarvatantreṣu sarvadā 
caturdaśayutaṃ bhadre tithīśāntasamanvitam  ParTri 
tṛtīyam brahma suśroṇi hṛdayam bhairavātmanaḥ 
etan nāyoginījāto nārudro labhate sphuṭam  ParTri 
hṛdayaṃ devadevasya sadyo yogavimokṣadam 
asyoccāre kṛte samyaṅ mantramudrāgaṇo mahān  ParTri 
sadyas tanmukhatām eti svadehāveśalakṣaṇam 
muhūrtaṃ smarate yas tu cumbakenābhimudritaḥ  ParTri 
sa badhnāti tadā dehaṃ mantramudrāgaṇaṃ naraḥ 
atītānāgatānarthān pṛṣṭo 'sau kathayaty api  ParTri 
praharād yad abhipretaṃ devatārūpam uccaran

praharadvayamātreṇa vyomastho jāyate smaran 
trayeṇa mātaraḥ sarvā yogeśvaryo mahābalāḥ  ParTri 
vīrā vīreśvarāḥ siddhā balavāñ chākinīgaṇaḥ 
āgatya samayaṃ dattvā bhairaveṇa pracoditāḥ  ParTri 
yacchanti paramāṃ siddhiṃ phalaṃ yad vā samīhitam 
anena siddhāḥ setsyanti sādhayanti ca mantriṇaḥ  ParTri 
yat kiṃcid bhairave tantre sarvam asmāt prasiddhyati 
adṛṣṭamaṇdalo 'py evaṃ yaḥ kaścid vetti tattvataḥ 
sa siddhibhāg bhaven nityaṃ sa yogī sa ca dīkṣitaḥ  ParTri 
anena jñātamātreṇa jñāyate sarvaśaktibhiḥ 
śākinīkulasāmānyo bhaved yogaṃ vināpi hi  ParTri 
avidhijño vidhānajño jāyate yajanaṃ prati  ParTri 
kālāgnim āditaḥ kṛtvā māyāntam brahmadehagam 
śivo viśvādyanantāntaḥ paraṃ śaktitrayaṃ matam  ParTri 
tadantar varti yat kiṃcic chuddhamārge vyavasthitam 
aṇur viśuddham acirād aiśvaraṃ jñānam aśnute  ParTri 
taccodakaḥ śivo jñeyaḥ sarvajñaḥ parameśvaraḥ 
sarvago nirmalaḥ svacchas tṛptaḥ svāyatanaḥ śuciḥ  ParTri 
yathā nyagrodhabījasthaḥ śaktirūpo mahādrumaḥ 
tathā hṛdayabījasthaṃ jagad etac carācaram  ParTri 
evaṃ yo vetti tattvena tasya nirvāṇagāminī 
dīkṣā bhavaty asaṃdigdhā tilājyāhutivarjitā  ParTri 
mūrdhni vaktre ca hṛdaye guhye mūrtau tathaiva ca 
nyāsaṃ krtvā śikhāṃ baddhvā saptaviṃśatimantritām  ParTri 
ekaikaṃ tu diśāṃ bandhaṃ daśānām api yojayet 
tālatrayam purā dattvā saśabdaṃ vighnaśāntaye  ParTri 
śikhāsaṃkhyābhijaptena toyenābhyukṣayet tataḥ 
puṣpādikaṃ kramāt sarvaṃ liṅge vā sthaṇḍile 'thavā  ParTri 
caturdaśābhijaptena puṣpeṇāsanakalpanā 
tatra sṛṣṭiṃ yajed vīraḥ punar evāsanaṃ tataḥ  ParTri

sarvatattvasusampūrṇām sarvābharaṇabhūṣitām  ParTri 
yajed devīṃ maheśānīṃ saptaviṃśatimantritām 
tataḥ sugandhipuṣpais tu yathāśaktyā samarcayet  ParTri
