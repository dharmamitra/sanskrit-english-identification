Viṃśikāvṛttiḥ Vvṛ
vijñaptimātramevedamasadarthāvabhāsanāt 
yadvat taimirikasyāsatkeśoṇḍrakādidarśanaṃ  Vvṛ 
na deśakālaniyamaḥ santānāniyamo na ca 
na ca kṛtyakriyā yuktā vijñaptiryadi nārthataḥ  Vvṛ 
deśādiniyamaḥ siddhaḥ svapnavat pretavatpunaḥ 
santānāniyamaḥ sarvaiḥ pūyanadyādidarśane  Vvṛ 
svapnopaghātavatkṛtyakriyā narakavatpunaḥ 
sarvaṃ narakapālādidarśane taiśca bādhane  Vvṛ 
tiraścāṃ sambhavaḥ svarge yathā ca narake tathā 
na pretānāṃ yatastajjaṃ duḥkhaṃ nānubhavanti te  Vvṛ 
yadi tatkarmabhistatra bhūtānāṃ sambhavastathā
iṣyate pariṇāmaśca kiṃ vijñānasya neṣyate  Vvṛ 
karmaṇo vāsanānyatra phalamanyatra kalpyate 
tatraiva neṣyate yatra vāsanā kiṃ nu kāraṇaṃ  Vvṛ 
rūpādyāyatanāstitvaṃ tadvineyajanaṃ prati 
abhiprāyavaśāduktamupapādukasattvavat  Vvṛ 
nāstīha satva ātmā vā dharmāstvete sahetukāḥ
yataḥ svabījādvijñaptiryadābhāsā pravartate 
dvividhāyatanatvena te tasyā munirabravīt  Vvṛ 
tathā pudgalanairātmyapraveśo hi anyathā punaḥ 
deśanā dharmanairātmyapraveśaḥ kalpitātmanā  Vvṛ 
na tadekaṃ na cānekaṃ viṣayaḥ paramāṇuśaḥ 
na ca te saṃhatā yasmātparamāṇurna sidhyati  Vvṛ 
ṣaṭkena yugapadyogātparamāṇoḥ ṣaḍaṃśatā 
ṣaṇṇāṃ samānadeśatvātpiṇḍaḥ syādaṇumātrakaḥ  Vvṛ 
paramāṇorasaṃyoge tatsaṅghāte 'sti kasya saḥ 
na cānavayavatvena tatsaṃyogād na sidhyati  Vvṛ 
dibhāgabhedo yasyāsti tasyaikatvaṃ na yujyate 
chāyāvṛtī kathaṃ vā anyo na piṇḍaścenna tasya te  Vvṛ 
ekatve na krameṇetiryugapanna grahāgrahau 
vicchinnānekavṛttiśca sūkṣmānīkṣā ca no bhavet  Vvṛ 
pratyakṣabuddhiḥ svapnādau yathā sa ca yadā tadā 
na so 'rtho dṛśyate tasya pratyakṣatvaṃ kathaṃ mataṃ  Vvṛ 
uktaṃ yathā tadābhāsā vijñaptiḥ smaraṇaṃ tataḥ 
svapnadṛgviṣayābhāvaṃ nāprabuddho 'vagacchati  Vvṛ 
anyonyādhipatitvena vijñaptiniyamo mithaḥ 
middhenopahataṃ cittaṃ svapne tenāsamaṃ phalaṃ  Vvṛ 
maraṇaṃ paravijñaptiviśeṣādvikriyā yathā 
smṛtilopādikānyeṣāṃ piśācādimanovaśāt  Vvṛ 
kathaṃ vā daṇḍakāraṇyaśūnyatvamṛṣikopataḥ 
manodaṇḍo mahāvadyaḥ kathaṃ vā tena sidhyati  Vvṛ 
paracittavidāṃ jñānamayathārthaṃ kathaṃ yathā
svacittajñānam ajñānādyathā buddhasya gocaraḥ  Vvṛ 
vijñaptimātratāsiddhiḥ svaśaktisadṛśī mayā 
kṛteyaṃ sarvathā sā tu na cintyā buddhagocaraḥ  Vvṛ 
viṃśatikā vijñaptimātratāsiddhiḥ 
kṛtiriyamācāryavasubandhoḥ
