Pratītyasamutpādahṛdayakārikā Pshknāgārjunakṛtādvādaśa ye ṅgaviśeṣā muninoddiṣṭāḥ pratītyasambhūtāḥ te kleśakarmaduḥkheṣu saṅgṛhītāstriṣu yathāvat  Pshk ādyāṣṭamanavamāḥ syuḥ kleśāḥ karma dvitīyadaśamau ca śeṣāḥ sapta ca duḥkhaṃ trisaṅgrahā dvādaśa tu dharmāḥ  Pshk tribhyo bhavati dvandvaṃ dvandvātprabhavanti sapta saptabhyaḥ traya udbhavanti bhūyastadevatu bhramati bhavacakram  Pshk hetuphalañcahisarvaṃ jagadanyo nāsti kaścidiha sattvaḥ śūnyebhya eva śūnyā dharmāḥ prabhavanti dharmebhyaḥ  Pshk svādhyāyadīpamudrādarpaṇaghoṣa rkakāntabījāmlaiḥ skandhapratisandhirasaṅkramaśca vidvadbhiravadhāryau  Pshk ya ucchedaṃ prakalpayatyatisūkṣme pi vastuni pratītyasambhavasyārthamavijñaḥ sa na paśyati  Pshk nāpaneyamataḥ kiñcit prakṣepyaṃ nāpi kiñcana draṣṭavyaṃ bhūtato bhūtaṃ bhūtadarśī vimucyate  Pshk pratītyasamutpādahṛdayakārikā ācārya nāgārjunakṛtā samāptā
