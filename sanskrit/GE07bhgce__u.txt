Bhga oṃ tat sad iti nirdeśo brahmaṇas trividhaḥ smṛtaḥ
Bhgc brāhmaṇās tena vedāś ca yajñāś ca vihitāḥ purā
Bhga tasmād om ity udāhṛtya yajñadānatapaḥkriyāḥ
Bhgc pravartante vidhānoktāḥ satataṃ brahmavādinām
Bhga tad ity anabhisaṃdhāya phalaṃ yajñatapaḥkriyāḥ
Bhgc dānakriyāś ca vividhāḥ kriyante mokṣakāṅkṣibhiḥ
Bhga sadbhāve sādhubhāve ca sad ity etat prayujyate
Bhgc praśaste karmaṇi tathā sacchabdaḥ pārtha yujyate

Bhgc karma caiva tadarthīyaṃ sad ity evābhidhīyate
Bhga aśraddhayā hutaṃ dattaṃ tapas taptaṃ kṛtaṃ ca yat
Bhgc asad ity ucyate pārtha na ca tat pretya no iha

Bhgc tyāgasya ca hṛṣīkeśa pṛthak keśiniṣūdana
Bhg śrībhagavān uvāca

Bhgc sarvakarmaphalatyāgaṃ prāhus tyāgaṃ vicakṣaṇāḥ
Bhga tyājyaṃ doṣavad ity eke karma prāhur manīṣiṇaḥ
Bhgc yajñadānatapaḥkarma na tyājyam iti cāpare
Bhga niścayaṃ śṛṇu me tatra tyāge bharatasattama
Bhgc tyāgo hi puruṣavyāghra trividhaḥ saṃprakīrtitaḥ
Bhga yajñadānatapaḥkarma na tyājyaṃ kāryam eva tat

Bhgc viṣādī dīrghasūtrī ca kartā tāmasa ucyate

Bhga yayā tu dharmakāmārthān dhṛtyā dhārayate 'rjuna
Bhgc prasaṅgena phalākāṅkṣī dhṛtiḥ sā pārtha rājasī

Bhgc na vimuñcati durmedhā dhṛtiḥ sā pārtha tāmasī
