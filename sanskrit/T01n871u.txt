Nāmāṣṭaśatakaya
namaḥ śākyasiṃhāya
 sambuddha puṇḍarīkākṣa sarvajña karuṇāspadam
samantabhadra śāstāra śākyasiṃham namāmy aham 
 śrīghana śrīmati śreṣṭha śīlarāśi śivaṅkaram
śrīmanta śrīkara śānta śāntaveśa namāmy aham 
 nairātmyavādina siddha niravadya nirāśravam
nītijña nirmalātmāna niṣkalaṅkam namāy aham 
 nirvāda nirahaṅkāra nirvikalpa tathāgatām
nirdhūtanikhilakleśa niṣprapañca namāmy aham 
 viśveśvara vimuktijña viśvarūpa vināyakam
viśvalakṣaṇasampūrṇa vītarāga namāmy aham 
 vidyācaraṇasampanna viśveśa vimalaprabham
vinītaveśa vipula vītadoṣa namāmy aham 
 durdāntadamaka sarvya śuddha śauddhodani munim
sugata sugati saumya śubhrakīrtti namāmy aham 
 yogīśvara daśabala lokajña lokapūjitam
lokācāryya nirācāryya lokanātha namāmy aham 
 kalaṅkamukta kāmārim akalaṅka kalādharam
kāntamūrtti dayāpātra kanakābha namāmy aham 
 paramārttha parajyoti parama parameśvaram
bhavābhavakara bhavya bhagavanta namāmy aham 
 mahāmati mahāvīryya mahābhijña mahābalam
mahodyama mahādhairyya mahābāhu namāmy aham 
 ādya pavitra sarvīyam aparājitam acyutam
mita parahita nātham amitābha namāmy aham 
  caturmārārivijita tatvajña śaṅkara śivam
satvasāra sadācāra sārtthavāha namāmy aham 
 devadeva mahādeva devavanditam avyayam
pramāṇātītadeveśa divyarūpa namāmy aham 
 jitendriya jitakleśa jinendra puruṣottamam
uttama sattama brāhma puṇyakṣetra namāmy aham 
 bhaktyeda yaḥ paṭhen nitya prātar utthāya mānavaḥ
nāmāṣṭaśataka puṇya pavitra pāpanāśanam
 sa labhetamitān bhogān bhaumān svargodbhvās tathā
vyādhayas ta na bādhante duḥsvapna tasya naśyati 
 āyurārogyasampannas sarvaiśvaryyasamanvitaḥ
medhāvī kulajo vāgmī bhavej janmani janmani 
nāmāṣṭaśatakaya nimi
