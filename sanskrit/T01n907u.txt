vajravilāsinīstotram
mahāpaṇḍitavibhūticandrapādakṛtam
om namaḥ śrīvajrayoginyai
devāsuranaravanditacaraṇe bhavamagnoddharaṇārpitacaraṇe 
tvayi rodimi nutisavyapadeśaḥ kiṃ paśyasi punaraniśamaśeṣam   
mātardevi kirasi kaṇamuṣṭiṃ janayad yajñasutaḥ kimatuṣṭim 
vajravārāhi narāhisurāṇāṃ tvaṃ śaraṇaṃ tava nāmaparāṇām   
harikarari
śikhiphaṇitaskarabhītistvatparacitte naiva sameti 
mātardevi nibhālaya mahyaṃ kiṃ sahase mama duḥkhamasahyam   
tarjanavajrakaroṭavi
dhāriṇi duṣṭaṃ tuṣṭaya māranivāriṇi 
abhinavakomalakaracaraṇāṅgi trivalisamucchadasaptavibhaṅgi   
grāhyagrāhakabhāvaviśuddhe pādakarāṅgulijālapinaddhe 
āyatapārśvāṅgulikaracaraṇe dhvastavyādhijarājanimaraṇe   
udgatareṇurūrdhvagataromā vajrayogavivṛtāmṛtadhāmā 
bālamarīcivilokanarakte jagatāṃ duḥkhanirākṛtiśakte   
subhru tanūdari madhyakṛśāṅgi karṇāntāyatalocanabhṛṅgibhaṅgi 
ḍākinye va hi
viśvamaśeṣaṃ loko vetti na te bahuveṣam   
yatnamṛte jagadarthasamṛddhiścintitamātrajanepsitasiddhiḥ 
mūḍho vetti na duṣkṛtakarma prāvṛṣīva na śilāṅkurajanma   
andha ivārkaśaśāṅkādarśī pāpajano hyanubhāvasparśī 
cintāmaṇimajñā na vidanti prajñe tvasya phalāni phalanti   
cakrāṅkitakarapādamanojñe jñānānalabhasmīkṛtasaṅge 
kāyavacanamanasā kṛtaduritaṃ rāgadveṣamohaparikarilitam   
taddiśāmi tava devi samakṣaṃ yāvadyāmi na durgatipakṣam 
manaḥ paramārthaṃ tanustava vedī sa bhavati naiva duritaparikhedī   
lakṣaṇanikhilālakṣitagātre sakalānuvyañjanagatapātre 
navayauvanamadamantharapiṇḍe calakuṇḍalayugamaṇḍitagaṇḍe   
tuṅganitambaghanastanabhāre galakālambiniraṃśukaṅkuśahāre 
saṃvaramadhupavicumbimukhābje tadbhujayugaparirabdhahṛdabje   
nirbharasuratasukhāvikalākṣi muktaśiroruhavasananirapekṣe 
herukarāhudaṣṭamukhacandre sasmitaracita hū hū kṛtamantre   
patimaulisthitavidhumamṛṣantī kimu candrārkau vapuramuṣantī 
mātaste jagadanupamarūpaṃ tadvicāraya paramārtha sasvarūpam   
kleśadāhiṃha
śamanāmṛtavacanaṃ tadvineyajanaśuddhiviracanam 
padminīti nalināmalagandhaḥ karuṇāparajagadarthanibandhaḥ   
divyasudhādharagata
rasapānaṃ tajjagadadvayabodhinidhānam 
pratyaṅgasparśo'pyanimittaṃ sahajāmbudhiviplāvitacittam   
kiṃ tvaṃ mātaḥ kariṣyasi tābhiḥ ṣaṭtriṃśacchatakoṭyabalābhiḥ 
yadyanyāṃ manute bahutātastadvihāya jhagiti dehi nu mātaḥ   
nāḍīcakranirodha  mahāsukhasaṃvara 
devi tvaṃ śatabhāvavikalpā śūnyasamādhirapākṛtatulyā talpā   
śūnyakṛpe sahajādvayamuktaṃ vajrāyajrayā
namidamavadhivibhaktam 
bhāvābhāvasamastākāśavyāpī bhavati sa tattvavikāśaḥ   
udbhavanti tata eva vimokṣā bodhipakṣanikhilapratipakṣāḥ 
buddhānāmāveṇikadharmaḥ sattvarāśiparipācitakarmaḥ   
yadvā yānatrayaniryāṇaṃ dhūmādikamapi cāparimāṇam 
tvadbhaktyā yadalambhi śubhaṃ me bhavatu mano bhavatīparamaṃ me 
sakalakaluṣarahitaṃ guṇasindhustvatparamaṃ hyata eva na bandhuḥ   
guhyasamayasādhanatantre śrīvajravilāsinīstotraṃ samāptam 
kṛtiriyaṃ mahāpaṇḍitavibhūticandrapādānām
