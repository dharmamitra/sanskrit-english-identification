Vigrahavyāvartanīkārikā 
sarveṣāṃ bhāvānāṃ sarvatra na vidyate svabhāvaś cet 
tvadvacanam asvabhāvaṃ na nivartayituṃ svabhāvam alam  NagVvy
atha savabhāvam etad vākyaṃ pūrvā hatā pratijñā te 
vaiṣamikatvaṃ tasmin viśeṣahetuś ca vaktavyaḥ  NagVvy
mā śabdavad ity etat syāt te buddhir na caitad upapannam 
śabdena hy atra satā bhaviṣyato vāraṇaṃ tasya  NagVvy
pratiṣedhapratiṣedho 'py evam iti mataṃ bhavet tad asad eva 
evaṃ tava pratijñā lakṣaṅato dūṣyate na mama  NagVvy
pratyakṣeṇa hi tāvad yady upalabhya vinivartayasi bhāvān 
tan nāsti pratyakṣaṃ bhāvā yenopalabhyante  NagVvy
anumānaṃ pratyuktaṃ pratyakṣeṇāgamopamāne ca 
anumānāgamasādhyā ye 'rthā dṛṣṭāntasādhyāś ca  NagVvy
kuśalānāṃ dharmāvasthāvidaś ca manyante 
kuśalaṃ janāḥ svabhāvaṃ śeṣeṣv apy eṣa viniyogaḥ  NagVvy
nairyāṇikasvabhāvo dharmā nairyāṇikāś ca ye teṣām 
dharmāvasthoktānām evaṃ anairyāṇikādīnām  NagVvy
yadi ca na bhavet svabhāvo dharmāṇāṃ niḥsvabhāva ity evam 
nāmāpi bhaven naivaṃ nāma hi nirvastukaṃ nāsti  NagVvy
atha vidyate svabhāvaḥ sa ca dharmāṇāṃ na vidyate tasmāt 
dharmair vinā svabhāvaḥ sa yasya tad yuktam upadeṣṭum  NagVvy
sata eva pratiṣedho nāsti ghaṭo geha ity ayaṃ yasmāt 
dṛṣṭaḥ pratiṣedho 'yaṃ sataḥ svabhāvasya te tasmāt  NagVvy
atha nāsti sa svabhāvaḥ kiṃ nu pratiṣidhyate tvayānena 
vacanenarte vacanāt pratiṣedhaḥ sidhyate hy asataḥ  NagVvy
bālānāṃ iva mithyā mṛgatṛṣnāyāṃ yathājalagrāhaḥ 
evaṃ mithyāgrāhaḥ syāt te pratiṣedhyato hy asataḥ  NagVvy
nanv evaṃ saty asti grāho grāhyaṃ ca tadgrahītā ca 
pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣedhyaṃ pratiṣeddhā ceti ṣaṭkaṃ tat  NagVvy
atha naivāsti grāho naiva grāhyaṃ na ca grahītāraḥ 
pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣedhyaṃ pratiṣeddhāro nanu sa santi  NagVvy
pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣedhyaṃ pratiṣeddhāraś ca yadyuta na santi 
siddhā hi sarvabhāvās teṣam eva svabhāvaś ca  NagVvy
hetoś ca te na siddhir naiḥsvābhāvyāt kuto hi te hetuḥ 
nirhetukasya siddhir na copapannāsya te 'rthasya  NagVvy
yadi cāhetoḥ siddhiḥ svabhāvavinivartanasya te bhavati 
svābhāvyasyāstitvaṃ mamāpi nirhetukaṃ siddham  NagVvy
atha hetor astitvaṃ bhāvāsvābhāvyam ity anupapannam 
lokeṣu niḥsvabhāvo na hi kaś cana vidyate bhāvaḥ  NagVvy
pūrvaṃ cet pratiṣedhaḥ paścāt pratiṣedhyam ity anupapannam 
paścāc cānupapanno yugapac ca yataḥ svabhāvaḥ san  NagVvy
hetupratyayasāmagryāṃ ca pṛthak cāpi madvaco na yadi 
manu śūnyatvaṃ siddhaṃ bhāvānām asvabhāvatvāt  NagVvy
yaś ca pratītyabhāvo bhāvānāṃ śūnyateti sā proktā 
yaś pratītyabhāvo bhavati hi tasyāsvabhāvatvam  NagVvy
nirmitako nirmitakaṃ māyāpuruṣaḥ svamāyayā sṛṣṭam 
pratiṣedhayeta yadvat pratiṣedho 'yaṃ tathaiva syāt  NagVvy
na svābhāvikam etad vākyaṃ tasmān na vādahānir me 
nāsti ca vaiṣamikatvaṃ viśeṣahetuś ca na nigadyaḥ  NagVvy
mā śabdavad iti nāyaṃ dṛṣṭānto yas tvayā samārabdhaḥ 
śabdena hi tac chabdasya vāraṇaṃ naivam etac ca  NagVvy
naiḥsvābhāvyānāṃ cen naiḥsvābhāvyena vāraṇaṃ yadi hi 
naiḥsvābhāvyanivṛttau svābhāvyaṃ hi prasiddhaṃ syāt  NagVvy
atha vā nirmitakāyāṃ yathā striyāṃ strīyam ity asadgrāham 
nirmitakaḥ pratihanyāt kasya cid evaṃ bhaved etat  NagVvy
atha vā sādhyasamo 'yaṃ hetur na hi vidyate dhvaneḥ sattā 
saṃvyavahāraṃ ca vayaṃ nānabhyupagamya kathayāmaḥ  NagVvy
yadi kā cana pratijñā syān me tata eṣa me bhaved doṣaḥ 
nāsti ca mama pratijñā tasmān naivāsti me doṣaḥ  NagVvy
yadi kiṃ cid upalabheyaṃ pravartayeyaṃ nivartayeyaṃ vā 
pratyakṣādibhir arthais tadabhāvān me 'nupālambhaḥ  NagVvy
yadi ca pramāṇatas te teṣāṃ teṣāṃ prasiddhir arthānām 
teṣāṃ punaḥ prasiddhiṃ brūhi kathaṃ te pramāṇānām  NagVvy
anyair yadi pramāṇaiḥ pramāṇasiddhir bhavet tad anavasthā 
nādeḥ siddhis tatrāsti naiva madhyasya nāntasya  NagVvy
teṣām atha pramāṇair vinā prasiddhir vihīyate vādaḥ 
vaiṣamikatvaṃ tasmin viśeṣahetuś ca vaktavyaḥ  NagVvy
viṣamopanyāso 'yaṃ na hy ātmānaṃ prakāśayaty agniḥ 
na hi tasyānupalabdhir dṛṣṭā tamasīva kumbhasya  NagVvy
yadi ca svātmānam ayaṃ tvadvacanena prakāśayaty agniḥ 
param iva nanv ātmānaṃ svaṃ paridhakṣyaty api hutāśaḥ  NagVvy
yadi ca svaparātmānau tvadvacanena prakāśayaty agniḥ 
pracchādayiṣyati tamaḥ svaparātmānau hutāśa iva  NagVvy
nāsti tamaś ca jvalane yatra ca tiṣṭhati parātmani jvalanaḥ 
kurute kathaṃ prakāśaṃ sa hi prakāśo 'ndhakāravadhaḥ  NagVvy
utpadyamāna eva prakāśayaty agnir ity asadvādaḥ 
utpadyamāna eva prāpnoti tamo na hi hutāśaḥ  NagVvy
aprāpto 'pi jvalano yadi vā punar andhakāram upahanyāt 
sarveṣu lokadhātuṣu tamo 'yam iha saṃsthito hanyāt  NagVvy
yadi svataś ca pramāṇasiddhir anapekṣya tava prameyāṇi 
bhavati pramāṇasiddhir na parāpekṣā svataḥsiddhiḥ  NagVvy
anapekṣya hi prameyān arthān yadi te pramāṇasiddhir iti 
na bhavanti kasya cid evam imāni tāni pramāṇāni  NagVvy
atha matam apekṣya siddhis teṣām ity atra bhavati ko doṣaḥ 
siddhasya sādhanaṃ syān nāsiddho 'pekṣate hy anyat  NagVvy
sidhyanti hi prameyāṇy apekṣya yadi sarvathā pramāṇāni 
bhavati prameyasiddhir nāpekṣyaiva pramāṇāni  NagVvy
yadi ca prameyasiddhir nāpekṣyaiva bhavati pramāṇāni 
kiṃ te pramāṇasiddhyā tāni yadarthaṃ prasiddhaṃ tat  NagVvy
atha tu pramāṇasiddhir bhavaty apekṣyaiva te prameyāṇi 
vyatyaya evaṃ sati te dhruvaṃ pramāṇaprameyāṇām  NagVvy
atha te pramāṇasiddhyā prameyasiddhiḥ prameyasiddhyā ca 
bhavati pramāṇasiddhir nāsty ubhayasyāpi te siddhiḥ  NagVvy
sidhyanti hi pramāṇair yadi prameyāṇi tāni tair eva 
sādhyāni ca prameyais tāni kathaṃ sādhayiṣyanti  NagVvy
sidhyanti ca prameyair yadi pramāṇāni tāni tair eva 
sādhyāni ca pramāṇais tāni kathaṃ sādhayiṣyanti  NagVvy
pitrā yady utpādyaḥ putro yadi tena caiva putreṇa 
utpādyaḥ sa yadi pitā vada tatrotpādayati kaḥ kam  NagVvy
kaś ca pitā kaḥ putras tatra tvaṃ brūhi tāv ubhāv api ca 
pitṛputralakṣaṇadharau yato bhavati no 'tra saṃdehaḥ  NagVvy
naiva svataḥ prasiddhir na parasparataḥ parapramāṇair vā 
na bhavati na ca prameyair na cāpy akasmāt pramāṇānāṃ  NagVvy
kuśalānāṃ dharmāṇāṃ dharmāvasthāvido bruvīran yat 
kuśalaṃ svabhāvam evaṃ pravibhāgenābhidheyaḥ syāt  NagVvy
yadi ca pratīya kuśalaḥ svabhāva utpadyate sa kuśalānām 
dharmāṇāṃ parabhāvaḥ svabhāva evaṃ kathaṃ bhavati  NagVvy
atha na pratītya kiṃ cit svabhāva utpadyate sa kuśalānām 
dharmāṇam evaṃ syād vāso na brahmacaryasya  NagVvy
nādharmo dharmo vā saṃvyavahārāś ca laukikā na syuḥ 
nityāś ca sasvabhāvāḥ syur nityatvād ahetumataḥ  NagVvy
evam akuśaleṣv avyākṛteṣu nairyāṇikādiṣu ca doṣaḥ 
tasmāt sarvaṃ saṃskṛtam asaṃskṛtaṃ te bhavaty eva  NagVvy
yaḥ sadbhūtaṃ nāmātra brūyāt sasvabhāva ity evam 
bhavatā prativaktavyo nāma brūmaś ca na vayaṃ sat  NagVvy
nāmāsad iti ca yad idaṃ tat kiṃ nu sato bhavaty utāpy asataḥ 
yadi hi sato yady asato dvidhāpi te hīyate vādaḥ  NagVvy
sarveṣāṃ bhāvānāṃ śūnyatvaṃ copapāditaṃ pūrvam 
sa upālambhas tasmād bhavaty ayaṃ cāpratijñāyāḥ  NagVvy
atha vidyate svabhāvaḥ sa ca dharmāṇāṃ na vidyata itīdam 
āśaṅkitaṃ yad uktaṃ bhavaty anāśaṅkitaṃ tac ca  NagVvy
sata eva pratiṣedho yadi śūnyatvaṃ nanu prasiddham idam 
pratiṣedhayate hi bhavān bhāvānāṃ niḥsvabhāvatvam  NagVvy
pratiṣedhayase 'tha tvaṃ śūnyatvaṃ tac ca nāsti śūnyatvam 
pratiṣedhaḥ sata iti te nanv eṣa vihīyate vādaḥ  NagVvy
pratiṣedhayāmi nāhaṃ kiṃ cit pratiṣedhyam asti na ca kiṃ cit 
tasmāt pratiṣedhayasīty adhilaya eṣa tvayā kriyate  NagVvy
yac cāharte vacanād asataḥ pratiṣedhavacanasiddhir iti 
atra jñāpayate vāg asad iti tan na pratinihanti  NagVvy
mṛgatṛṣṇādṛṣṭānte yaḥ punar uktas tvayā mahāṃś carcaḥ 
tatrāpi nirṇayaṃ śṛṇu yathā sa dṛṣṭānta upapannaḥ  NagVvy
sa yadi svabhāvataḥ syād grāho na syāt pratītya saṃbhūtaḥ 
yaś ca pratītya bhavati grāho nanu śūnyatā saiva  NagVvy
yadi ca svabhāvataḥ syād grāhaḥ kas taṃ nivartayed grāham 
śeṣeṣv apy eṣa viddhis tasmād eṣo 'nupālambhaḥ  NagVvy
etena hetvabhāvaḥ pratyuktaḥ pūrvam eva sa samatvāt 
mṛgatṛṣṇādṛṣṭāntavyāvṛttividhau ya uktaḥ prāk  NagVvy
yas traikālye hetuḥ pratyuktaḥ pūrvam eva sa samatvāt 
traikālyapratihetuś ca śūnyatāvādināṃ prāptaḥ  NagVvy
prabhavati ca śūnyateyaṃ yasya prabhavanti tasya sarvārthāḥ 
prabhavati na tasya kiṃ cin na prabhavati śūnyatā yasya  NagVvy
yaḥ śūnyatāṃ pratītyasamutpādaṃ madhyamāṃ pratipadaṃ ca 
ekārthāṃ nijagāda praṇamāmi tam apratimabuddham  NagVvy
