Satyasūtra
Wal śrāvastyā nidānam 
Wal ye kecid bhikṣavaḥ samyag eva śraddhayā agārād anagārikā pravrajati sarve te caturṇā āryasatyānā jñānāya 
Wal katameṣā caturṇām 
Wal duḥkham āryasatyasya duḥkhasamudayasya duḥkhanirodhasya duḥkhanirodhagāminī pratipad āryasatyasya jñānāya 
Wal tasmāt tarhi caturṇām āryasatyānām anabhisamitānām abhisamayāya atimātra tīvracchando vyāyāmaś cotsudhiś cāprativāṇiś ca smṛtyā saprajanyena  
ca yogaḥ karaṇīyaḥ
