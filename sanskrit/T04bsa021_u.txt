Vigrahavyāvartanī Vvsarveṣāṃ bhāvānāṃ sarvatra na vidyate svabhāvaścet tvadvacanamasvabhāvaṃ na nivartayituṃ svabhāvamalam  Vv atha sasvabhāvametadvākyaṃ śrutvā hatā pratijñā te vaiṣamikatvaṃ tasmin viśeṣahetuśca vaktavyaḥ  Vv mā śabdavadityetat syātte buddhirna caitadupapannam śabdenātra satā bhaviṣyato vāraṇaṃ tasya  Vv pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣeddhyo pyevamiti mataṃ bhavet tadasadeva evaṃ tava anumānāgamasādhyā ye rthā dṛṣṭāntasādhyāśca  Vv kuśalānāṃ dharmāṇāṃ dharmāvasthāvidaśca manyante kuśalaṃ janasvabhāvaṃ śeṣeṣvapyeṣa viniyogaḥ  Vv nairyāṇikasvabhāvo dharmo nairyāṇikāśca ye teṣām dharmāvasthoktānāmeva ca nairyāṇikādīnām  Vv yadi ca na bhavet svabhāvo dharmāṇāṃ niḥsvabhāva ityevam nāmāpi bhavennaivaṃ nāmāpi nirvastukaṃ nāsti  Vv atha vidyate svabhāvaḥ sa ca dharmāṇāṃ na vidyate tasmāt dharmairvinā svabhāvaḥ sa yasyāsti tad yuktamupadeṣṭum  Vv sata eva pratiṣedho nāsti ghaṭo geha ityayaṃ yasmāt dṛṣṭaḥ pratiṣedho yaṃ sataḥ svabhāvasya te tasmāt  Vv atha nāsti sa svabhāvaḥ kiṃ nu pratividhyate tvayānena vacanenarte vacanāt pratiṣedhaḥ sidhyate hyasataḥ  Vv bālānāmiva mithyā mṛgatṛṣṇāyāṃ yathā jalagrāhaḥ evaṃ mithyāgrāhaḥ syātte pratiṣidhyato hyasataḥ  Vv nanvevaṃ satyasti pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣedhyaṃ pratiṣeddhāraśca yadyuta na santi siddhā hi sarvabhāvā yeṣāmevaṃ svabhāvaśca  Vv hetostato na siddhirnaiḥsvābhāvyāt kuto hi te hetuḥ nirhetukasya siddhirna copapannāsya te rthasya  Vv yadi cāhetoḥ siddhiḥ svabhāvavinivartanasya te bhavati svābhāvyasyāstitvaṃ mamāpi nirhetukaṃ siddham  Vv atha hetorastitvaṃ bhāvanaiḥsvābhāvyamityanupapannam loke naiḥsvābhāvyānna hi kaścana vidyate bhāvaḥ  Vv pūrvaṃ cet pratiṣedhaḥ paścāt pratiṣedhyamiti ca nopapannam paścādanupapanno yugapacca yataḥ svabhāvo san  Vv hetupratyayasāmagryāṃ pṛthagbhāve pi madvaco na yadi nanu śūnyatvaṃ siddhaṃ bhāvānāmasvabhāvatvāt  Vv yaśca pratītya bhāvo bhāvānāṃ śūnyateti sā hyuktā yaśca pratītya bhāvo bhavati hi tasyāsvabhāvatvam  Vv nirmitako nirmitakaṃ māyāpuruṣaḥ svamāyayā sṛṣṭam pratisedhayase yadvat pratiṣedho yaṃ tathaiva syāt  Vv na svābhāvikametad vākyaṃ tasmānna vādahānirme nāsti ca vaiṣamikatvaṃ viśeṣahetuśca na nigadyaḥ  Vv mā śabdavaditi nāyaṃ dṛṣṭānto yastvayā mamārabdhaḥ śabdena hi tacca śabdasya vāraṇaṃ naiva me vacaḥ  Vv naiḥsvābhāvyānāṃ cennaiḥsvābhāvyena vāraṇaṃ yadi hi naiḥsvābhāvyanivṛttau svābhāvyaṃ hi prasiddhaṃ syāt  Vv athavā nirmitakāyāṃ yathā striyāṃ striyamityasaṃgrāham nirmitakaḥ pratihanyāt kasyacidevaṃ bhavedetat  Vv athavā sādhyasamo yaṃ heturna hi vidyate dhvaneḥ sattā saṃvyavahāraṃ ca vayaṃ nānabhyupagamya kathayāmaḥ  Vv yadi kācana pratijñā tatra syādeṣa me bhaveddoṣaḥ nāsti ca mama pratijñā tasmānnaivāsti me doṣaḥ  Vv yadi kiṃcidupalabheyaṃ pravartayeyaṃ nivartayeyaṃ vā pratyakṣādibhirarthaistadabhāvānme nupālambhaḥ  Vv yadi ca pramāṇatasteṣāṃ teṣāṃ prasiddhirarthānām teṣāṃ punaḥ prasiddhiṃ brūhi kathaṃ te pramāṇānām  Vv anyairyadi pramāṇaiḥ pramāṇasiddhirbhavatyanavasthā nādeḥ siddhistatrāsti naiva madhyasya nāntasya  Vv teṣāmatha pramāṇairvinā prasiddhirvihīyate vādaḥ vaiṣamikatvaṃ tasmin viśeṣahetuśca vaktavyaḥ  Vv viṣamopanyāso yaṃ na hyātmānaṃ prakāśayatyagniḥ na hi tasyānupalabdhirdṛṣṭā tamasīva kumbhasya  Vv yadi svātmānamayaṃ tvadvacanena prakāśayatyagniḥ paramiva na tvātmānaṃ paridhakṣyatyapi hutāśaḥ  Vv yadi ca svaparātmānau tvadvacanena prakāśayatyagniḥ pracchādayiṣyati tamaḥ svaparātmānau hutāśa iva  Vv nāsti tamaśca jvalane yatra ca tiṣṭhati sadātmani jvalanaḥ kurute kathaṃ prakāśaṃ sa hi prakāśo ndhakāravadhaḥ  Vv utpadyamāna eva prakāśayatyagnirityasadvādaḥ utpadyamāna eva prāpnoti tamo na hi hutāśaḥ  Vv aprāpto pi jvalano yadi vā punarandhakāramupahanyāt sarveṣu lokadhātuṣu tamo yamihasaṃsthita upahanyāt  Vv yadi ca svataḥpramāṇasiddhiranapekṣya te prameyāṇi bhavati pramāṇasiddhirna syānnāsiddho pekṣate hyanyat  Vv sidhyanti hi prameyāṇyapekṣya yadi sarvathā pramāṇāni bhavati prameyasiddhiranapekṣyaiva pramāṇāni  Vv yadi ca prameyasiddhiranapekṣyaiva bhavati pramāṇāni kiṃ te pramāṇasiddhyā tāni yadarthaṃ prasiddhaṃ tat  Vv atha tu pramāṇasiddhirbhavatyapekṣyaiva te prameyāṇi vyatyaya evaṃ sati te dhruvaṃ pramāṇaprameyāṇām  Vv atha tai pramāṇasiddhyā prameyasiddhiḥ prameyasiddhyā cābhavati pramāṇasiddhirnāstyubhayasyāpi te siddhiḥ  Vv sidhyanti hi pramāṇairyadi prameyāṇi tāni taireva sādhyāni ca prameyaistāni kathaṃ sādhayiṣyanti  Vv sidhyanti ca prameyairyadi pramāṇāni tāni taireva sādhyāni ca prameyaistāni kathaṃ sādhayiṣyanti  Vv pitrā yadyutpādyaḥ putro yadi tena caiva putreṇa utpādyaḥ sa yadi pitā vada tatrotpādayati kaḥ kam  Vv kaśca pitā kaḥ putrastatra tvaṃ brūhi tāvubhāvapi ca pitāputralakṣaṇadharau yato naḥ putrasaṃdehaḥ  Vv naiva svataḥprasiddhirna parasparataḥ pramāṇairvā bhavati na ca prameyairna cāpyakasmāt pramāṇānām  Vv kuśalānāṃ dharmāṇāṃ dharmāvasthāvidho brūvate yat kuśalasvabhāvamevaṃ pravibhāgenābhidheyaḥ syāt  Vv yadi ca pratītya kuśalaḥ svabhāva utpadyate sa kuśalānām dharmāṇāṃ parabhāvaḥ svabhāva evaṃ kathaṃ bhavati  Vv atha na pratītya kiṃcit svabhāva utpadyate sa kuśalānām dharmāṇāmevaṃ syāda vāso na brahmacaryasya  Vv nādharmo dharmo vā saṃvyavahārāśca laukikā na syuḥ nityāśca sarvabhāvāḥ syurnityatvādahetumataḥ  Vv eṣa cākuśaleḥvavyākṛteḥu nairyāṇādiḥu ca doḥaḥ tasmāt sarvaṃ saṃskṛtamasaṃskṛtaṃ te bhavatyevam  Vv yaḥ sadbhūtaṃ nāma brūyāt sa svabhāva ityevam bhavatā prativaktavyo nāma brūmaśca na vyaṃ sat  Vv nāmāsaditi ca yadidaṃ tatkiṃ nu sato bhavatyutāsataḥ yadi hi sato yadyasato dvidhāpi te hīyate vādaḥ  Vv sarveṣāṃ bhāvānāṃ śūnyatvaṃ copapāditaṃ pūrvam sa upālambhastasmād bhavatyayaṃ ca pratijñāyāḥ  Vv atha vidyate svabhāvaḥ sa ca dharmāṇāṃ na vidyata iti idamāśaṅkitaṃ yaduktaṃ bhavatyanāśaṅkitaṃ tacca  Vv sata eva pratiṣedho yadi śūnyatvaṃ nanvapratiṣiddhamidam pratiṣedhayate hi bhavān bhāvānāṃ niḥsvabhāvatvam  Vv pratiṣedhayase tha tvaṃ śūnyatvaṃ tacca nāsti śūnyatvam pratiṣedhaḥ sata iti te nanvevaṃ hīyate vādaḥ  Vv pratiṣedhayāmi nāhaṃ kiṃcit mṛgatṛṣṇādṛṣṭānte yaḥ punaruktaṃ tvayā mahāṃścaryaḥ tatrāpi nirṇayaṃ śṛṇu yathā sa dṛṣṭānta upapannaḥ  Vv sa yadi svabhāvataḥ syāt bhāvo na syāt pratītyasamudbhūtaḥ yaśca pratītya bhavati grāho nanu
