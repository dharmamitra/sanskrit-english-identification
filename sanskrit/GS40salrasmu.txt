dūto raktakaṣāyakṛṣṇavasano dantī jarāmarditas tailābhyaktaśarīrakāyudhakaro dīnāśrupūrṇānanaḥ 
bhasmāṅgārakapālapāṃśumuśalaḥ sūryāstasūryodaye yaḥ sūryasvarasaṃsthito gadavatāṃ kālāya sa syādasau  Rmañj 
akasmāccittavikṛtirakasmātpuruṣottamaḥ 
akasmādindriyotpattiḥ sannipātasya lakṣaṇam  Rmañj 
śarīraṃ śītalaṃ yasya prakṛter vikṛtir bhavet 
tatrāriṣṭaṃ samāsena vyāptaṃ tatra nibodha me  Rmañj 
duṣṭaśabdena ramate sādhuśabdena kupyati 
yaścākasmānna śṛṇute taṃ gatāyuṣamādiśet  Rmañj 
yo vā gandhaṃ na gṛhṇāti dīpe śānte ca mānavaḥ 
divājyotīṃṣi yaścāpi jvalitāni ca paśyati  Rmañj 
candraṃ sūryaprabhaṃ paśyet sūryaṃ vā candravarcasam 
taḍidvātoṣitān meghān nirmale gagane carān  Rmañj 
vimānayānaprāsādairyaśca saṃkulamambaram 
yaśca nīlaṃ mūrtimantam antarikṣaṃ ca paśyati  Rmañj 
yastūṣṇam iva gṛhṇāti śītamuṣṇaṃ ca śītavat 
saṃjātaḥ saṃśayo yasya taṃ vadanti gatāyuṣam  Rmañj 
viparītena gṛhṇāti bhāvānanyāṃśca yo naraḥ 
dhūmanīhāravāsobhirāvṛtāmiva medinīm  Rmañj 
pradīptamiva lokaṃ ca yo'valuptamivāmbhasā 
bhūmim aṣṭādaśākārāṃ lekhābhiryastu paśyati  Rmañj 
jyotsnādarśe hi toyeṣu chāyāṃ yaśca na paśyati 
paśyatyekāṅgahīnāṃ ca vaikṛtaṃ cāpi paśyati  Rmañj 
śvakākakaṅkagṛdhrāṇāṃ prayātaṃ yakṣarākṣasām 
piśācoraganāgānāṃ vikṛtāmapi yo naraḥ  Rmañj 
hrīśriyau yasya naśyetāṃ teja ojaḥ smṛtis tathā 
akasmājjṛmbhate yaśca sa parāsurasaṃśayam  Rmañj 
yasyādharoṣṭhaḥ patati sthitaścordhvaṃ tathottaram 
pāśaśaṅkā bhavedyasya durlabhaṃ tasya jīvitam  Rmañj 
kuṭilā sphuṭitā vāpi suptā yasya ca nāsikā 
avasphūryati magnā vā sa parāsurasaṃśayam  Rmañj 
svarūpaṃ paranetreṣu puttikāṃ yo na paśyati 
yadā hi paṭudṛṣṭiśca tadā mṛtyur adūrataḥ  Rmañj 
karṇahīnaṃ yadātmānaṃ paśyatyātmā kathaṃcana 
na sa jīvati loke'smin kālena kavalīkṛtaḥ  Rmañj 
rātrau dāho bhavedyasya divā śītaṃ ca jāyate 
kaphapūritakaṇṭhasya mṛtyuścaiva na saṃśayaḥ  Rmañj 
caraṇau śītalau yasya śītalaṃ nābhimaṇḍalam 
śirastāpo bhavedyasya tasya mṛtyurna saṃśayaḥ  Rmañj 
huṅkāraḥ śītalo yasya phutkāro vahṇisannibhaḥ 
sadā dāho bhavedyasya tasya mṛtyurna saṃśayaḥ  Rmañj 
arundhatīṃ dhruvaṃ caiva viṣṇostrīṇi padāni ca 
hīnāyudho na paśyanti caturthaṃ mātṛmaṇḍalam  Rmañj 
araśmibimbaṃ sūryasya vahneścaivāṃśuvarjitam 
dṛṣṭvaikādaśamāsāstu naraścordhvaṃ na jīvati  Rmañj 
vātaṃ mūtraṃ purīṣaṃ yaḥ suvarṇaṃ rajataṃ tathā 
pratyakṣamathavā svapne daśamāsaṃ na jīvati  Rmañj 
kvacit paśyati yo dīptaṃ svarṇavat kānanaṃ naraḥ 
virūpāṇi ca bhūtāni navamāsaṃ na jīvati  Rmañj 
sthūlāṅgo'pi kṛśaḥ kṛśo'pi sahasā sthūlatvamālambate śyāmo vā kanakaprabho yadi bhavedgauro'pi kṛṣṇacchaviḥ 
dhīro dhīratayārthadharmanipuṇaḥ śāntopakārī pumān ityevaṃ prakṛte tu śāntacalanaṃ māsyaṣṭame mṛtyudam  Rmañj 
pīḍā bhavetpāṇitale ca jihvāmūlaṃ samūlaṃ rudhiraṃ ca kṛṣṇam 
vṛddhiṃ naraḥ kāmapi yanna dṛṣṭvā jīvenmanuṣyaḥ sa hi saptamāsān  Rmañj 
madhyāṅgulīnāṃ tritayaṃ viraktaṃ rogaṃ vinā śuṣyati yasya kaṇṭhaḥ 
muhurmuhuḥ prasravaṇaṃ ca jāḍyaṃ ṣaṣṭhe ca māse pralayaṃ prayāti  Rmañj 
yasya na sphuraṇaṃ kiṃcid vidyate yasya karmaṇi 
so'vaśyaṃ pañcame māsi skandhārūḍho gamiṣyati  Rmañj 
yasya na sphurate jyotiḥ pīḍite nayanadvaye 
maraṇaṃ tasya nirdiṣṭaṃ caturthe māsi niścitam  Rmañj 
spandate vṛṣaṇo yasya na kiṃcidapi pīḍitaḥ 
tṛtīye māsi so'vaśyaṃ yamaloke gamiṣyati  Rmañj 
tārā divā candraprabhaṃ niśānte yo vidyutaṃ paśyati caiva śvabhre 
indrāyudhaṃ vā svayameva rātrau māsadvaye tasya vadanti nāśam  Rmañj 
yasya jānugataṃ marma na kiṃcidapi ceṣṭitam 
māsānte maraṇaṃ tasya na kenāpi vilambyate  Rmañj 
kaniṣṭhāṅguliparva syāt kṛṣṇaṃ ca madhyamaṃ yadā 
gatāyuḥ procyate puṃsām aṣṭādaśadināvadhiḥ  Rmañj 
ghṛte taile jale vāpi darpaṇe yasya dṛśyate 
śirorahitamātmānaṃ pakṣamekaṃ sa jīvati  Rmañj 
śaityaṃ dadhyannapānāni yasya tāpakarāṇi ca 
śītaraśmi bhaveccāruhāsaṃ cātha sunirmalam  Rmañj 
na vetti vai cāruhitaṃ na coṣṇaṃ vetti yo naraḥ 
kālajñānena samproktaṃ pakṣamekaṃ sa jīvati  Rmañj 
snātamātrasya yasyaite trayaḥ śuṣyanti tatkṣaṇāt 
hṛdayaṃ hastapādau ca daśarātraṃ sa jīvati  Rmañj 
nāsāgraṃ rasanāgraṃ ca cakṣuścaivauṣṭhasampuṭam 
yo na paśyetpurādṛṣṭaṃ saptarātraṃ sa jīvati  Rmañj 
dhārā bindusamā yasya patate ca mahītale 
saptāhājjāyate mṛtyuḥ kālajñānena kathyate  Rmañj 
athātaḥ sampravakṣyāmi chāyāpuruṣalakṣaṇam 
yasya vijñānamātreṇa trikālajño bhavennaraḥ  Rmañj 
kālo dūrasthito'syāpi yenopāyena lakṣyate 
taṃ vadanti samāsena yathoddiṣṭaṃ śivāgame  Rmañj 
ekānte vijane gatvā kṛtvādityaṃ svapṛṣṭhataḥ 
saṃnirīkṣya nijacchāyāṃ kaṇṭhadeśasamāhitām  Rmañj 
tataścākāśamīkṣeta tataḥ paśyati śaṃkaram 
aṣṭottaraśataṃ japtvā tato vai dṛśyate śubham  Rmañj 
śuddhasphaṭikasaṃkāśaṃ nānārūpadharo haram 
ṣaṇmāsābhyāsayogena bhūcarāṇāṃ patirbhavet  Rmañj 
varṣadvayena he nātha kartā hartā svayaṃ prabhuḥ 
trikālajñatvam āpnoti sa yogī nātra saṃśayaḥ  Rmañj 
yatkṛtābhyāsayogena nāsti kiṃcit sudurlabham 
tadrūpaṃ kṛṣṇavarṇaṃ ca paśyati vyomni nirmale  Rmañj 
ṣaṇmāsān mṛtyumāpnoti sa yogī nātra saṃśayaḥ 
pīto vyādhibhayaṃ rakto nīlo hatyāṃ vinirdiśet  Rmañj 
nānāvarṇe svarūpe'sminudvego jāyate mahān 
pādau gulphaṃ ca jaṭharaṃ vināśakṛśatā bhavet  Rmañj 
ardhavarṣeṇa varṣe vā jīvanvarṣadvayena na vā 
vināśo dakṣiṇe bāhau svabandhur mriyate dhruvam  Rmañj 
vāmabāhau tathā bhāryā vinaśyati na saṃśayaḥ 
śiro dakṣiṇabāhubhyāṃ vināśo mṛtyumādiśet  Rmañj 
aśiro māsamaraṇaṃ vinā jaṅghe divā nava 
aṣṭabhiḥ skandhanāśena chāyāluptena tatkṣaṇāt  Rmañj 
tīrthasnānena dānena tapasā sukṛtena vā 
japena jñānayogena jāyate kālabandhanam  Rmañj 
rasāyanaṃ ca pūrvoktaṃ guṭikā mṛtajīvanī 
naraiḥ sevyā yathoktaṃ ca paraṃ kālasya bandhanam  Rmañj 
rakṣaṇīyamato dehaṃ yato dharmādisādhanam 
śarīraṃ nāśayantyete doṣā dhātumalāśrayāḥ  Rmañj 
vaidyanāthatanūjena śālināthena dhīmatā 
śāstramālokya cākṛṣya racitā rasamañjarī  Rmañj
