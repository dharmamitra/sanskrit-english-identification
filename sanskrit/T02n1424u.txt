adhṛṣyāścāpyabaddhādhyā
śca ramadhvamakutobhayāḥ  
nivṛtaṃ bhavaduḥkhaṃ vo'tyantabhavaśuddhaye 
saṃbhūtāḥ śāsināmagrā atyantabhavasiddhaye  
ayaṃ vaḥ satataṃ rakṣaḥkṣyaḥ
siddhaḥ samayasaṃvaraḥ 
sarvabuddhaiḥ samaṃ proktājñā paramaśāśvatī  
bodhicittaṃ na vai tyājyaṃ yadvajramiti mudrayā 
yasyotpādanamātreṇa buddha eva na saṃśayaḥ  
saddharmo na pratikṣepyo na ca tyājyaḥ kadācana 
ajñānādvātha mohādvā na vai vivṛṇuyāt sa tu  
svamātmānaṃ parityājya tapobhirna ca pīḍayet 
yathāsukhaṃ sukhaṃ dhāryaḥ saṃbuddho'yamanāgataḥ  
vajraṃ ghaṇṭā ca mudrā ca na vai tyājyā kadācana 
ācāryo nāvamantavyaḥ sarvabuddhasamo hyasau  
jvarairgarairviṣai rogairḍākinyupadravairgrahaiḥ 
vighnairvināyakairghorairmārito narakaṃ vrajet  
tasmāt sarvaprayatnena vajrācāryaṃ mahāgurum 
pracchannavarakalyāṇaṃ nāvamanyet kadācana  
anurūpaṃ ca te deyaṃ gurubhaktaṃ sadakṣiṇam 
tato jvarādayastāpā na bhūyaḥ prabhavanti hi  
nityaṃ svasamayaḥ sādhyo nityaṃ pūjyāstathāgatāḥ 
nityaṃ ca gurave deyaṃ sarvabuddhasamo hyasau  
tad dānāt puṇyasaṃbhāraḥ sambhārādbodhiruttamā 
datte'smai sarvabuddhebhyo dattaṃ bhavati śāśvatam  
adya vaḥ saphalaṃ janma yadasmin supratiṣṭhitāḥ 
samāḥ samayadevānāṃ bhavitāstha na saṃśayaḥ  
adyābhiṣiktā āyuṣmantaḥ sarvabuddhaiḥ savajribhiḥ 
traidhātukamahārājyaṃjāḥ
rājādhipatayaḥ sthirāḥ  
adya mārān vinirjitya praviṣṭāḥ paramaṃ puram 
prāptamadyaiva buddhatvaṃ bhavadbhirnātra saṃśayaḥ  
iti kuruta manaḥ prasādavajraṃ
svasamayamakṣayasaukhyadaṃ bhajadhvam 
jagati laghusukhe'dya vajrasattva
pratisamaśāśvatatāṃ gatā bhavantaḥ  
praṇipatya guroḥ pādau śiṣyāḥ sadbhaktivatsalāḥ 
brūyureva kariṣyāmo yathājñāpayase vibho  
satyeva saṃbhave teṣāṃ pratyekaṃ vāmapāṇinā 
savyāṅguṣṭhakamāgṛhya śāntiṃ kuryād vidhānataḥ  
trisaptāhutimekāṃ vā rājño vā bhūpateratha 
dikpālasvātmaśāntau ca hutvā yācetta dakṣiṇām  
sarvasattvārthakartavye śrutādau vā prati prati 
bhūgajādi suvarṇādau svasiddhau vā sahāyatām  
saṃgṛhya yattadutsṛṣṭaṃ saṃtoṣya dattadakṣiṇān 
sarvāhāravihāraistaiḥ svaparaiścakramarcayet  
saṃgṛhya yogyasacchiṣyān vicitraparamāyubhiḥ 
santarpya copasaṃhṛtya muḥkārāntaiśca tryakṣaraiḥ  
akāretyādi mantreṇa śūnyavaccakramukttamam 
dattvārthine rajaḥ stokaṃ mahattoye rajaḥ sṛjet  
garttāpūre pratiṣṭhāyāṃ home cakre ca yaddhanam 
ācāryasyaiva tatsarvamityāha varavajradhṛk  
ityādikarmikasyāyamuktaścakravidhiḥ sphuṭaḥ 
jñānāveśasulabdhaḥ sattvāveśenaiva kathyate  
dvādaśābde samāveśya saṃpūjya bālabālike 
tābhyāṃ yadracayeccakraṃ cittavākkāyikaṃ matam  
citte tvakṣobhyamāmakyorādeve
śaḥ samudāhṛtaḥ 
pāṇḍarāmitayorvāci kāye śāśvatalocane  
cittavākkāyasaṃstobhādracanā tviyamadbhutā 
cittastobhāt parijñānaṃ vākstobhānmantrabhāṣaṇam  
kāyastobhācca khe sthānaṃ stobhāveśe vidhistvayam 
ācāryaśiṣyaseko'tra prāṅnyāyenaiva saṃsthitaḥ  
prāptajñānavaśī kuryāccetasaiva tricakrakam 
tadadhiṣṭhānataścakraṃ dṛśyate svaparairyataḥ  
praveśo'trābhyanujñātaḥ spaṣṭasvapnavadiṣyate 
yatastenottamo jñeyaścittamavṛ
ttidṛḍhatvataḥ  
triyogināmapi prāgvad vidhirvākkāyacakrayoḥ 
tatsthāne'kṣobhyacihnaṃ syādvākkāyaguhyaśuddhaye  
jñānādikarmisaṃleśisaṃprāptavaśināmapi 
cetasaiva vidhiḥ sarvastaddhīno naiva sidhyati  
vajrācāryāgraśiṣyāṇāṃ niṣyandādiratātmanām 
manasokto vidhiḥ śreṣṭho vāṅmātreṇātra kiṃ bhavet  
vṛttamātrānna buddhatvaṃ śrutamātrācca no bhavet 
cintayāpi na yāvacca bhāvanāto nirudhyate

alaṃ bahuvisarpiṇyā kathayā mantravādiṃnām 
cetaḥ sādhyaṃ viśeṣeṇa cittāt saṃbodhisambhavaḥ
