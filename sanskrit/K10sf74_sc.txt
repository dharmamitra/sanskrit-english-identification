Candropama
eva mayā śrutam ekasmi samaye bhagavā rājagṛhe viharati devadatta lābha satkāra
ślokam ārabhya bhikṣūn āmantrayati  candropamā bhikṣavo viharata  nitya navakā
iva hrīmantaḥ kuleṣv apragalbhā avakṛṣya kāyam avakṛṣya citta kulāny upasakramata 
tad yathā cakṣuṣmā puruṣo jarodapāna vā nadīdurga vā parvata viṣama vā avakṛṣya
kāyam avakṛṣya citta vyavalokayed evam eva candropamā viharata nitya navakā iva
hrīmantaḥ kuleṣv apragalbhā avakṛṣya kāyam avakṛṣya citta kulāny upasakramata 
kāśyapo hi bhikṣuś candropamo viharati nitya navaka iva hrīmān kuleṣv apragalbho
vakṛṣya kāyam avakṛṣya citta kulāny upasakramati  tad yathā cakṣuṣmā puruṣo
jarodapāna vā nadī durga vā parvata viṣama vā avakṛṣya kāyam avakṛṣya citta vyavalokayed
evam eva kāśyapo hi bhikṣuś candropamo viharati nitya navaka iva hrīmā kuleṣv apragalbhaḥ
avakṛṣya kāyam avakṛṣya citta kulāny upasakrāmati  ki manyatha
