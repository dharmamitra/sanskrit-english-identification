saṃbhāṣaṇārthinā  Bhmj nirnimeṣekṣaṇo maunī taṃ vīkṣya kṣapitāśayaḥ netrapāṇendriyaiḥ kṣipraṃ tameva sahasāviśat  Bhmj dehaṃ nirmokavattyaktvā praviṣṭe vidure nṛpaḥ vihāya rājasaṃ bhāvaṃ babhūva māturantike  Bhmj phalāhāraḥ kṣamāśāyī kṣapayitvātha śarvarīm prātardatvā yathākāmaṃ dvijebhyo ratnakāñcanam  Bhmj rājarṣibhiḥ sahāsīnaṃ praṇamya kurupuṃgavam dadarśa tejasāṃ rāśiṃ prāptaṃ satyavatīsutam  Bhmj vyāso pi dharmatanayaṃ saṃbhāvya racitāñjalim yatidharmeṇa tāṃ siddhiṃ ślāghyāṃ kṣatturapūjayat  Bhmj arthitaḥ kṣatravṛddhena gāndhāryā pṛthayā tayā
