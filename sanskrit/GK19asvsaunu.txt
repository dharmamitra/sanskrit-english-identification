CANTO I
gautamaḥ kapilo nāma muni dharmabhṛtāṃ varaḥ 
babhūva tapasi śrāntaḥ kākṣīvān iva gautamaḥ  Saund 
aśiśriyad yaḥ satataṃ dīptaṃ kāśyapavat tapaḥ 
āśiśrāya ca tadvṛddhau siddhim kāśyapavat parām  Saund 
haviḥṣu yaś ca svātmārthaṃ gām adhukṣad vasiṣṭhavat 
tapaḥśiṣṭeṣu ca śiṣyeṣu gām adhukṣad vasiṣṭhavat 
māhātmyād dīrghatapaso yo dvitīya ivābhavat  Saund 
tṛtīya iva yaś cābhūt kāyāṅgirasayor dhiyā  Saund 
tasya vistīrṇatapasaḥ pārśve himavataḥ śubhe 
kṣetraṃ cāyatanaṃ caiva tapasām āśramo 'bhavat  Saund 
cāruvīruttaruvanaḥ prasnigdhamṛduśādvalaḥ 
havirdhūmavitānena yaḥ sadābhra ivābabhau  Saund 
mṛdubhiḥ saikataiḥ snigdhaiḥ kesarātarapāṇḍubhiḥ 
bhūmibhāgair asaṃkīrṇaiḥ sāṅgarāga ivābhavat  Saund 
śucibhis tīrthasaṃkhyātaiḥ pāvanair bhāvanair api 
bandhumān iva yas tasthau sarobhiḥ sasaraoruhaiḥ  Saund 
paryāptaphalapuṣpābhiḥ sarvato vanarājibhiḥ 
śuśubhe vavṛdhe caiva naraḥ sādhanavān iva  Saund 
nīvāraphalasaṃtuṣṭaiḥ svasthaiḥ śāntair anutsukaiḥ 
akīrṇo 'pi tapobhṛdhibhiḥ śūnyaśūnya ivābhavat  Saund 
agnīnāṃ hūyamānānāṃ śikhinā kūjatām api 
tīrthānāṃ cābhiṣekeṣu śuśruve yatra nisvanaḥ  Saund 
virejur hariṇā yatra suptā medhyāsu vediṣu 
salājair mādhavīpuṣpair upahārāḥ kṛtā iva  Saund 
api kṣudramṛgā yatra sāntāś ceruḥ samaṃ mṛgaiḥ 
śarañyebhyas tapasvibhyo vinayaṅ śikṣitā iva  Saund 
saṃdigdhe 'py apunarbhāve vidudhheṣv āgameṣv api 
pratyakṣiṇa ivākurvaṃs tapo yatra tapodhanāḥ  Saund 
yatra sma mīyate brahma kaiś cit kaiś cin na mīyate 
kāle nimīyate somo na cākāle pramīyate  Saund 
nirapekṣāḥ śarireṣu dharme yatra svabhuddhayaḥ 
saṃhṛṣṭā iva yatnena tāpasās te pire tapaḥ  Saund
śrāmyanto munayo yatra svargāyodyuktacetasaḥ 
taporāgeṇa dharmasya vilopam iva cakrire  Saund 
atha tejavisadanaṃ tapaḥkṣetraṃ tam āśramam 
kecid ikṣvākavo jagmū rājaputrā vivatsavaḥ  Saund 
suvarṇastambhavarṣmāṇaḥ siṃhoraskā mahābhujāḥ 
pātraṃ śabdasya mahataḥ śriyāṃ ca vinayasya ca  Saund 
arharūpā hy anarhasya mahātmānaś calātmanaḥ 
prājñāḥ prajñāvimuktasya bhrātṛyasya yavīyasaḥ  Saund 
mātṛśulkād upagatāṃ te śriyaṃ na viṣehire 
rarakṣuś ca pituḥ kautsās te bhavanti sma gautamāḥ  Saund 
ekapitror yathā bhrātroḥ pṛthagguruparigrahāt 
rāma evābhavad gārgyo vāsubhadro 'pi gautamaḥ  Saund 
śākravṛkṣapraticchannaṃ vāsaṃ yasmāc ca cakrire 
tasmād ikṣvākuvaṃśyās te bhuvi śākya iti smṛtāḥ  Saund 
sa teṣāṃ gautamaś cakre svavaṃśasadṛśīḥ kriyāḥ 
munir ūrdhvaṃ kumārasya sagarasyeva bhārgavaḥ  Saund 
kaṇvaḥ śākuntalasyeva bharatasya tarasviaḥ 
vālmīkir iva dhīmāṃś ca dhīmator maithileyayoḥ  Saund 
tadvanaṃ muninā tena taiś ca kṣatriyapuṅgavaiḥ 
śāntāṃ guptāṃ ca yugapad brahmakṣatraśriyaṃ dadhe  Saund 
athodakalaśaṃ gṛhya teṣā vṛddhicikīrṣayā 
muniḥ sa viyad utpatya tān uvāca nṛpātmajān  Saund 
yā patet kalaśād asmād akṣayyasalilān mahīm 
dhārā tām anatikramya mām anveta yathākramam  Saund 
tataḥ paramam ity uktvā śirobhiḥ praṇipatya ca 
rathān āruruhuḥ sarve śīghravāhān alaṅkrṛtān  Saund 
tataḥ sa tair anugataḥ syandanasthair nabhogataḥ 
tadāśramamahīprāntaṃ paricikṣepa vāriṇā  Saund 
aṣṭāpadam ivālikhya nimittaiḥ surbhīkṛtam 
tān uvāca muniḥ sthitvā bhūmipālasutān idam  Saund 
asmin dhārāparikṣipte nemicihnitalakṣaṇe 
nirmimīdhvaṃ puraṃ yūyaṃ mayi yāte triviṣṭapam  Saund 
tataḥ kadācit te vīrās tasmin pratigate munau 
babhramur yauvanoddāmā gajā iva niraṅkuśāḥ  Saund 
baddhagodhāṅgulītrāṇā hastaviṣṭhitakārmukāḥ 
śarādhmātamahātūṇā vyāyatābaddhavāsasaḥ  Saund 
jijñāsamānā nāgeṣu kauśalaṃ śvāpadeṣu ca 
anucakrur vanasthasya dauṣmanter devakramaṇaḥ  Saund 
tān dṛṣṭvā prakṛtiṃ yātān vṛddhān vyāghraśiśūn iva 
tāpasās tadvanarh hitvā himavantarh siṣevire  Saund 
tatas tadāśramasthānaṃ śūnyarh taiḥ śūnyacetasaḥ 
paśyanto tadāśramasthānarṃ śūnyaṃ taiḥ niśaśvasuḥ  Saund 
atha te puṇyakarmāṇaḥ pratyupasthiravṛddhayaḥ 
tatra tajjñair upākhyātān avāpur mahato nidhīn  Saund 
alaṃ dharmārthakāmānāṃ nikhilānām svāptaye 
nidhayo naikavidhayo bhūrayas te gatārayaḥ  Saund 
tatas tatpratilambhāc ca pariṇāmāc ca karmaṇaḥ 
tasmin vāstuni vāstujñāḥ puraṃ śrīman nyaveśayan  Saund 
saridvistīrṇaparikhaṃ spaṣṭāñcitamahāpatham 
śailakalpamahāvapraṃ givirajam ivāparam  Saund 
pāṇḍurāṭṭālasumukhaṃ suvibhaktāntarāpaṇam 
harmyamālāparikṣiptaṃ kukṣiṃ himagirer iva  Saund 
vedavedāṅgaviduṣas tasthuṣaḥ ṣaṭsu karmasu 
śāntaye vṛddhaye caiva yatra viprān ajītjapan  Saund 
tadbhūmer abhiyoktṝṇāṃ prayuktān vinivṛttaye 
yatra svena prabhāvena bhṛtyadaṇḍān ajītjapan  Saund 
cāritradhansaṃpannān salajjān dīrghadarśinaḥ 
arhato 'tiṣṭhipan yatra śūrān dakṣān kuṭumbinaḥ  Saund 
vyastais tais tair guñair yuktān mativāgvikramādibhiḥ 
karmasu patirūpeṣu saicāṃs tān nyayūyujan  Saund 
vasumadbhir avibhrāntair alaṃvidyair avismitaiḥ 
yad babhāse naraiḥ kīrṇaṃ mandaraḥ kinnarair iva  Saund 
yatra te hṛṣṭamanasaḥ pauraprītickīrṣayā 
śrīmanty udyānasaṃjñāni yaśodhāmāny acīkaran  Saund 
śvāḥ puṣkariṇīś caiva paramāgryaguṇāmbhasaḥ 
nājñāyā cetanotkarṣād dikṣu sarvāsv acīkhanan  Saund 
manojñāḥ śrīmatiḥ praśṭhīḥ pathiṣūpavaneṣu ca 
sabhāḥ kūpavatīś caiva samantāt pratyatiṣṭhipan  Saund 
hastyaśvarathasakīrṇam asaṃkīrṇam anākulam 
anigūḍhārthivibhavaṃ nigūḍhajñānapauruṣam  Saund 
saṃnidhānam ivārthānām ādhānam iva tejasām 
niketam iva vidyānāṃ saṃketam iva saṃpadām  Saund 
vāsavṛkṣaṃ guṇavatām āśrayaṃ śaraṇaiṣiām 
ānartaṃ kṛtaśāstrāṇām ālānaṃ bābhuśālinām  Saund 
samājair utsavair dāyaiḥ kriyāvidhibhir eva ca 
alañcakrur alaṃvīryās te jagaddhāma tatpuram  Saund 
yasmād anyāyatas te ca kaṃ cin nācīkaran karam 
tasmād alpena kālena tat tadāpūpuran puram  Saund 
kapilasya ca tasyarṣes tasminn āśramavāstuni 
yasmāt te tatpuraṃ cakrus tasmāt kapilavāstu tat  Saund 
kakandasya makandasya kuśāmbasyeva cāśrame 
puryo yathā hi śrūyante tathaiva kapilasya tat  Saund 
āpuḥ puraṃ tatpuruhūtakalpās te tejasāryeṇa na vismayena 
āpur yaśogandham ataś ca śaśvat sutā yayāter iva kīrtimantaḥ  Saund 
tannāthavṛttair api rājaputrair arājakaṃ naiva rarāja rāṣṭram 
tārāsahasrair api dīpyamānair anutthite candra ivāntarīkṣam  Saund 
yo jyāyān atha vayasā guṇaiś ca teṣāṃ bhrātṝṇāṃ vṛṣabha ivaujasā vṛṣāṇām 
te tatra priyaguruvas tam abhyaṣiñcann ādityā daśaśatalocanaṃ divīva  Saund 
ācāravān vinayavān nayavān kriyāvān dharmāya nendriyadukhāya dhṛtātparaḥ 
tadbhrātṛbhiḥ parivṛtaḥ sa jugopa rāṣṭraṃ saṃkrandano divam ivānuṣrto marudbhiḥ  Saund 
Saundaranada mahākāvye kapilavāstuvarṇano nāma prathamaḥ sargaḥ 
CANTO II
tataḥ kadā cit kālena tad avāpa kulakramāt 
rāja śuddhodhano nāma śuddhakarmā jitendriyaḥ  Saund 
yaḥ sasañje na kāmeṣu śrīpāptau na visismiye 
nāvamene parānṛddhyā parebhyo nāpi vivyathe  Saund 
balīyān sattvasaṃpannaḥ śrutavān buddhimān api 
vikrānto nayvāṃś caiva dhīraḥ sumukha eva ca  Saund 
vapuṣmāṃś ca na ca stabdho dakṣiṇo na ca nārajavaḥ 
tejasvī na ca na kṣāntaḥ kartā ca na ca vismitaḥ  Saund 
ākṣiptaḥ śatrubhiḥ saṃkhye suhṛdbhiś ca vyapāśritaḥ 
abhavad yo na vimukhas tejasā ditsayaiva ca  Saund 
yaḥ pūrvai rājabhir yātāṃ yiyāsur dharmapaddhatim 
rājyaṃ dīkṣām iva vahan vṛttetānvagamat pitṝn  Saund 
yasya suvyahārāc ca rakṣanāc ca sukhaṃ prajāḥ 
śiśyire vigatodvegāḥ pitur aṅkagatā iva  Saund 
kṛtaśāstraḥ kṛtāstro vā jāto vā vipule kule 
akṛtārtho na dadṛśe yasya darśanameyivān  Saund 
hitaṃ vipriyam apy ukto yaḥ śuśrāva na cukṣubhe 
duṣkṛtaṃ bahv api tyaktvā sasmāra kṛtam aṇv api  Saund 
praṇatān anujagrāha vijagrāha kuladviṣaḥ 
āpannān parijagrāha nijagrāhāsthitān pathi  Saund 
prāyeṇa viṣaye yasya tacchīlam anuvartinaḥ 
arjayanto dadṛśire dhanānīva guṇān api  Saund 
adhyaiṣṭa yaḥ paraṃ brahma na vyaiṣṭa satataṃ dhṛteḥ 
dānān yadita pātrebhyaḥ pāpaṃ nākṛta kiṃ cana  Saund 
dhṛtyāvākṣīt pratijñāṃ sa sadvājivodyatāṃ dhuram 
na hy avānchīc eyutaḥ satyān muhūrtam api jīvitam  Saund 
viduṣaḥ paryupāsiṣṭa vyakāśiṣṭātmavattayā 
vyarociṣta ca śiṣṭebhyo māsīṣe candramā iva  Saund 
avedīd buddhiśāstrābhyām iha cāmutra ca kṣamam 
arakṣīd dhairyavīryābhyām indriyāṇy api ca prajāḥ  Saund 
ahārṣīd duḥkham ārtānāṃ dviṣatāṃ corjitaṃ yaśaḥ 
acaiṣte ca nayair bhūmiṃ bhūyasā yaśasaiva ca  Saund 
apy āsīd duḥkhitān paśyan prakṛtyā karuṇātmakaḥ 
nāhauṣte ca yaśo lobhād anyāyādhigatair dhanaiḥ  Saund 
sauhārdaṛḍhabhaktitvān maitreṣu viguṇeṣv api 
nādidāsīdaditsīt tu saumukhvāt svaṃ svam arthavat  Saund 
anivedyāgram arhadbhyo nālikṣat hiṃ cid aplutaḥ 
gām adharmeṇa nādhukṣat kṣīratarṣeṇa gām iva  Saund 
nāsṛkṣad balim aprāptaṃ nārukṣan mānam aiśvaram 
āgamair buddhim āhikṣad dharmāya na tu kīrtaye  Saund 
kleśārhān api kāṃś ci tu nākliṣṭa kliṣṭakarmaṇaḥ 
āryabhāvāc ca nāghukṣad dviṣato 'pi sato guṇān  Saund 
ākṛṣad vapuṣā dṛṣṭīḥ prajānāṃ candramā iva 
parasvaṃ bhuvi nāmṛkṣan mahāviṣam ivoragam  Saund 
nākrukṣad viṣaye tasya kaś cit kaiś cit kva cit kṣataḥ 
adikṣat tasya hastastham ārtebhyo hy abhayaṃ dhanuḥ  Saund 
kṛtāgaso 'pi praṇatān prāg eva priyakāriṇaḥ 
adarśat snighayā dṛṣṭyā ślakṣṇena vacasāsicat  Saund 
bahvīr adhyagamad vidyā viṣayeṣv akutūhalaḥ 
adarśat kārtayuge dharme dharmāt kṛcchre 'pi nāsrasat  Saund 
avardhiṣṭa guñaiḥ śaśvad avṛdhan mitrasaṃpadā 
avartiṣṭa ca vṛddheṣu nāvṛtad garhite pathi  Saund 
śarair aśīśam acchatrūn guṇair bandhūn arīramat 
randhrair nācūcudad bhṛtyān karair nāpīpiḍat prajāḥ  Saund 
rakṣaṇāc caiva śauryāc ca nikhilāṃ gām avīvapat 
spaṣṭayā daṇḍanītyā ca rātrisattrān avīvapat  Saund 
kulaṃ rājarṣivṛttena yaśogandham avīvapat 
dīptyā tama ivādityas tejasārīn avīvapat  Saund 
apaprathat pitṝṃś caiva satputradaṛśair guṇaiḥ 
salileneva cāmbhodo vṛttenājihṇadat prajāḥ  Saund 
dānair ajasravipulaiḥ somaṃ viprān asūṣavat 
rājadharmasthiatvāc ca kāle sasyam asūṣavat  Saund 
adharmiṣṭhām acakathan na kathām akathaṅkataḥ 
cakravartīva ca parān dharmāyābhyudasīṣahat  Saund 
rāṣṭram anyatra ca baler na sa kiṅ cid dadīdapat 
bhṛtyair eva ca sodyogaṃ dviṣaddarpam adīdapat  Saund 
svair evādīdapac cāpi bhūyo bhūyo guṇaiḥ kulam 
prajā nādīdapac caiva sarvadharmavyavasthayā  Saund 
aśrāntaḥ samaye yajvā vajñabhūmim amīmapat 
pālanāc ca cvijān brahma nirduvignān amīmapat  Saund 
gurubhir vidhivatkāle saumyaḥ somam amīmapat 
tapasā tejasā caiva dviṣatsainyam amīmapat  Saund 
prajāḥ paramadharmjñaḥ sūkṣmaṃ dharmam avīvasat 
darśanāc caiva dharmasya kāle svargam avīvasat  Saund 
vyaktam apy arthakṛcchreṣu nādharmiṣṭham atiṣṭhipat 
priya ity eva cāśaktaṃ na saṃrāgād avīvṛdhat  Saund 
tejasā ca tviṣā caiva ripūn dṛptān abhībhasat 
yaśodīpena dīptena pṛthivīṃ ca vyabhībhasat  Saund 
ānṛśaṃsyān na yaśase tenādāyi sadārthine 
dravyaṃ mahad api tyaktvā na caivākīrti kiṃ cana  Saund 
tenārir āpi duḥkhārto nātyāji śaraṇāgataḥ 
jitvā dṛptān api ripūn na tenākāri vismayaḥ  Saund 
na tenābhedi māryādā kāmād dveṣād bhayād api 
tena satsv api bhogeṣu nāsevīndriyavṛttitā  Saund 
na tenārdarśi viṣamaṃ kāryaṅ kva cana kiṃ cana 
vipriyapriyayoḥ kṛtye na tenāgāmi nikriyāḥ  Saund 
tenāpāyi yathākalpaṃ somaś ca yaśa eva ca 
vedaś cāmnāyi satataṃ vedokto dharma eva ca  Saund 
evamādibhir atyakto babhūvāsulabhair guṇaiḥ 
aśakyaśakyasāmantaḥ śākyarājaḥ sa śakravat  Saund 
atha tasmin tathā kāle dharmakāmā divaukasaḥ 
vicerur diśi lokasya dharmacaryā didṛkṣavaḥ  Saund 
dharmātmānaś carantas te dharmajijñāsayā jagat 
dadṛśus taṃ viśeṣeṇa dharmātmānaṃ narādhipam  Saund 
devebhyas tuṣitebhyo 'tha bodhisattvaḥ kṣitiṃ vrajan 
upapattiṃ praṇidadhe kule tasya mahīpateḥ  Saund 
tasya devī nṛdevasya māyā nāma tadābhavat 
vītakrodhatamomāyā māyeva divi devatā  Saund 
svapne 'tha samaye garbham āviśantaṃ dadarśa sā 
ṣaḍdantaṃ vāraṇaṃ śvetam airāvatam ivaujasā  Saund 
taṃ vinirdidiṣuḥ śrutvā svapnaṃ svapnavido dvijāḥ 
tasya janma kumārasya lakṣmīdharmayaśobhṛtaḥ  Saund 
tasya sattvaviśeṣasya jātau jātikṣayaiṣinaḥ 
sācalā pracacālorvī taraṅgābhihateva nauḥ  Saund 
sūryaraśmibhir akliṣṭaṃ puṣpavarṣaṃ papāta khāt 
digvāraṇakarādhūtād vanāc caitrarathād iva  Saund 
divi dundubhayo nedur dīvyatāṃ marutām iva 
didīpe 'bhyadhiaṃ sūryaḥ śivaś ca pavano vavau  Saund 
tutuṣus tuṣitāś caiva śuddhāvāsāś ca devatāḥ 
saddharmabahumānena sattvānāṃ cānukampayā  Saund 
samāyayau yaśaḥketuṃ śreyaḥketukaraḥ paraḥ 
babhrāje śāntayā lakṣmyā dharmo vigrahavān iva  Saund 
devyām api yavīyasyām araṇyām iva pāvakaḥ 
nando nāma suto jajñe nityānandakaraḥ kule  Saund 
dīrghabāhur mahāvakṣāḥ siṃhāṃso vṛsabhekṣanaḥ 
vapuṣāgryeṇa yo nāma sundaropapadaṃ dadhe  Saund 
madhumāsa iva prāptaś candro naa ivoditaḥ 
aṅgavān iva cānaṅgaḥ sa babhau kāntayā śriyā  Saund 
sa tau saṃvardhayāmāsa narendraḥ parayā mudā 
arthaḥ sajjanahastastho dharmakāmau mahān iva  Saund 
tasya kālena satputrau vavṛdhāte bhavāya tau 
āryasyārambhamahato darmārthāv iva bhūtaye  Saund 
tayoḥ satputrayor madhye śākyarājo rarāja saḥ 
madhyadeśa iva vyakto himavatpāripātrayoḥ  Saund 
tatas tayoḥ saṃskṛtayoḥ krameṇa narendrasūnvoḥ kṛtavidyayoś ca 
kāmeṣv ajasraṅ pramamāda nandaḥ sarvārthasiddhas tu na saṃrarañja  Saund 
sa prekśyaiva hi jīrṇam āturaṅ ca mṛtaṅ ca vimṛśan jagadanbhijñam ārtacittaḥ 
hṛdayagataparaghṛṇo na viṣayaratim agamaj jananamaraṇabhayam abhito vijighāṃsuḥ  Saund 
udvegād apunarbhave manaḥ praṇidhāya sa yayau śayitavarāṅganādanāsthaḥ 
niśi nṛpatinilayanād vanagamankṛtmanāḥ sarasa iva mathitanalināt kalahaṃsaḥ  Saund 
Saundaranande mahākāvye rājavarṇano nāma dvitīyaḥ sargaḥ

tattvakṛtamatir upāsya jahāvayam apy amārga iti māgakovidaḥ  Saund 
sa vicārayan jagati kiṃ nu paramam iti taṃ tam āgamam 
niścayam anadhigataḥ parataḥ paramaṃ cacāra tapa eva duṣkaram  Saund 
atha naiṣa mārga iti vikṣya tad api vipulaṃ jahau tapaḥ 
dhyānaviṣayam avagamya paraṃ bubhuje varānnam amṛtatvabuddhaye  Saund
