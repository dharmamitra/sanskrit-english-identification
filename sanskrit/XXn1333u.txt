 Cittatatvasaṅgrahaḥ namas tasmai bhagavaterhate samyaksambuddhāya agraḥ śreṣṭaś ca jyeṣṭhas tribhuvanajagataś śāstṛbhūtaś ca loke yobhūl lakṣmīghanas tat padakamalayugam mastakenabhivandya lokānāṃ dlrghakālam pihitamaviditaṃ cittadharmasya tatvaṃ jijñāsūnām pravakṣye sugatamunimatam gauraveṇanugamya  ī sarvā cittakathā dharme  proktaivādityabandhunā caturbhir eva vacanaiḥ  pañcaskandhādidarśane   prathamā vedanā tatra  saṃjñā tu syād dvitlyīkā tṛtīyas tu ca saṃskāras  turyaṃ vijñānam eva hi   etaccatuṣṭayaṃ yatrai  katvena gṛhyate yadā tadā tasya samūhasya  vayaṃ cittaṃ vadāmahe   vedanāsaṃjñāsaṃskārāḥ  prabuddhasya gatir bhavet caturthaṃ tu ca vijñānaṃ  susuptasya bhaved gatiḥ   avasthāhyantarbūtā tu  svapnaṃ evetyudīritā gatis tasyām prabuddhasya  susuptiś ca na vidyate   prabuddhasya gatis tatra  kriyavjñānameva hi anyad akriyavijñānaṃ  susuptasya gatiḥ punaḥ   avasthām antarbhūtām tu  dvayamiśravaśād viduḥ  atītasṃṛtisaṃskāra  rūpenāvirbhavantite  indriyārthasanṇikarṣāj  janyaṃ jñānaṃ tu vedanā upalabdhiś ca pratyakṣo  viṣayānubhavopi ca vedanāyās tu paryāyā  dṛśyante nyāyadarśane   vedanāśabdasyārthasya  ṛgvede caiva saṃskṛte sambuddhena prayuktārtha  samaṃ kiñcin na dṛśyate   vīthivijñānatraividhye  vedanā prathamābhavet saivarjujñānamārgaṃ syād  anye dyenṛjuke tathā   vīthivijñānasaṃjñā tu  cintanārthaṃ prakāśate tarkānumānajānana  sarvaṃjñāgataṃ bhavet   vedanānāmapratyakso  vṛddhiṃ gaccati cintane dṛṣṭismṛtipsāsarve tu  saṃjñasādhakahetavaḥ   vedanāsaṃjñāyugalam  ekaikaṃ hi prabhāsvaram prabhāsvaracittakathā  dharme tasmād dhi dṛśyate   aprabhāsvaracittan tu  saṃskārasahitaṃ bhavet bāladarakacittaṃ hi  tasmād evāprabhāsvaram   vedanāsaṃjñāvijñāna  saṃskārā buddhadarśane dṛṣṭārthayuktāḥ kutrāpi  dṛśyante nānyadarśane   vedanāsaṃjñāsaṃskāra  trayaiḥ śabdair udīritam cittasya vīthivijñānam  muninādityabandhunā   saṃskārakṣayaśabdārthaṃ  nirvāṇañ ca samam bhavet visaṃskāragataṃ cittam  iti buddhaprakāśanam   saṃskāratṛṣṇāsambandhaḥ  katham jñātuṃ hi śakyate buddha evāvadat tatra  tṛṣṇānāṃ kṣayam adhyagām   vyākṛtānivrtas tatra  tṛṣṇādoṣās trayo matāḥ ātmasnehātmamānātma  dṛṣṭināmavaśāt punaḥ   etat trayaṃ hi tṛṣṇāyā  ātmamohavaśād bhavet tasmād avidyātṛṣṇātma  mohāḥ paryāyavācakāḥ   etair jātās tu saṃskārā  na vidyanterhate kvacit kliśyate sarvakāle tu  cittam etaiḥ pṛthagjane   pāriśuddhiṃ hi cittasya  saṃskārakṣayam eva tat puṇyapāpaprahīṇasya  cittaṃ nirvutam ucyate   prabhāsvaraṃ hi taccittam  anyat kliṣṭam bhavet sadā tasmāt pṛthagjanāḥ sarve  unmattā ityudīritāḥ   yad vedayati tat sarvaṃ  saṃjānāti tathā punaḥ vitarkayati tajjñātaṃ  tad vitarkam prapañcayet   ityuvāca mahārhā sa  kāśyapo manasaḥ kriyām nikāye madhyame pālyāṃ  sūtre tu madhupiṇḍike   prapañco nāma saṃskāro  yāvad āharate phalam tāvad akriyavijñāna  rūpeṇaivāvatiṣṭhati   karmasaṃskārasaṃkleśa  prapañcāśravakiñcanāḥ puṇyapāpe ca kuśalā  kuśaletīñjitāmalāḥ   saṅgasaṃyojane śalyā  nuśayānuśravāśayāḥ upādānānganāḥ kleśāḥ  sarve paryāyavācakāḥ   vedanāsaṃjñāyugalaṃ  sasaṃskāratrikañ ca yat vīthivijñānanāmena  kriyakarmavaśaj javet   kriyaṃ hi vīthivijñānaṃ  nirvutāvyākṛtam bhavet karmabhūtaṃ vītthicittaṃ  vyākṛtañ cāpyanirvutam   buddhārhatāṃ suddhacittaṃ  kriyarūpeṇa dhāvati pṛthagjanasya cittan tu  karmatatvena dhāvayet   puṇyapāpaprahīṇaṃ yat  suddhaṃ buddhasya mānasam taddhi lokottaraṃ jñātamnāsti tasya punarbhavaḥ   bhavāṅgajaṃ vīthicittaṃ  cakṣurādikrameṇa ṣaṭ tadālambavaśāt tacca  vīthimuktam praviśyati   kiryacittaṃ karmacittaṃ  dvayaṃ vīthiṃ pracakṣate vīthimuktan tu vijñānaṃ  vīthicittaiḥ pravardhate   vipākākriyavijñānaṃ  pratisandhir bhaved bhave bhavāṅgam pravṛttau bhūtvā  jīvitānte bhaveccyutiḥ   mūlavijñānam ālaya  vijñānam iti nāmake buddhavijñānaśabdārthe  vasubandhuḥ prayūyujat   bhavam bhavāṅgam tatsroto  vijñānam eva bhāvayet pratisandhicyutībhyāṃ tad  vīthimuktaṃ prakīrtitam   kriyabhūtaṃ karmabhūtaṃ  vīthicittaṃ dvidhā matam akriyaṃ vīthimuktaṃ vai  vipākam eva sarvadā   sakarmaṃ vīthicittan tu  buddhādiṣu na vidyate teṣāṃ hi vīthicittaṃ vai  kriyarūpeṇa dhāvati   āryāṣṭāṅgikamārgeṇa  karmakṣayakṛtam manaḥ buddhānām arhatañ caiva  nāhvayate punarbhavam   kriyākarmavipākais tu  sarvaṃ cittaṃ vibhāvyate vedanāsaṃjñāsaṃskāra  vijñānaiś ca tathaiva tat   pratītyasamutpādākhye  dharme deśitavedanā saṃjñārthaṃ vedanārthañ ca  dvayam eva prakāśate   trīṇyevārthāni cittasya  prayogasya vaśāt punaḥ mūladvaitīyasāmānya  nāmais tattrikam ucyate   mūlārtho hi tu cittasya  vedanārtham prakāśate vedanaṃ tasminnarthe tu  buddha eva prayūyujat   dvaitīyārthan tu cittasya  saṃjñārtham eva bhāvayet sāmānyārtho hi tasya syāt  cittaṃ vāthonam aṅgikam   tathaiva ca manaśśabdas  tadevārthatrayaṃ dadet mūlārthaṃ hi bhavet saṃjñā  dvaitīyārthaṃ tu vedanā sāmānyārtho manaśśabde  cittayuktasamo bhavet   vedanāsaṃjñā saṃskāra  vijñānānāṃ yathākramam antargatārthān manasaś  cittasyaivañ ca jñāyatām   vijñānam eva vijñaptiḥ  kriyākriyavaśā dvidhā vijñaptimātratā siddhā  cittasyaivaṃ hi niścayāt   kṣaṇajanyā ca vijñaptir  anityā kṣaṇabhangurā tadātmakesmiṃś citte tu  māyā syād ātmakalpanā   pratītyasamutpāde ca  pañcaskandhe ca darśane vidyamānam cittatatvaṃ  samyag evaṃ prakāśitam  śrī lankāputrabhūtena  paṇḍitācāryabhikṣuṇā āmerikādharmadūta  priyanandena dhīmatā vijñaptimātrātasiddhi  pañcāśiketi nāmikā kṛtaivaṃ kṛ tir eṣā tu  cittatatvasya saṅgrahaḥ
