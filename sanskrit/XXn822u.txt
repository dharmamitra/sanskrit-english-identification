 narakoddhārastotram
 dāridrayapaṅkasaṃmagnaṃ saṃsārākhyamahodadhau 
 pratijñātaṃ samutpāde trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 timirāgārasaṃviṣṭamanarthaduḥkhavedinam 
 bandhuvargaiḥ parityaktaṃ trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 mātāpitṛbhaginyādi putradārasuhṛjjanāḥ 
 indrajālasamā dṛṣṭāstrāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 mayā arijanasyārthe sukṛtaṃ karma duṣkṛtam 
 ekākī taṃ hi bhokṣyāmi trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 jīrṇakūpe mahāghore anavagāhasāgare 
 andhībhūto'smyahaṃ nātha trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 jīrṇanauikāsamārūḍho mahāsāgaralaṃghane 
 durlaṅghyaṃ ca mayā dṛṣṭaṃ trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 dharmādharma na vijñātaṃ gamyāgamyaṃ na veditam 
 acetano'smyahaṃ nātha trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 mātṛghātādikaṃ pañcānantaryaṃ vā mayā kṛtam 
 pacyāmi narake ghore trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 kṛtaṃ mayā stūpabhedaṃ saṃghakāryaṃ vināśitam 
 kṛtā mayā sattvahiṃsā trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 ihaloke sukhairhīnaṃ paraloke na vedanam 
 veṣṭitaṃ karmasūtreṇa trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 kāśapuṣpaṃ yathā''kāśe bhramate vāyunā hatam 
 īdṛśaṃ jīvitaṃ loke trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 aṭavī kaṃṭakācchannā bahuvṛkṣasamākulā 
 panthānaṃ nātra paśyāmi trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 anaparādhāḥ kupitena mayā'kāṇḍe hatā mṛgāḥ 
 rājahatyāṃ tadā manye trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 narake pacyamānasya kaścit trātā bhaviṣyati 
 gacchāmi śaraṇaṃ kasya trāhi māṃ hi tathāgata   
 vaidyānāṃ vaidyarājastvaṃ sarvavyādhicikitsakaḥ 
 lokanātha bhavatrātā trailokye sacarācare   
 narakoddhārastotraṃ samāptam
