 samādhiḥ saṃbhavet tena 
saṃbhavet ca mana unmanī 
 manaḥ mūrcchām samāsādya 
manaḥ ātmani yojayet 
 para ātmanaḥ samāyogāt 
samādhim samavāpnuyāt 
 iti te kathitam caṇḍa 
samādhiḥ mukti lakṣaṇam 
 rāja yogaḥ samādhiḥ syāt 
eka ātmani eva sādhanam 
 unmanī saha ja avasthā 
sarve ca eka ātma vācakāḥ 
 jale viṣṇuḥ sthale viṣṇuḥ 
viṣṇuḥ parvata mastake 
 jvālā māla ākule viṣṇuḥ 
sarvam viṣṇumayam jagat 
 bhū carāḥ khe carāḥ ca amī 
yāvantaḥ jīva jantavaḥ 
 vṛkṣa gulma latā vallī 
 tṛṇa ādyāḥ vāri parvatāḥ 
 sarvam brahma vijānīyāt 
sarvam paśyati ca ātmani 
 ātmā ghata stha caitanyam 
a dvaitam śāśvatam param 
 ghaṭāt bhinnataram jñānam 
vīta rāgam vi vāsanam 
 evam vidhiḥ samādhiḥ syāt 
sarva saṃkalpa varjitaḥ 
 sva dehe putra dāra ādi 
 bāndhaveṣu dhana ādiṣu 
 sarveṣu niḥ mamaḥ bhūtvā 
samādhim samavāpnuyāt 
 laya a mṛtam param tattvam 
śiva uktam vi vidhāni ca 
 teṣām saṃkṣepam ādāya 
kathitam mukti lakṣaṇam 
 iti te kathitaḥ caṇḍa 
samādhiḥ duḥ labhaḥ paraḥ 
 yam jñātvā na punaḥ janma 
jāyate bhūmi maṇḍale 
  iti śrī gheraṇḍa saṃhitāyām gheraṇḍa caṇḍa saṃvāde ghata stha yoga sādhane yogasya sapta sāre samādhi yogaḥ nāma  
saptama upadeśaḥ samāptaḥ
