aṣṭabhāgahīnaṃ tu śodhye dadyād ghṛtaplutam  Na yathoktena vidhānena viprān sprṣṭvānumoditaḥ Nc sopavāsaś ca khādeta devabrāhmaṇasaṃnidhau  Na viṣaṃ vegaklamāpetaṃ sukhena yadi jīryate Nc viśuddham iti taṃ jñātvā rājā satkṛtya mokṣayet  Na tvaṃ viṣa brahmaṇaḥ putraḥ satyadharmaratau sthitaḥ Nc śodhayainaṃ naraṃ pāpāt satyenāsyāmṛtībhava  kośaḥNa ataḥ paraṃ pravakṣyāmi kośasya vidhim uttamam  Na pūrvāhṇe sopavāsasya snātasyārdrapaṭasya ca Nc saśūkasyāvyasaninaḥ kośapānaṃ vidhīyate  Na yadbhaktaḥ so abhiyuktaḥ syāt taddaivatyaṃ tu pāyayet Nc saptāhād yasya dṛśyate dvisaptāhena vā punaḥ Ne pratyātmikaṃ tu yatkiṃcit saiva tasya vibhāvanā  Na dvisaptāhāt paraṃ yasya mahad vā vaikṛtaṃ bhavet Nc nābhiyojyaḥ sa viduṣāṃ kṛtakālavyatikramāt  Na mahāparādhe nirdharme kṛtaghne klībakutsite Nc nāstikavrātyadāseṣu kośapānaṃ vivarjayet  Na yathoktena prakāreṇa pañca divyāni dharmavit Nc dadyād rājābhiyuktānāṃ pretya ceha ca nandati  Na na viṣaṃ brāhmaṇe dadyān na lohaṃ kṣatriyo haret Nc na nimajjyāpsu vaiśyaś ca śūdraḥ kośaṃ na pāyayet  Na varṣāsu na viṣaṃ dadyāt hemante nāpsu majjayet Nc na lohaṃ hārayed grīṣme na kośaṃ pāyayen niśi
