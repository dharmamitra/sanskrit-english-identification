abhyavagāhya yenānyatama vṛkṣamūla tenopasakramati  upasakramya āyuṣmatam ānandam āmatrayāmāsa Wal prajñapayānandāsmi vṛkṣamūle tathāgatasya niṣīdana pṛṣṭhī me āglāyati tā tāvad āyāmayiṣye Wal eva bhadatāyuṣmān ānando bhagavataḥ pratiśrutya tasmi vṛkṣamūle bhagavato niṣīdana prajñapayāmāsa Wal nyaṣīdad bhagavā prajñapta evāsane āyuṣmān apy ānando bhagavata ekāte nyaṣīdat Wal niṣadya bhagavā Wal yāvato bhadanta bhikṣavo gandhamādanacaityam upaniḥśṛtya viharati sarve te upasthānaśālāyā sanipatitāḥ  yasyedānī bhagavān kāla  manyate  Wal atha bhagavān utthāyāsanād yenopasthānaśālā tenopasakrānta upasakramya prajñapta evāsane nyaṣīdat Wal niṣaṇṇo bhagavā bhikṣūn āmatrayāmāsa Wal tasmāt tarhi bhikṣavo ye mayā dharmāḥ svayam abhijñāya sākṣīkṛtvopasapadya praveditāstadyathā catvāri smṛtyupasthānāni catvāri samyakprahāṇāni catvāro ṛddhipādāḥ pañcendriyāṇipañca balāni sapta bodhyṅgāny āryāṣṭāṅgo mārgaḥ tatra vaḥ sarvaiḥ sahitaiḥ samagraiḥsamodamānair avivadadbhir ekāgrair
