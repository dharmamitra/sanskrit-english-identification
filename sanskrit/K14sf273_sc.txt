Mahāparinirvāṇasūtra nd
Wal vitarkā viditā utpadyate viditās tiṣṭati viditā adhyastagacchati 
Wal idam api yūyam ānanda tathāgatasyāścaryādbhutadharma dhārayata  evam āścaryādbhutadharmasamanvāgata ānanda tathāgataḥ 
Wal atha bhagavān āyuṣmantam ānandam āmatrayāmāsa  āgamayānanda yena gandhamādana caitya tenopasakramiṣyāvaḥ 
Wal eva bhadata āyuṣmān ānando bhagavataḥ pratyaśrauṣīt 
Wal atha bhagavān utthāyāsanād yena gandhamādana caitya tenopasakramati  āyuṣmān ānando niṣīdanam asenāropya bhagavata pṛṣṭhataḥ pṛṣṭhataḥ  
samanubaddhaḥ  myānulomatayā  
Wal atha bhagavā gandhamādana caityam abhyavagāhya yenānyatama vṛkṣamūla tenopasakramati  upasakramya āyuṣmatam ānandam āmatrayāmāsa 
Wal prajñapayānandāsmi vṛkṣamūle tathāgatasya niṣīdana pṛṣṭhī me āglāyati tā tāvad āyāmayiṣye 
Wal eva bhadatāyuṣmān ānando bhagavataḥ pratiśrutya tasmi vṛkṣamūle bhagavato niṣīdana prajñapayāmāsa 
Wal nyaṣīdad bhagavā prajñapta evāsane āyuṣmān apy ānando bhagavata ekāte nyaṣīdat 
Wal niṣadya bhagavā āyuṣmatam ānandam āmantrayāmāsa 
Wal yāvata ānanda bhikṣavo gandhamādana caityam upaniḥśṛtya viharati tān sarvān upasthānaśālāyā sanipātaya 
Wal eva bhadatāyuṣmān ānanda bhagavataḥ pratiśrutya yāvato bhikṣavo gandhamādana
caityam upaniḥṣṛtya viharati tān sarvān upasthānaśālāyā sanipātayāmāsa  sanipātya
śirasā bhagavataḥ pādau vattvaikānte asthāt 
Wal ekāntasthita āyuṣmān ānando bhagavatam idam avocat 
Wal yāvato bhadanta bhikṣavo gandhamādanacaityam upaniḥśṛtya viharati sarve te upasthānaśālāyā sanipatitāḥ  yasyedānī bhagavān kāla  
manyate  
Wal atha bhagavān utthāyāsanād yenopasthānaśālā tenopasakrānta upasakramya prajñapta evāsane nyaṣīdat 
Wal niṣaṇṇo bhagavā bhikṣūn āmatrayāmāsa 
Wal tasmāt tarhi bhikṣavo ye mayā dharmāḥ svayam abhijñāya sākṣīkṛtvopasapadya praveditās
tadyathā catvāri smṛtyupasthānāni catvāri samyakprahāṇāni catvāro ṛddhipādāḥ pañcendriyāṇi
pañca balāni sapta bodhyṅgāny āryāṣṭāṅgo mārgaḥ tatra vaḥ sarvaiḥ sahitaiḥ samagraiḥ
samodamānair avivadadbhir ekāgrair ekoddeśair ekakṣīrodakabhūtaiḥ  dīpayamānaiḥ
sukhasparśa vihartavyam 
Wal tasmāt tarhi bhikṣavo ye mayā dharmā ākhyātās tadyathā sūtra geya vyākaraṇa gāthodānā
nidānāvadānā itivṛttakajātakavaipulyādbhutadharmopadeśās te dharmā bhavadbhiḥ sādhu
ca suṣṭhu codgrahītavyā udgṛhya dhārayitavyā dhārayitvā tulayitavyāḥ tulayitvā niśāmayitavyā
niśāmya tathā tathā pratipattavyam 
Wal tat kasmād dhetoḥ  acireṇa tathāgatasya parinirvāṇa bhaviṣyati  trayāṇā māsānām
atyayena tathāgatasya parinirvāṇa bhaviṣyati kuśinagaryupavartane mallānā sālavane
antare yamakasālayoḥ 
Wal atha te bhikṣavo bhagavatontikād ida evarūpa vākya śrutvā ekatyā bhikṣavaḥ cchinnaprapāta  pṛthivyā nipetuś cetobh samarpitāḥ atikṣipra
