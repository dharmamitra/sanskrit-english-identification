Vimalaratnalekhaḥ bhāratabhāṣāyām vimalaratnalekho nāma bhoṭabhāṣāyām dri ma med pa rin po chei phrina cesa vya va guruve namaḥ tārādevyai namaḥ  mahāgatikāya dṛṣṭvā barddhayan buddhaśāsanam dharmeṇa pālayan rājya nayapālo nṛpobhavat   tvaya pūrvva dāna daśa kuśalāni poṣadhaḥ kṣāntirvīryañcābhyastamiti deva tvamadhunā sampannaḥ    guruṇāmājñā dhammānugata sūtrādikañcedam bhaktyā śirasā gṛhāṇa ātmaparayorhita bhavet   vivarjayan sarvvasaśaya siddhye kuru mahāyatnam nidrālutva tandrāñca vivarjayan nityamudyama kuryā vīryāya    smṛtijñānāpramādena sadendriyadvārāṇi rakṣet ahorātra punaḥpuna ścittasantāna parīkṣeta   svadoṣajñāna cakṣuriva kuryāt paradoṣajñāna tvandhavat smṛtimanahaṅkāra śūnyatāñca sadā bhāvayet    kuryāt svadoṣakīrttana nāparasya bhrāntimanusandadhyāt kīrttayedaparasya guṇa ātmaguṇantu gopayet   tyajellābha satkārañca nityamātmakhyāti tyajet maitrī karuṇāñca bhāvayed bodhicitta sthira kuru    daśākuśalāni heyāni sadā śraddhā dṛḍhīkāryā manāgapi priya santoṣaḥ kṛtānukampā ca smarttavyam   krodho garvañca prahātavya kāryā ca hīnacittatā mithyājīvikā vivarjanīyā dharmajīvikā jīvitavyā    sarvamāmiṣa vivarjayed āryadhanena ātmānasampanna kuryyāt sadā samāja tyajed araṇye nivaset   tyajed vācamasambaddhā nitya syāt śuddhasayataḥ gurūpādhyāyadaśane śraddhayā paricaryā kuryāt   viśiṣṭe puruṣe dharmacakṣuṣi pudgale ādikarmmikasattveṣu ca śāstṛsajñāmutpādayet    duḥkhataptānā sarvveṣā sattvānāñca darśane bodhicittamutpādya mātṛpitāputrasajñāñcotpādayet   sarva lokakṛtya tyaktvā nitya samādhibhāvanā kuru tyaja pāpamitra kalyāṇamitramāya    duḥśīlabhikṣūṇām anyeṣā dharmmahīnānām pāpācārāṇāñca darśane teṣā nigrahānna viramet   sarvverakalyāṇamitraiḥ sarvvaiḥ pāpasahāyaiḥ ratnatraye gurā vupādhyāya ācārye ca śraddhāhīnaiḥ    akṛtajñai raihikabuddhibhiḥ paratrālpaśraddhaiśca dinatrayātikrameṇa na vaset   duṣṭāmaśubhā bhūmi parityajya yatra śubha gantavyam yatra rāgastat parityājyam anāsakta vaset   rāgo hi sugatimaprāpayan mokṣajīvana chīnatti yatra kalyāṇamitra vasati tatra nitya vaset    guru sadāśritya nitya sūtranikāyamavalokayet ādau yadārabdha prathama tadeva sādhayitavyam anyathā nobhaya sidhyati   anyeṣu pāpadeśanāyā paramapuṇye ca yateta vṛttānukūla loka sadā paralokacittañca rakṣet    yadā cittamunnata jāyate tadā garva chindyāt apramādavirahe gurūpadeśaḥ smarttavyaḥ   līnacitte jāte cittonnatimapi praśaset smṛtvā prajñāpāramitā nikhilaprapañcopaśama kuryāt    yadā rāgadveṣaviṣayo jāyate māyāprapañcavat paśyet nindāvākyaśravaṇe pratiśrutkāvajjānīyāt    kāyapīḍodbhave pūrvakarmāvalokayet prāntāraṇye pravasan mṛgaśavavat   yatra kenāpi na jñāyate  tatra ātmanātmāna gopayet sadeṣṭadevatāmāśrayet kāme vyāpāde middhe styāne   ālasye tandrāyāñca yadā cittamutpadyate tadā svapāpa gaṇayet vrata hṛdi smared anityatā mṛtyuñca bhāvayet   anyasya puratovasthānakāle śāntamṛju ca bhāṣeta bhrūkuṭimukhena hāsya prahātavya sadā smitena sthātavya nityamanyeṣu prītiśca kāryā    amātsaryeṇa tyāge prīti kuryāt nityamīrṣyā prahātavyā anyasya citta rakṣet sarvve vādāḥ prahātavyāḥ   paricayena vinā nūtanena saṅga na kuryāt sadā mantre samarthaḥ syāt sadā kṣāntimān alpakāmaśca san santoṣa kuryāt    caṇḍāladāsasajñaḥ syāt lajjājñānepatrapāyā tasyā paralokārādhanāyāñca yateta ātmasayama kuryāt   parāvajñā prahātavyā śraddhāśīlena tiṣṭhet paropadeśakaraṇe kāruṇyopakāracittaḥ tat kuryāt    buddhadharmme śraddhā saddharmmañca na jātu tyajet nitya ratnatraye śraddhā sasāratraya ca pariśuddha kuryāt   pūrva karuṇāgato horātra trikṛtvaḥ prasiddhasyārādhanāsaptakasya skandhatraya gṛhṇīyāt   jagadduḥkhanirākaraṇakāma udārapraṇidhāna sthāpayet sattvaparipāka bodhiñcecchet sarva mahābodhau pariṇamayet    cira pratijñāya sadodyogī bhavet eva kṛte dvivargaḥ sampadyata āvaraṇadvayañca kṣīyate   naradehalābhaḥ sārthako bhavati dṛṣṭadharmme nirvvāṇa bhavati svaparārthaḥ sampadyate paramatattva ca prāpyate    nidāghakokilapotaḥ madhura kūjatīti ki mayūrapoto na madhura kūjati yadyapi bahubhiḥ paṇḍitairukta tathāpirājñā vyasanacchedāya likhitamidam    kathā śreyobhiprāyeṇa kuru anyasmāyapi matimate janāya kathaya  nitya ṣaḍdevatāḥ smara śuddha vratamācara rāja dharmmeṇa pālaya ātmāna ca kṣama kuru   vimalaratnalekho nāma sthaviramahāpaṇḍitadīpaṅkaraśrījñānād rājñe nayapālāya preritā ślokacatvāriśat sampūrṇā tasminneva kāle tenaiva guruṇā parivarttakena bhikṣuṇā jayaśīlena ca
