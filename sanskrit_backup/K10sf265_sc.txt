Wal_1 śrāvastyāṁ nidānam /
Wal_2 ye kecid bhikṣavaḥ samyag eva śraddhayā agārād anagārikāṁ pravrajaṁti sarve te caturṇāṁ āryasatyānāṁ jñānāya /
Wal_3 katameṣāṁ caturṇām /

Wal_5 tasmāt tarhi caturṇām āryasatyānām anabhisamitānām abhisamayāya atimātraṁ tīvracchando vyāyāmaś cotsudhiś cāprativāṇiś ca smṛtyā saṁprajanyena  #
