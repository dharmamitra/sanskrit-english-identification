 vasudhārāstotram
 yā samyaguktavidhibhiḥ paripathyamānā
 lakṣmīṃ dadāti vipulāṃ sugatopabhogān |
 tāṃ dhāraṇīṃ sakalasattvahitaikacittāṃ
 bhaktyā namāmi satataṃ vasudhārasaṃjñām || 1 ||
 yā saṃsmṛtā sucirasusthataraṃ pravṛddhaṃ
 dāridrayaduḥkhaduritaṃ śamate narāṇām |
 tāṃ kalpavṛkṣasadṛśīṃ vasudhārasaṃjñāṃ
 nityaṃ namāmi śirasā jagatāṃ hitāya || 2 ||
 ratnākarīṃ ratnanidhānakoṣāṃ
 vicitraratnāṃ pratibhāsavarṣām |
 ratnāvalīṃ ratnamayīṃ vicitrāṃ
 namāmi cāryāṃ vasudhāradhārām || 3 ||
 tāṃ ratnapāṇisaphalāmavabhāsayā te
 tyāgāvataṃsadayaśekharamudvahantīm |
 hārārdhahāracalakuṇḍalabhūṣitāṅgī-
 māryāṃ namāmi vasuvṛṣṭikarīṃ śaraṇyām || 4 ||
 śrīvasudhārākalpoddhṛtaṃ vasudhārāstotraṃ samāptam |
