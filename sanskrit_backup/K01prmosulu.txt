[I. catvāraḥ pārājikā dharmāḥ]
ime kho punar āyuṣmanto catvāraḥ pārājikā dharmā anvardhamāsaṃ sūtre prātimokṣe uddeśam āgacchanti /
PrMoSū(Mā-L)Pār.1. yo puna bhikṣu bhikṣūṇāṃ śikṣāsāmīcīsamāpanno śikṣām apratyākhyāya
daurbalyam anāviṣkṛtvā maithunaṃ grāmyadharmaṃ pratiṣeveya antamaśato tiryagyonigatāyam

divase dvādaśame,
purebhaktam uttarāmukhaniṣaṇṇena dvyardhapauruṣāyāṃ cchāyāyāṃ,
āyuṣmantaṃ yaśikaṃ kalandakaputram ārabhya /
