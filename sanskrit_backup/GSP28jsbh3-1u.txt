viśeṣataḥ prāptatve 'pi sarvapuroḍāśapratipattitvena vidhitvasaṃbhave anuvādatvāyogāt tena viśeṣaparyavasāyitvānupapatteḥ /
ato yadaivānena vidhānamārabhyate,
tadaiva sarvasādhāraṇapratibandhaphalamupasaṃhāratvaṃ vaktavyam /
tatracānirdhāritaviśeṣatayā viśeṣānvayayogyatvarūpāspaṣṭatvasya sāmānyavākye tadabhāvarūpaspaṣṭatvasyaca
viśeṣavākye saṃbhavo 'styevetyabhipretyaivoddeśyatāvacchedakānavacchinne lakṣaṇāpattyā
sāmānyavākyasya na viśeṣopasaṃhārakatvamityuktaṃ pūjyapādairiti bhāvaḥ //
siddhāntamupasaṃharati --------- tasmāditi //
prayojanaṃ spaṣṭatvāt noktam //
iti pañcadaśamupasaṃhārādhikaraṇam // @- - - - - - -
