Nāmāṣṭaśatakaya
namaḥ śākyasiṃhāya
1. sambuddha puṇḍarīkākṣa sarvajña karuṇāspadam
samantabhadra śāstāra śākyasiṃham namāmy aham |
2. śrīghana śrīmati śreṣṭha śīlarāśi śivaṅkaram
śrīmanta śrīkara śānta śāntaveśa namāmy aham |
3. nairātmyavādina siddha niravadya nirāśravam
nītijña nirmalātmāna niṣkalaṅkam namāy aham |
4. nirvāda nirahaṅkāra nirvikalpa tathāgatām
nirdhūtanikhilakleśa niṣprapañca namāmy aham |
5. viśveśvara vimuktijña viśvarūpa vināyakam
viśvalakṣaṇasampūrṇa vītarāga namāmy aham |
6. vidyācaraṇasampanna viśveśa vimalaprabham
vinītaveśa vipula vītadoṣa namāmy aham |
7. durdāntadamaka sarvya śuddha śauddhodani munim
sugata sugati saumya śubhrakīrtti namāmy aham |
8. yogīśvara daśabala lokajña lokapūjitam
lokācāryya nirācāryya lokanātha namāmy aham |
9. kalaṅkamukta kāmārim akalaṅka kalādharam
kāntamūrtti dayāpātra kanakābha namāmy aham |
10. paramārttha parajyoti parama parameśvaram
bhavābhavakara bhavya bhagavanta namāmy aham |
11. mahāmati mahāvīryya mahābhijña mahābalam
mahodyama mahādhairyya mahābāhu namāmy aham |
12. ādya pavitra sarvīyam aparājitam acyutam
mita parahita nātham amitābha namāmy aham |
13 | catur-mārārivijita tatvajña śaṅkara śivam
satvasāra sadācāra sārtthavāha namāmy aham |
14. devadeva mahādeva devavanditam avyayam
pramāṇātītadeveśa divyarūpa namāmy aham |
15. jitendriya jitakleśa jinendra puruṣottamam
uttama sattama brāhma puṇyakṣetra namāmy aham |
16. bhaktyeda yaḥ paṭhen nitya prātar utthāya mānavaḥ
nāmāṣṭaśataka puṇya pavitra pāpanāśanam,
17. sa labhetamitān bhogān bhaumān svargodbhvās tathā
vyādhayas ta na bādhante duḥsvapna tasya naśyati |
18. āyurārogyasampannas sarvaiśvaryyasamanvitaḥ
medhāvī kulajo vāgmī bhavej janmani janmani |
nāmāṣṭaśatakaya nimi |
