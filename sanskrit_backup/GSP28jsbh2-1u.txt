tatrāpi saṃpūrṇapadasyaivohāpatteḥ pratyayamātrādhyāhāre bādhakābhāvādayuktamiti vyaktaṃ kaustubhe /
evañca prakṛtimātrabhreṣa eva yajurbhreṣaprāyaścittaṃ,
pratyayabhreṣe tu na tadityapi prayojanaṃ spaṣṭatvānnoktam //
ityaṣṭādaśamadhyāhārādhikaraṇam //
